Your body crashes up the gears and throws all its resources into getting you moving.

你的身體會激發腎上腺素，將所有能量運用在你身體的移動上。

壓力存在的原因？教你消除壓力的方法！ (Managing Stress - Brainsmart - BBC)


1.
* gear [gɪr] (n.) 排檔
- Drive gear 前進檔
- Reverse gear 後退檔
- Parking gear 停車檔
- Crash up the gears切換檔
- You should shift/switch/change into the lower gear (轉到低檔）while driving the mountains so the brake won’t burn out（煞車不會過熱失靈）.

* gear [gɪr] (n.) 裝備
- Fishing gear釣魚裝備
- Camping gear露營裝備
- There were over a hundred police officers in riot gear （防暴裝備)standing by the scene of the protest(在抗議現場）.

2.
* crash up [kræʃ ʌp] (phr.) 調高檔速
- The runner crashed up through the gears (讓身體馬上換檔進入備戰狀態） on the final lap to win the race.

3.
* resource [rɪˋsors;ˋrɪsors] (n.) 資源

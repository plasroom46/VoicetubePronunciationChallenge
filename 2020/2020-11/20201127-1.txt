When summer ends, there's a temptation to ditch the salads and fill up with starchy comfort foods.

過了夏天，人們會有拋棄沙拉、改成以澱粉滿足餐代替的慾望。

總是感到疲倦嗎？4 招教你如何消除疲勞！(How To Stop being Tired All The Time | How To Reduce Winter Tiredness | Best Way To Reduce Tiredness)


1.
* temptation [tɛmpˋteʃən] (n.) 引誘；誘惑
- temptation to + v. 受到⋯誘惑
- Zora can not resist the temptation(誘惑） to eat junk food.
* tempt [tɛmpt] (v.) 引誘，誘惑

2.
* ditch [dɪtʃ] (v.) 拋棄，遺棄
- Linda ditched (拋棄）her old bicycle.
* ditch [dɪtʃ] (n.) 溝渠

3.
* fill up [fɪl ʌp] (ph.) 充滿，填滿
- fill sb up 讓...吃得很飽
- Remember to fill up (裝滿)the tank （油箱）before you hit the road.

4.
* starchy [ˋstɑrtʃɪ] (adj.) 澱粉的
- Fiona is trying to eat less starchy food (澱粉食物）.
* starch [stɑrtʃ] (n.) 澱粉

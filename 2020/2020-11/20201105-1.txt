Numerous studies have shown that a life full of fun and friends comes with cognitive benefits.

許多研究顯示，生活中充滿樂趣和朋友，對認知功能有助益。

常常忘東忘西嗎？5 招強化記憶力的方法！(Top 5 Tips To Improve Your Memory)


1.
* numerous [ˋnjumərəs] ( adj.) 許多的，大量的
- Your report contains numerous (許多）errors. You need to redo it.
* abundant [əˋbʌndənt] ( adj.) 大量的；充足的；豐富的
- There are abundant (充足的）balloons here. Take as many as you like.
* countless [ˋkaʊntlɪs] ( adj.) 無數的；數不清的
- I tried to apologise countless times(無數次）. She just won’t forgive me.
* copious [ˈkoʊpiəs] ( adj.) 大量的，豐富的；過量的
- Copious(大量的） amounts of food and wine are on the table.

2.
* cognitive [ˋkɑgnətɪv] (adj .) 感知的；認知的；認識力的
- As kids grow older, their cognitive processes become sharper(感知處理能力會更明顯/鋭利）.

3.
* benefit [ˋbɛnəfɪt] (n.) 利益，好處；優勢
- Lemon has multiple health benefits(好處）. It’s a fruit of nutrients.
* benefit [ˋbɛnəfɪt] (v.) 得益，受惠；使受益；對…有幫助
- The only one who can benefit(受益） from this situation is yourself.

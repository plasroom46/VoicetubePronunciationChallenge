1. _
* fate [fet ] (n.) 命中註定的事；命運；天意
- Now that we’ve done every possible thing we can. All there’s left to do is let the fate decide(剩下的讓命運決定）.
* fated [ˋfetɪd ] (adj.) 命中註定的
- Hey! Gorgeous! Out of the hundreds of bars in the city, we both chose to come here tonight. It seems that we are fated to get together.
嘿，美女，城裡有上百個酒吧我們卻都選在這裡，這像是我們命中註定要相聚。

2. _
* command [kəˋmænd ] (n.) 控制，指揮；命令，指示
- You have to take command of your own life and learn when to say no.
- Alright! Raise the curtain on my command.(好了！聽我的指示拉起布簾) 3,2,1,go!
+ raise the curtain => 拉起舞台的布幕(按我的命令)
3. _
* destiny [ˋdɛstənɪ] (n.) 前途；命運
- I will control my own destiny(還未知的命運).
- Destiny and Fate are synonyms but there is still a slight difference between them.
+ Destiny => 正面
+ Fate => 負面
4. _
* faith [feθ ] (n.) 信念，信心，信仰
- To have faith in somebody or something means to firmly believe in somebody or something
- She has a lot of faith in her husband believing that he will amount to (變為；成爲)something incredible someday.
- Have faith in your dreams (對你的夢想有信念）and someday. Your rainbow will come smiling through.
5. _
* beyond [bɪˋjɑnd ] (prep.) 超出…；超越...
* Beyond our command 超出我們的控制
* Beyond compare 超出比較範圍
* Beyond doubt 超出疑惑範圍；無庸置疑的
* Beyond belief 難以置信的
- The opera singer's beautiful voice is beyond compare.
+ 無與倫比
- His competence as the new manager is beyond doubt. 
他擔任新任經理的能力是無庸置疑的。

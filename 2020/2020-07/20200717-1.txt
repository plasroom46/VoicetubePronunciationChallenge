It's proven to help you shed pounds fast. But there's more to this diet than meets the eye.

它被證實能快速幫助你減肥。但這種減肥法並不像表面上看起來那麼簡單。

句子選自此影片：你的 Summer Body 練好了沒？生酮飲食又是什麼？ (Is your summer body ready? What is the Keto Diet?)

我似乎隱隱約約聽到 "diet" 好像少了一個音節～ 要唸成 /di-et/兩個音節唷！

1. _
* ketogenic [͵kitoˋdʒɛnɪk] (adj.) 生酮的
2. _
* prove [pruv] (v.) 證明是，結果是
- Exercising three to five times a week has proven to be effective and allowing people to shed off some pounds(減重）.
* proof [pruf] (n.) 證據；物證
3. _
* diet [͵kitoˋdʒɛnɪks] (n.) （日常）飲食
- on a diet
- I'm on a diet.
- My religious diet prohibits (我的宗教信仰的飲食禁止）me to eat pork or beef.
4. _
* be more to this than meets the eye [] (idiom.) 比開始認為的還要難應付；比當初看上去的更複雜
- Being a KOL （key opinion leader ；網紅）is difficult. There’s more to this than meets the eye(這比看起來的更難）.

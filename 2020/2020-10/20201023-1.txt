練習講英文講得更快。收聽英文播客 (podcast)，試著跟上講者的速度。聽不懂全部的內容也沒關係，試著跟上速度即可。

英文口試時，我該講多快？ (TOEFL Speaking Tips | The Ultimate Guide to Pace)



1.
* practice [ˋpræktɪs] (v.) 練習
* in practice [ˋpræktɪs] (phr.) （用來描述真正發生的事，而不是預計會發生的事）實際上
- I was expecting our plan to go in a disaster (我們的計劃失敗）but in practice(事實上), it went okay(進行的還不錯）.
2.
* listen [ˋlɪsn] (v.) 聽；傾聽
- I like listening （選聽）to pop songs when I work out（健身）.
* hear [hɪr] (v.) 聽到；聽見
- I heard （聽過）this song on Spotify before.
3.
* match up [mætʃ ʌp] (phr.) 相一致；接近一致
- JJ has a high pitch in his songs. Not many male singers can match up with （跟的上）him.
- He’s no match to you(他不是你對手） cause you’re so much better.
* pace [pes] (n.) （移動的）步調；（發生的）速度；節奏
- I’m very used to a slow-paced (慢步調的)lifestyle since I’ve lived in the countryside for 5 years.

Instead of focusing on the negatives, think about a time that you enjoyed stress, like during a horror movie, or while riding a roller coaster.

與其想著壓力的壞處，不如多想想看恐怖電影或是搭雲霄飛車時你所享受的刺激感受。

句子選自此影片：覺得生活充滿壓力嗎？其實適當壓力是有好處的 (The Upside of Stress)

1. _
* upside [ˋʌpˋsaɪd] (adj.) 優點；好處；有利的一面
- Downside of stress 壓力的缺點；壞處
- Upside of stress壓力的優點；好處
- The antonym for upside is downside, negative.

2. _
* negative [ˋnɛgətɪv] (n.) 不好的方面
- When we fall in love, many people are good at seeing the positive side(好的一面） of their partner without seeing the negative side（不好的ㄧ面）.
- Blinded by love 被愛的感覺盲目
* negative [ˋnɛgətɪv] (adj.) 負面的；消極的

3. _
* horror movie [ˋhɔrɚ] (n.) 恐怖片；恐怖電影
* thriller [ˋθrɪlɚ] (n.) （常指關於偵破罪案的）驚悚小說（或戲劇、電影）
- The thriller (懸疑驚悚片)has many plot twists(劇情反轉）

4. _
* roller coaster [ˋrolɚ ˋkostɚ] (n/adj.) 雲霄飛車；過山車 （也可以只心情高低起伏）
- When I go to an amusement park, the first ride I want to experience is the scariest roller coaster（最恐怖的雲霄飛車） they have.
- This week has been a roller coaster ride(像做雲霄飛車的心情）for me. I got promoted but had to start a really stressful project at the same time.

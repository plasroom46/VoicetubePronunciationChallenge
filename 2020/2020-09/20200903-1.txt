Astronomers have found dozens of potentially habitable planets outside of our solar system.

天文學家已經在我們太陽系之外發現了數十個潛在宜居的行星。

1. 
* astronomer [əˋstrɑnəmɚ] (n.) 天文學家
- The newly discovered star attracted the attention of astronomers (天文學家）around the globe.
* astrology [əˋstrɑlədʒɪ] (n.) 占星術；占星學
- He first learnt astrology to impress (使⋯被注意）the girls in his class never thinking that he will fall in love with it.
* astronaut [ˋæstrə͵nɔt] (n.) 太空人
- Videos of people trying real astronaut food (太空人的食物）had been trendy (流行）on YouTube.

2. 
* habitable [ˋhæbɪtəb!] (adj.) 適於居住的，可以居住的
- Mars is the most habitable (最適居住的）planet in our solar system besides Earth.
* habitat [ˋhæbɪ͵tæt] (n.)（動植物的）生長地，棲息地
- Cutting down trees will destroy some of the animals’ native habitat.
* inhabit [ɪnˋhæbɪt] (v.) 居住於
- Some aboriginal tribes(原住民部落）still inhabit (居住在)the more remote areas of our country.

3. 
* solar system (np.) 太陽系
- By the age of 3, he had already memorized(記下） the names of the eight planets in our solar system(太陽系）.

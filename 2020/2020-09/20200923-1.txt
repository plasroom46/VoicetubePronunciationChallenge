Can you pick out different instruments? Can you hear a drum beat or a certain rhythm? Let yourself focus on the music, and let other thoughts drift away.

你能分辨出有哪些樂器嗎？你能聽見鼓聲或特定的節奏嗎？讓自己專注於音樂中，讓其他想法不再干擾你。

句子選自此影片：上班上課心好累？給你 8 個放鬆心靈的好方法！ (How to relax | 8 relaxation tips for your mental health)


1. instrument [ˋɪnstrəmənt ] (n.)樂器
= musical instrument
→ I think guitar is a rather easy instrument to pick up.

2. pick out (phr v.)辨認出，分辨出，認真挑選出
→ I picked him out from a huge crowd faces althought I only met him once.
→ After the course, we'll pick out three best performers to work with professtional producer in the studio.

3. rhythm [ˋrɪðəm ] (n.)節奏，韻律，律動
→ Hey, just relax and dance with the rhythm.
→ She has a great sense of rhythm. (節奏感)

• rhyme [raɪm ] (v./n.)押韻；押韻詞
→ Most song lyrics rhyme.

4. drift [drɪft ] (v.)飄，漂流，漂移
→ The little girl's paper boat is drifting in the pond.

• drift off (phr v.)漸漸睡著
→ I drifted off in math class.

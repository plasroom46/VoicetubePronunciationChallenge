Instead of saying: "Thank you for being very open with me.", you can say "transparent."

與其說「謝謝你對我這麼坦率」，你可以用「transparent (毫不隱瞞；毫無秘密)」這個字。

1. _
* open [ˋopən] (adj.) 不保密的；公開的；毫不掩飾的
- open minded
- How am I gonna  understand you if you are never open to me?
+ 如果你永遠不向我開放，我將如何理解你？

2. _
* instead of [] (prep.) 作為…的替代
- in place of someone or something
- Hey, instead of complaining, why don't we try to change this.
+ 我們沒有抱怨為什麼不嘗試改變這一點。

3. _
* transparent [trænsˋpɛrənt] (adj.) 透明的
- see through/ transparent <=> invisible (看不到的)
- I like how our company is very transparent with every policy and decision.
+ 我喜歡我們的公司在每項政策和決定中都非常透明。

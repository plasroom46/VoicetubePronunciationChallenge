We all wish we could change something we see, but the reality is that those changes are things almost no one else would notice.

我們都希望可以改變看到的缺點，但其實其他人根本不會發現那些缺點。

句子選自此影片：追求完美的性格常帶給你痛苦嗎？(Stop being a perfectionist)


1. _
* reality [riˋælətɪ] (n.) 現實，事實
- This film blurs the line between reality and fantasy.
+ 這部電影模糊了現實與幻想之間的界線。
- reality show 實境秀
* virtual [ˋvɝtʃʊəl] (adj.) (電腦) 虛擬的
- virtual reality VR 虛擬實境
* augmented [ɔgˋmɛntɪd] (adj.) adj. 加強的
- Augmented reality AR擴增實境

2. _
* else [ɛls] (adv.) 其他，另外，別的
any else, every else, no else
可用於 How What Where Who Why
不可用於 Ｗhich

- The book isn't here. Where else should I look?/ In what other places should I look?
+ 這本書不在這裡，我還應該看其他地方。
+ where else => 其他地方
* elsewhere [ˋɛls͵hwɛr] (adv.) 在別處，到別處，去別處
- Is there intelligent life elsewhere in the universe?
+ 宇宙中其他地方是否存在智能生命？

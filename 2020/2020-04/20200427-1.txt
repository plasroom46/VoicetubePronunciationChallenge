Melancholy is a species of sadness that arises when we're open to the fact that life is inherently difficult.

「憂傷」是一種悲傷的形式，它湧現於當我們坦然接受「人生很辛苦是固來地」這個事實。

句子選自此影片：人生的意義：擁抱自己的憂傷 (On Feeling Melancholy)

1. _
* melancholy [ˈmel.əŋ.kɑː.li] (n.) 憂鬱；憂傷(更加憂傷)
- It means sadness that lasts for a long of time. Often without any reasons.
- Rainy days give me a feeling of melancholy.
+ 雨天給我一種憂鬱的感覺。

2. _
* species [ ˋspiʃiz] (n.) 物種；形式
- There are many species of chrysanthemum
+ 菊花種類很多。
- chrysanthemum /krəˈsænθ.ə.məm/ 菊花
- The giant panda has become an endangered species.
+ 大熊貓已成為瀕危物種。

3. _
* sadness [ˈsæd.nəs] (n.) 悲哀；悲傷(一般)
- The feeling of being sad or unhappy.
- Her eyes were full of sadness.
+ 她的眼睛充滿悲傷。

4. _
* inherently [ɪnˋhɪrəntlɪ] (adv.) 天性地；固有地；本來就
= naturally
- Music is inherently/naturally an important part of every culture.
+ 音樂天生就是每種文化的重要組成部分。

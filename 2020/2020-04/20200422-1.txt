More British actors tend to have a drama school training in which speech and accents and phonetics is part of it.

很多英國演員都會受到戲劇學校的訓練，其中說話方式、腔調和語音都是訓練的一部分。

句子選自此影片：為什麼美國演員這麼不會模仿英國腔 (Why American Actors Suck At British Accents)

1. _
* tend [tɛnd] (v.) 傾向；易於；往往會
- tend to v. 
- I tend to eat more in the winter. According to the studies people to eat more during the colder months.
+ 我傾向於在冬天吃更多的東西。根據研究，人們在較冷的月份多吃東西。
- tend to  n. 照顧
- Nurses tended to the injured.
+ 護理師照護受傷的人。

2. _
* speech [spitʃ] (n.) 演說、發言、致詞；說話方式
演說、發言、致詞
- Did you hear her acceptance speech at the Oscar ceremony?
+ 您在奧斯卡頒獎典禮上看到她的致辭嗎？
說話方式
- Our studio offers voice and speech training classes for actors.
+ 我們的錄音室為演員提供語音和語音培訓課程。

3. _
* accent [ˋæksɛnt] (n.) 口音
- He's got a strong accent.
- He's a Spanish comedian with a very thick accent.
+ 他是西班牙喜劇演員，口音很濃。
- strong/thick accent => collocation

4. _
* phonetics [foˋnɛtɪks] (n.) 語音學
- My roommate told me that the professor of the compulsory subject, Phonetics, is a tough grader what better enough not to skip any classes or she will flunk you.
+ 我的室友告訴我，這個必修課的教授，語音學，是個難過關課程，最好不要翹任何一堂，不然你會不及格。
- compulsory subject = required subject 必修課目
- flunk 當掉

It sounds harmless, creative, and fun right? But too much of anything is always bad for you.

這聽起來很無害，富有創意且很好玩對吧？但任何事物都是物極必反的。

句子選自此影片：愛做白日夢？小心患上「適應不良性白日夢」 (What is Maladaptive Daydreaming?)


1. harmless [ˋhɑrmlɪs] (adj.)無害的；不會導致損傷的
<> harmful (adj.) 有害的
** harmful gas 有害氣體
** carbon monoxide 一氧化碳
** chlorine 氯
** nitrogen dioxide 二氧化氮
Buffy may look harmless, but he is in fact a fierce little puppy.
Buffy 可能看起來很無害，但實際上牠是相當兇狠的小狗狗
fierce 凶狠的

2. creative [krɪˋetɪv] (adj.)有創造力的；創造性的；有創意的
- producing or using original or unusual idea
** creative thinking 創造性思維
** content creator 內容創造者
Let's get creative with this.
讓我們來發揮創造力吧!

3. Too much of something can be bad for you. (phr.)物極必反

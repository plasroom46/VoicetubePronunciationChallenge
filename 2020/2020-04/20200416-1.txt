Your router is one of many routers which make up your provider's network.

供應商的網路由眾多的路由器所組成，而你的只是其中之一。

- I think my router isn’t working. I need to call my telecom company (telecom provider).

2. provider [prəˋvaɪdɚ] (n.) 供應商
- Data(手機)網路流量
- Unlimited data吃到飽網路
- Plan（網路）方案
- The website has the worst service in the world. I’m gonna call the provider(供應商) and yell at them.

3. network [ˋnɛt͵wɝk] (n.) 電腦網路
- The company just spent three million dollars on improving the computer’s network (固網）.

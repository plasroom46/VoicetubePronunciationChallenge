1. thinker [ˋθɪŋkɚ] (n.) 思想家
- Thinker (思想家)is someone who considers important subjects or produces new ideas.
- To be a critical thinker(批判性思想家).
- It is extremely important to learn how to be a critical thinker at this time of exploding information(資訊爆炸的時代).


2. subjective [səbˋdʒɛktɪv] (adj.) 主觀的
- Subjective 主觀的
- Objective 客觀的
- Trying not to make a subjective judgment(主觀的判斷） is rather(very) hard for John.

3. influence [ˋɪnflʊəns] (n.) 影響；作用；有影響的人 (或物)
- Under the influence of...受影響之下
- Driving under the influence （of alcohol)酒駕
- It is very risky to make decisions under the influence of irrational feeling(不理智的情緒下).

4. feeling [ˈfiː.lɪŋ] (n.) 感情；感觸；情緒
= emotion
- I think I’m developing feelings(萌生情感) for this girl.
- Don’t let your feelings take over you.別讓你的情緒控制你
take over = control

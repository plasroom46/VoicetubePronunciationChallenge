Youth is a one-way trip with no return. That's why you should make the most out of it.

因為青春是不可能回頭的。 這就是為什麼你應該盡情揮灑青春。

句子選自此影片：人生無法重來…為自己展開一場冒險旅程吧！ (Tripinsiders: Go On an Adventure)


1. _
* one-way [ˌwʌnˈweɪ] (adj.) 單程的；單向的，單行的
* Round-trip來回行程的
- Hi, I’d like to purchase a one-way ticket （單程票）to 屏東。
2. _
* return [rɪˈtɝːn] (n.) 回；返回；回來
- There’s no return./ There’s no going back. 沒有回頭路。
- Don’t go. There’s no return to this journey(去了這趟旅程就無法回來了）.
3. _
* make the most (out) of sth [] (phr.) 充分利用，盡量利用
: to take full advantage of something because it may not last long:
- I wanna make the most out of this long weekend. I’m going to travel to 馬祖.

She's technically an adult, but she has the practical life skills of a toddler.

她基本上是個成人，但只有幼兒的生活技能。

句子選自此影片：你的朋友有這樣的人嗎？每個朋友群裡都有個需要保母的幼稚鬼 (The Baby of the Friend Group)

Freshman in life.生活白癡

1. _
* technically [ˈtek.nɪ.kəl.i] (adv.) 事實上，嚴格說來
- Well, technically(事實上；嚴格來說）, he didn’t eat your cake cause he bought you the cake, so he’s only eating his cake.
2. _
* practical [ˈpræk.tɪ.kəl] (adj.) 實際的；實做的；有實際經驗的
- practical experience
- practical life skills
- Applied science 應用科學
- Applied math 應用數學
- I looked at your resume and I find you lack practical experiences (缺乏實際經驗）so we won’t hire you.
3. _
* toddler [ˈtɑːd.lɚ] (n.) （尤指處於學步期或剛學會走路的）幼童
- Parents should never leave a toddler unattended in a car.家長不能把幼童獨自留在車上無人照料
+ unattended => 無人照料

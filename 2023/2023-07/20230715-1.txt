1.
* assault [əˋsɔlt] (n;v.) 毆打；攻擊
: a violent attack
- The defendant was charged with assault after attacking a bystander in the street.
被告在街上襲擊一名路人後被以襲擊罪名起訴。

2.
* permission [pɚˋmɪʃən] (n.) 允許，許可，准許
: If someone is given permission to do something, they are allowed to do it.
- I asked my boss for permission to take a day off from work for a family emergency.
因為家裡有急事，我請老闆準我一天假。

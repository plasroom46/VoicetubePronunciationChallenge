1.
* barrel [ˋbærəl] (n.) 桶
: a large container, made of wood, metal, or plastic, with a flat top and bottom and curved sides that make it fatter in the middle
- The brewery uses oak barrels to age their beer, which gives it a unique flavor and aroma.
該釀酒廠使用橡木桶來陳釀他們的啤酒，使其擁有獨特的口味和香氣。

2.
* drive sth down (phr.) 壓低
: to force a price, value, etc. to go down
- The company was able to drive down the cost of production by outsourcing to a cheaper supplier.
該公司透過向更便宜的供應商外包，成功地降低了生產成本。

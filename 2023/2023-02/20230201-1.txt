Pronunciation tips:

1.Told them: Linking
2.Wanted: /ˈwɑːn.t̬ɪd/ (1) There is a shot i sound between the flap t and /d/.
3.Wanted to: Linking.
4.Height: /haɪt/ (2) No need to pronounce the “g” and “h” in this word. (3) “ei” here is /aɪ/.
5.Coordination: /koʊˌɔːr.dənˈeɪ.ʃən/ (1) Separate the word like this: co-o-di-na-tion (2) In “co,” notice the pronunciation of “o” here is /oʊ/, instead of /ɔ/. (3) The second “o” in this word, the one in “or,” is then the /ɔ/ sound.

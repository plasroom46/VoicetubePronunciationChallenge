Pronunciation tips:

* impede [ɪmˋpid] (v.) 妨礙，阻礙；阻止
: to make it more difficult for something to happen or more difficult for someone to do something
- His shyness didn’t impede his career in any way.
他害羞的個性一點也沒有妨礙到他的事業發展。

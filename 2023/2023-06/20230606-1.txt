1.
* nausea [ˋnɔʃɪə] (n.) 噁心，嘔吐感
: the feeling that you are going to vomit
- The bumpy car ride caused a wave of nausea to wash over her, making her feel queasy.
顛簸的車程讓她引起了一陣噁心感，感到不舒服。

2.
* diarrhea [͵daɪəˋriə] (n.) 腹瀉
: an illness in which the body's solid waste is more liquid than usual and comes out of the body more often
- After eating the spoiled seafood, he developed severe diarrhea and had to stay close to the restroom.
在吃了壞掉的海鮮之後，他出現了嚴重的腹瀉，必須待在洗手間附近。

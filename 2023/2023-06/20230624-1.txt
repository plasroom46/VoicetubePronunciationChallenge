1.
* horizontally [͵hɑrəˋzɑnt!ɪ] (adv.) 水平地
: a way that is parallel to the ground or to the bottom or top edge of something
- The gymnast gracefully swung horizontally on the high bar, showcasing her impressive strength and agility.
體操運動員優雅地在高低杠上水平擺動，展示了她令人驚艷的力量和靈活性。

2.
* vertically [[ˋvɝtɪk!ɪ] (adv.) 豎直地；垂直地
: straight up or at an angle of 90° to a horizontal surface or line
- The elevator moved vertically, smoothly ascending to the top floor of the skyscraper.
電梯垂直上升，平穩地到達摩天大樓的頂層。

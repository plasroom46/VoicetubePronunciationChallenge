Pronunciation tips:



1.
* boutique [buˋtik] (n.) 賣流行服飾的小商店，精品店
: a small shop that sells fashionable clothes, shoes, jewellery, etc.
- She owns a boutique in the city center specializing in handmade hats and bags.
她在市中心擁有一家精品店，專賣手工製作的帽子和包包。

2.
* nostalgic [nɑsˋtældʒɪk] (adj.) 懷舊的
: feeling happy and also slightly sad when you think about things that happened in the past
- This restaurant immediately brought back nostalgic memories from my childhood.
這家餐廳立刻勾起了我童年的懷舊回憶。

3.
* operate [ˋɑpə͵ret] (v.) （使）運行；運作；操作
: to (cause to) work, be in action or have an effect
- He is teaching the new staff how to operate the cash register.
他正在教新員工如何操作收銀機。

Pronunciation tips:

1.Begins to: linking
2.Obsessive: (1) /əbˈses.ɪv/. (2) ob-se-ssive(3) Notice the pronunciation of “o” at the beginning is /ə/
3.territory:(1) /ˈter.ə.tɔːr.i/ te-rri-tory
4.Find that: Linking. Weaken the /d/ sound in “find.”
5.Need to: Linking. It is like /nidʊ/
6.Constantly: The “t” in constantly is a stop t, so you don’t really have to pronounce it, just hold the air for a moment.
7.Contact: /ˈkɑːn.tækt/ Notice the stress of the word is at the very beginning of the word.
8.if you find that you “need to” constantly be in contact with this person: The speaker highlights “need to” by saying these two words louder.

Pronunciation tips:

1. obligations (how to pronounce the o and li)
2. unwritten (the pronunciation of tt)
3. supposed to (linking sound)
4. abide (how to pronounce the a and bi)

1.
* obligation [͵ɑbləˋgeʃən] (n.) 義務
: the fact that you are obliged to do something
- I have an obligation to inform the police under this circumstance.
在這種情況下，我有義務通知警方。

2.
* abide by [əˋbaɪd baɪ] (phr.) 遵守
: to accept or obey an agreement, decision, or rule
- Competitors must abide by the judge's decision.
參賽者必須服從裁判的裁決。

If you put shame in a petri dish, it needs three things to grow exponentially: secrecy, silence, and judgment.

如果你把羞愧感放在培養皿裡，它需要三件事才能按指數增長：保密、沉默和判斷。

Pronunciation tips:

1.in a: Linking. L
2.Petri: (1) /ˈpiː.tri (2) Notice the pronunciation of the “e” and “i” in “petri.”
3.Exponentially: (1) /ˌek.spoʊˈnen.ʃəl.i/ (2) Separate the word like this: ex-po-nen-tially (3) Notice 4.in “tially,” you don’t have to pronounce the /ə/ sound that clearly, just try to link it with /ʃ/ and /li/.
5.Secrecy: /ˈsiː.krə.si/ (1) The first “e” in this word is /i/, a long i sound.
6.Silence: The pronunciation of “i” in this word is /aɪ/.

1.
* exponentially [͵ɛkspoˋnɛnʃəlɪ] (adv.) 急劇地
: in a way that becomes quicker and quicker as something that increases becomes larger
- The quantity of chemical pollutants has increased exponentially.
化學污染物的數量劇烈增加。

2.
* secrecy [ˋsikrəsɪ] (n.) 保密
: the state of being secret or of keeping something secret
- The building was opened amid great secrecy last year.
該建築於去年在高度保密的情況下啓用。

1. _
 * psychiatric [͵saɪkɪˋætrɪk] (adj.) 精神病 (學) 的
 - A few victims were sent to psychiatric hospitals for being severely traumatized after the attack.
 + 一些受害者被送往精神病醫院，因為他們在襲擊後受到嚴重創傷。
 - psychiatric hospital =  mental hospital

2. _
 * illness [ˋɪlnɪs] (n.) (身體或精神上的) 疾病
 = sickness
 - Don't be afraid to seek for help if you think you're suffering from mental illness. It's nothing to be ashamed of.
 + 如果你認為自己患有精神疾病，就不要害怕尋求幫助。

3. _
 * auditory [ˋɔdə͵torɪ] (adj.) 聽的；聽覺的
 - Our auditory experience is often linked to our memories.
 + 聽覺經驗往往與我們的記憶有關。
 - audi-
 - Audio 聲音的;音頻的
 - Audition 試鏡；試奏
 - Audience 觀眾；聽眾

4. _
 * hallucination [hə͵lusnˋeʃən] (n.) 幻覺
 - It's possible to have hallucinations at high altitudes. That's what makes mountain climbing so dangerous.
 + 在高海拔地區可能出現幻覺，這使得登山非常危險。

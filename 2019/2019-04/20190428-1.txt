1. _
 * aid [ed] (v.) 支持；幫助
 - band-aid
 - Coffee and sweets giving you that gastroesophageal reflux again. Here, these pills can aid your pain.

 * fundamental [͵fʌndəˋmɛnt!] (adj.) 基礎的；基本的
 - essential, foundational, basic
 - Being sweet is the fundamental rule to win a girl's heart.
 + 甜蜜是贏得女孩的根本原則。

3. _
 * property [ˋprɑpɚtɪ] (n.) 屬性；性能；特性
 - One of the best properties of copper is that it conducts heat and electricity very well.
 + 銅的最佳特性之一是它可以很好地傳導熱量和電力。

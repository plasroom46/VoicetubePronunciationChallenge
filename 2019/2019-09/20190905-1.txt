This next one is one of the oldest, psychological techniques in the book. It's called mirroring.

接下來這個是書中一個最古老的、心理學的技巧，稱作鏡像。

句子選自此影片 : 讓別人喜歡你的 7 個心理學技巧！ (7 Psychological Tricks To Get Someone To Like You!)

1. _
* psychological [͵saɪkəˋlɑdʒɪk!] (adj.) 心理的；心理學的
- psychological problem/ mental issue
- It's great to have a manager who concerns the physical and psychological well-being of her employees.
+ 擁有一位關心員工身心健康的經理真是太好了。
+ well-being => 幸福安康

2. _
* technique [tɛkˋnik] (n.) 技術；技能
- Troy is keen to perfect his coding technique.
+ Troy熱衷於完善編碼技術。
+ perfect => 發音注意  /pəˈfekt/ (v.) 使完美；改善，提高

3. _
* mirroring [] (n.) 鏡像
- Do you see Vanesa is totally mirroring Troy? Let me tell you. She's got a thing for him.
+ 你看到Vanesa完全跟Troy動作依樣嗎？ 讓我告訴你，她對他有感覺。
- have a thing for sb.

Ambiverts tend to be passive in conversation, but will join in when addressed or a subject they like comes up.

中向性格的人通常在對話中是被動的，但當被提到或講到自己喜歡的主題時會加入聊天。

句子選自此影片 : 原來你不是內向也不是外向？「中向性格」大解析！ (11 Signs You're an Ambivert)


1. _
 * address [əˋdrɛs] (v.) 對…講話；處理；在 (信封或包裹) 上寫上姓名或地址
 - address an important issue(處理)
 - address the public/the nation(對…講話)
 - sb is addressed/mentioned
 - He felt embarrassed when he was addressed(mentioned) in the conversation.
 + 當他在談話中被提及時，他感到很尷尬。

2. _
 * ambivert [ˋæmbɪvɚt] (n.) 中間性格的人
 - Ambiverts tend to feel energetic at the beginning of a party, but they will soon feel exhausted and want to go home.
 + 中間性格的人在聚會開始時往往會感到精力充沛，但他們很快就會感到筋疲力盡，想要回家。
 - extrovert, extroverted
 - introvert, introverted

3. _
 * passive [ˋpæsɪv] (adj.) 被動的；消極的；順從的
 - Passive Voice 被動語態 
 - active Voice 主動語態 
 - passively
 - passive metal 鈍金屬
 - The employee was very passive, so his manager couldn't assign him any important tasks.
 + 這名員工非常被動，所以他的經理可以為他分配任何重要的任務。

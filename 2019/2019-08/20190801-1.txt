1-1. baggy [ˋbægɪ] (adj.) (衣服) 寬大的；寬鬆下垂的
There was a time(曾有段時間） when a boy loves wearing baggy jeans. I just don’t get it.

*sloppy of a garment (寬鬆的服裝）is casual and loose-fitting.
E.g. She wore a sloppy sweater and jeans.

1-2. drape [drep] (v.) 把…披、搭、蒙在…上
Drape something across/over/ on 把⋯披上

E.g. I draped my coat over the back of the chair and sat down for lunch.

Can you drape this cloth over that table? 你可以把桌巾鋪在上面嗎？

3-1. sag [sæg] (v.) (中間部位) 下垂；下陷
The bed frame sagged(床架下陷）under the weight of the heavy man.

A sagging roof 下陷的屋頂
A sagging floor下陷的地板

*A sagging mattress 下陷的床墊

4-1. unflattering [ʌnˋflætərɪŋ] (adj.) 不恭維的；貶損的；有損形象的
An unflattering photo照的很難看的照片

An unflattering dress
An unflattering color

OMG! This is such an unflattering photo of me. You’re such a horrible friend.

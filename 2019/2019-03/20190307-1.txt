Because[z] we◡have(h省) associated[ʃ] happiness(強)/ with◡these tangible things[z], it's◡impossible to get(t不送氣) core(強 慢) happiness[b].

* with(省th)◡these(th重) 舌頭頂在門牙
* impossible "o"有兩種發音 [ɑ] or [ɔ]

1-1. associate [əˋsoʃɪ͵et] (v.)將…(與…) 聯繫起來；把…聯繫在一起
associate A with B
A lot of people associate brand Nike with athletes.很多人將Nike和運動員聯想在一起。

2-1. tangible [ˋtændʒəb!] (adj.)真實的；非想像的；可觸摸的；可感知的
Can you tell us a little bit more about the tangible benefits(實質上的好處） that I'll get from this contract（從⋯這合約）?
- tangible benefits(實質上的好處）
- contract

2-2. theoretical[͵θiəˋrɛtɪk!] (adj.) 推想的；假設的
Everything I told you is still theoretical(假設的，推想的，理論上的）. We can’t charge him for the crime just yet(我們還不能將他定罪）.
- charge
- charge somebody for the crime
I charge (收費）you $20 dollars.

3-1. impossible [ɪmˋpɑsəb!] (adj.)(行動或事件) 不可能的；辦不到的
That’s impossible. How did you manage to escape my trap(陷阱)?

4-1. core [kor] (adj.)核心的；精髓的；最重要的
She is one of the core players （核心的球員）in our team and that’s why she is well-respected (很受尊敬的）by the entire school.
- well-respected by

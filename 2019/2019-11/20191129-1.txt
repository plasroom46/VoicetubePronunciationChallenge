He walks toward（走向） the house.

1-1. influence [ˋɪnflʊəns] (v.) (n.) 影響

influence, affect, impact 作動詞時都是「影響」的意思

1-1. influence (v.) (緩慢的）影響，例如對行為、性格、觀點產生間接或潛移默化的影響。
* What you read influences your thinking.

1-2. affect (v.) (一時、當下快速的）影響，沒有特定正面或負面的意思。
* The disease affects every aspect of her life.

1-3. impact (v.)（重大的）影響，當頭棒喝的語氣。通常是指激烈的事件。
* The outbreak of the scandal impacted the election results.
* Sometimes people become easily influenced by others and lose themselves.

1-4. influencer (n.) 影響者，具有影響力者
* social media influencer 社群媒體影響者

2-1. believe [bɪˋlif] (v.) 相信
* believe in God
believe in democracy
believe in science
* do something and believe that…
基於對…的相信而做某事

2-2. belief (n.) 信念，信仰
* religious belief
political belief

2-3. believe (v.)（某些存在或現象經過檢驗證實後的）觀點
* Believe in the validity([vəˋlɪdətɪ]有效性n.) of the scientific statement

2-4. faith (n.) 信仰，信念（對神的信仰，對某人的忠誠度，宗教信仰中的某派系或支派）
* The Protestant faith 新教信仰

2-5. belief vs. faith
belief 個人觀點或價值觀，無特定對錯
faith 信任一個無法經由事實檢驗證實的事件，例如對神的信仰。

* religious belief(o)
religious faith(x)

* I have a faith in God.(o)
I have a belief in God.(x)
I believe in God.(o)
I have a religious belief.(o)

* The CEO has firm beliefs about the employees’ abilities, so he never tries to micromanage(控制所有的地方；甘涉) them.

3-1. toward [təˋwɔrd] (prep.) 朝(某物，某方向)
* Drive toward the city.

3-2. toward (prep.) 關於⋯，與⋯相關
* an attitude toward work

3-3. toward (prep.) 接近（某地，某時間）
* The bench toward the lake

3-4. toward (prep.) 為了達成⋯
* I work toward a better environment.

3-5. toward vs. towards
英國、澳洲(英語體系)多用towards
加拿大、美國(美語體系) toward, towards 通用

* Our attitudes toward others actually reflect on who we really are.

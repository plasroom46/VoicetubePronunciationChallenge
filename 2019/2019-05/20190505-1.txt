The star of the menu is the combo meal. You can order an entree, a side, and a drink just by saying one easy number. 


菜單的主打餐正是「套餐」。只要簡單說出一個數字，你就能點主菜、副餐和飲料。

句子選自此影片 : 如何榨取荷包？速食業者可會了！ (Sneaky Ways Fast Food Restaurants Get You To Spend Money)

* The star of (the) something 它是某個事情的重點
- The star of the show.這場表演的重點人物

* The highlight of the something 某事的聚焦點
- The highlight of the game這場比賽的高潮
- The highlight of the trip was me falling in the pool.這趟旅行最難忘的地方就是我掉入泳池中
- The highlight of the game was his amazing slam dunk(精采的灌藍).

1. _
 * combo meal [] (n.) 組合餐；套餐
 - Combination meal = Combo meal = Combo 組合餐
 - Set menu套餐
 - Do you want to order a combo meal? I am not that hungry today and maybe we can share a combo.
 + 你想點一份套餐嗎？我今天不是那麼餓，也許我們可以分享一個組合。
 - Happy meal快樂兒童餐

2. _
 * entrée [ˋɑntre] (n.) 主菜；正菜
 - main dish
 - Escargot（s)  /ˌesˈkɑːr.ɡoʊ/ (n.) 田螺；食用鍋牛
 - Foie gras /ˌfwɑːˈɡrɑː/ (n.) 鵝肝醬
 - Pâté /pæˈteɪ/ = Liver mousse  /muːs/ (n.) 肝醬；肉醬；魚醬
 - steak tartare /ˌsteɪk tɑːrˈtɑːr/ 韃靼牛排；生拌牛肉
 - I think we can get two entrees, for now. Do you want anything else? Perhaps some cocktails.
 + 我想我們可以得到兩個主菜，現在，你還想要別的什麼調酒嗎？
 - cocktail 調酒

3. _
 * side dish [] (n.) (餐館的) 配菜；附加菜
 - side = side dish
 - I made some garlic butter and sauteed some veggies in the butter. It was a great side dish for our anniversary dinner.
 + 我做了一些大蒜黃油，並在黃油中炒了一些蔬菜，這對我們的周年晚宴來說是一道很棒的配菜。
 + saute /sɔːˈteɪ/ (v.) => 煎炸炒

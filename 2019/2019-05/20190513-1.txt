Those are horoscopes, right? They're purposely written vague enough so anyone can really fit and identify with them.

那些是星座，沒錯吧？他們故意寫得非常模糊，所以任何人都可以認同並對號入座。

句子選自此影片 : 你是什麼星座？讓我們一起破解星座迷思吧！ (Horoscopes: Hate it or like it?)


1. _
 * horoscope [ˋhɔrə͵skop] (n .) 星相；占星術
 - David's horoscope said that it's not a good day for him to leave his house. He ignored the advice and went out for donut. He got hit by a bus.
 + David的星座表示，現在離開他的房子並不是一個好日子，他忽略了這個建議，然後出去吃了一輛他被公共汽車擊中的甜甜圈。
 - -scope => 觀察儀器
 - Microscope 顯微鏡
 - Telescope 望遠鏡
 - Stethoscope /ˈsteθ.ə.skoʊp/ 聽診器

2. _
 * purposely [ˋpɝpəslɪ] (adv.) 故意地；刻意地
 - Brooklyn dropped her handkerchief purposely(on purpose) to get the handsome man behind him to pick it up for her. To her surprise, the man stepped on it and walked away.
 + Brooklyn故意放棄她的手帕，讓那個身後的英俊男子為她檢，讓她驚訝的是，男人踩到它並走開了。
 - handkerchief  /ˈhæŋ.kɚ.tʃiːf/ (n.) 手帕

3. _
 * vague [veg] (adj .) 含糊的；不明確的
 - Vague description 模糊的描述
 - Vague memory 模糊的記憶
 - Vague idea 模糊的想法
 - vogue /voʊɡ/ => 時尚(相似)
 - Dave is a new vogue for taking pictures and dancing. Everybody is doing it.
 + 這是一個新的時尚，用於拍照和跳舞，每個人都在這樣做。

4. _
 * identify [aɪˋdɛntə͵faɪ] (v.) 認出；識別
 - The man cried uncontrollably after identifying her daughter who had been lost for seventeen years.
 + 在確認失去了十七年的女兒後，這名男子無法控制地哭了起來。
 - Hannah Montana hid her identity as a pop star from her classmates. Having two different identities allows her to have the best of both worlds.
 + Hannah Montana隱藏了她作為流行歌星的身份，她擁有兩種不同的身份，這使她能夠擁有兩全其美的完美世界。

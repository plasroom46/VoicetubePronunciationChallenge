Pronunciation Tips: 

hydrogen - long i, schwa sound
vehicle - long e, silent h, short i


1.
* grid [grɪd] (n.) 電力網
: a system of wires through which electricity is connected to different power stations across a region
* off-grid [ɔf grɪd] (adj.) 不和主電網接通的
- Derives from off-the-grid 
- Solar panels and wind turbines are two methods of generating electricity off-grid.
太陽能板和風力發電機是離網發電的兩種方法。

2.
* renewable energy [rɪˋnjuəb! ˋɛnɚdʒɪ] (n.) 可再生能源
: energy that is produced using the sun, wind, etc., or from crops, rather than using fuels such as oil or coal
- The Canadian side of Niagara Falls generates nearly 2 million kilowats of electricity, making it a key source of renewable energy.
 加拿大一側的尼加拉大瀑布發電近200萬千瓦，使其成為可再生能源的主要來源。

3.
* integrate [ˋɪntə͵gret] (v.) 使合併
: to combine two or more things in order to become more effective.
- Our new light-rail system will integrate with our existing network of subways and buses to provide a comprehensive public transit service. 
我們新的輕軌系統會和現有的捷運和公車的網撞系統來做合併，去提供一個更完整的大眾交通服務。

發音
especially a 連音 有4個音接， C 裡面是soft C 所以唸S的音
you are a girl 連音
fight against 連音


1.
* especially [əˋspɛʃəlɪ] (adv.) 特別, 尤其
: very much; more than usual or more than other people or things
- I love to listen to music, especially classical music.
我喜歡聽音樂，尤其是古典音樂。
: 專門，特地
- I bought this carpet epsecially for you.「我特地為你買了這條地毯」。

2.
* against [əˋgɛnst] (prep.) 反對，違反
: disagreeing with a plan or activity
- Are you for or against my plan?
你是支持還是反對我的計畫？
* against all odds 儘管困難重重；在極為不利的情況下
- Against all odds, he recovered from lung cancer. 
「儘管困難重重，他還是從肺癌中痊愈了」
* against one’s better judgment 「違心」假如你覺得某人的衣服很醜，但你還是在她詢問你的時候稱讚她
- Against my better judgment, I compliment her clothes.

3.
* passive [pæsɪv] (adj.) 被動的,消極的
: not acting to influence or change a situation; allowing other people to be in control
- She is passive in this relationship.
她在這段感情中很被動。
passive 被動語態的 < - > active 主動語態的
*  impassive (adj.)「神情冷漠的，淡漠的」

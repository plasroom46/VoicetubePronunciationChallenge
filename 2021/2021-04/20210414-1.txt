1.
* frequency [ˋfrikwənsɪ] (n .) 1. （事件發生的）頻度，次數；頻繁 2. （尤指聲波或無線電波每秒振動的）頻率
: the number of times something happens within a particular period, or the fact of something happening often or a large number of times/ the number of times that a wave, especially a light, sound, or radio wave, is produced within a particular period, especially one second
- The frequency of going to the toilet increased when I got pregnant.
我懷孕之後上廁所頻率上升了。
- The human ear cannot hear this high-frequency sound, but some animals can.
人類的耳朵不能聽到這麼高頻率的聲音，但有一些動物可以。
* frequent [ˋfrikwənt] (adj.) 頻繁發生的；常見的
: happening often
- We are used to him arguing with the teacher. It’s a frequent occurrence in our class.
我們習慣他跟老師吵架了，這件事在班上很常發生。

2.
* extend [ɪkˈstɛnd] (v.) 擴大；擴展；使增加長度
: to add to something in order to make it bigger or longer
- After heating up the glass, you can extend it by pulling it with tools.
把玻璃加熱後，你可以用工具延展它。
* extended [ɪkˋstɛndɪd] (adj.) 延伸的；長的；延長的
: long or longer than usual
- We had an extended winter break because of COVID-19.
因為疫情的關係，我們的寒假延長了。

* lifespan [ˈlaɪfˌspæn] (n.) 壽命；可持續年限，有效期

- The minimum lifespan of a Greenland shark is 272 years, making it one of the animals that have the longest lives.

Pronunciation tips

tech /tek/
Feasibly /ˈfiː.zə.bli/

1.
* emulate [ˋɛmjə͵let] (v.) 效仿，模仿；和…競爭，努力趕上
Synonyms - copy, imitate, etc.
: to copy something achieved by someone else and try to do it as well as they have
- Sydney does her best to emulate Serena’s charm but to no avail.
Sydney 盡她所能的效仿 Serena 的魅力，但無濟於事。

2.
* feasibly [ˈfiː.zə.bli] (adv.) 可行地
: in a way that could be made, done, or achieved; possibly
- Make sure that this plan can be feasibly executed before introducing it to the team.
先確保這個計畫能可行地被執行再推介給團隊。

3.
* handle [ˋhænd!] (v.) 處理，應付，負責
: to deal with, have responsibility for, or be in charge of
- Cole handles things with such grace, it’s hard to not be in awe.
Cole 應付事物是如此的體面，很難不令人敬佩。
- Door handle 門把

你的工作勢必會佔據你生命中很大一部分，而真正感到滿足的唯一方法就是做你認為很棒的工作，而唯一能做到很棒的工作就是熱愛你所做的。

Pronunciation tips:

Blended words: fill a
Satisfied (3 syllables)
What you (sometimes sounds like a ‘ch’ sound at the end of what)

1.
* fill [fɪl] (v.) 裝滿、填滿、使充滿
: To fill means to make or become full, or to use up the empty space.
- Before we can swim, we need to fill the pool with water. 
在游泳之前，我們需要將水池填滿水。

2.
* truly [ˋtrulɪ] (adv.) 真實、真正地
: Truly is a word used to emphasize that what you are saying is true.
- I truly care about what happens to him. 
我真的很關心他發生什麼事。

3.
* satisfied [ˋsætɪs͵faɪd] (adj.) 感到滿意的、滿足的
: To be satisfied means to be pleased because you received what you wanted, or because something happened in the way that you wanted it to.
- I was very hungry before, but after that huge dinner, I feel very satisfied. 
我本來很餓，但吃完那頓豐盛的晚餐後，我感到很滿足。

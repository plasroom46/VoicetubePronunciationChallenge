Pronunciation tips:

1. security /səˈkjʊr.ə.t̬i/ (In the American accent, we pronounce the 't' as a 'd' sound.)
2. empower (Remember to stress the second syllable.)

1.
* empower [ɪmˋpaʊɚ] (v.) 給（某人）做…的權力；授權；使自主
: to give someone official authority or the freedom to do something
- A good sports coach will empower the team players, instead of just telling them what and how to play.
一位優秀的體育教練會賦予團隊成員權力，而不只是教他們而已。

2.
* food security [fud sɪˋkjʊrətɪ] (n.) 食物生產充足
: the fact that a place is able to produce or obtain enough food to feed its population
- Temporarily losing power and water during the typhoon gave me a small idea of what it’s like to live without food security.
在颱風期間短暫的斷電斷水讓我對沒有充足糧食的生活有了一點概念。

3.
* economy [ɪˋkɑnəmɪ] (n.) 經濟；經濟制度
: the system of trade and industry by which the wealth of a country is made and used
- Fixing the sidewalks so that shops can get more foot traffic would be really helpful for the local economy.
整修人行道，讓商店獲得更多人潮，這對當地經濟非常有幫助。

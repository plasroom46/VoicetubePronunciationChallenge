Atmosphere (has 5 syllables, and the accent should be on the first “A”)

1.
* enjoy [ɪnˋdʒɔɪ] (v.) 享受…的樂趣；喜愛；欣賞
: to get pleasure from something
- While it is good exercise, the main reason I ride a bicycle is simply because I enjoy it.
雖然騎腳踏車是很好的鍛煉，但我騎車的主要原因純粹是因為喜歡這個運動。

2.
* wonderful [ˋwʌndɚfəl] (adj.) 絕妙的，令人驚嘆的，極好的
: If something is wonderful, then it’s extremely good.
- When I went to Alberta and saw the Rocky Mountains, it was a truly wonderful sight that I’ll never forget.
當我去艾伯塔看落基山脈時，那真是我永遠不會忘記的美妙景象。

3.
* atmosphere [ˋætməs͵fɪr] (n.) 氣氛；環境
: The atmosphere is the character, feeling, or mood of a place or situation. 
- This horror movie is so great because the music and dark tones create a really creepy atmosphere that leaves you terrified of what’s coming next.
恐怖電影拍得非常棒，因為它的音樂和暗黑色調營造出一種非常令人毛骨悚然的氛圍，讓你對接下來發生的事情感到恐懼。

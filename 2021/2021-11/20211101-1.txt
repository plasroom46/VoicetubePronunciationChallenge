Pronunciation tips:

appreciating (second syllable = long ee sound)
about this (linking words)
women (plural for the singular “woman”)

1.
* appreciate [əˋpriʃɪ͵et] (v.) 欣賞、賞識
: to recognize how good someone or something is and to value him, her, or it
- He used to listen to rock music only but has recently started to appreciate classical music more and more.
他原本只聽搖滾樂，但最近開始越來越能夠欣賞古典音樂。

2.
* process [ˋprɑsɛs] (n.) 過程、進程
: a series of actions that you take in order to achieve a result
- Repetition is important during the learning process.
學習過程中，重複是個很重要的環節。

3.
* empower [ɪmˋpaʊɚ] (v.) 授權、使能夠
: to give someone official authority or the freedom to do something
- She isn’t empowered to make important decisions at her company.
她在公司裡沒有權利做重要的抉擇。

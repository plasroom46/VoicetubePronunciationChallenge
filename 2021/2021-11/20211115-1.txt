Don't let the noise of others' opinions drown out your own inner voice, and most important, have the courage to follow your heart and intuition. They somehow already know what you truly want to become.

不要讓別人意見的噪音淹沒了自己內心的聲音，最重要的是，要有勇氣跟隨自己的內心和直覺，它們在某種程度上已經知道你真正想成為什麼。

[你的時間有限，所以不要浪費時間過別人的生活。 (Stay hungry, stay foolish. Steve Jobs)](https://tw.voicetube.com/videos/35151)

Pronunciation tips:

1. opinion [ uh-pin-yuhn ] (break into four syllables)
2. drown [ droun ] (can open your mouth slightly wider)
3. courage (sound like Kur-ridge)
4. intuition [ in-too-ish-uhn, -tyoo- ] (you may have heard two different pronunciations before)

1.
* inner [ˋɪnɚ] (adj.) 內部、核心的
: inside or contained within something else
- He told funny jokes to disguise his inner sadness.
他講了一些笑話來隱藏心中的難過。

2.
* courage [͵kɝɪdʒ] (n.) 膽量、勇氣
: the ability to control your fear in a dangerous or difficult situation
- The front-line soldiers showed great courage in battle.
前線士兵在戰場上展現出巨大的勇氣。

3.
* intuition [͵ɪntjuˋɪʃən] (n.) 直覺
: (knowledge from) an ability to understand or know something immediately based on your feelings rather than facts
- My intuition tells me that her boyfriend might propose to her at the party tonight.
我的直覺告訴我，她男朋友今晚會在派對中向她求婚。

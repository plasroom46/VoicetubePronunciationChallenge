Pronunciation tips:



1.
* transform [trænsˈfɔrm] (v.) 轉變 ； 變革
: To change completely into another state or something else
- Name a movie where cars transform into robots.
說出一部由汽車變身成機器人的電影。

2.
* virtual [ˈvɚtʃuəl] (adj.) 虛擬的
: Something that’s almost real, but not 100% real
- Do we actually live in a virtual reality?
我們是否真實活在虛擬實境中?

3.
* battleground [ˈbætlˌɡraʊnd] (n.) 戰場 ； 鬥爭的舞台
: a place where a battle is being fought or has been fought in the past
-  We visited the Normandy battlegrounds and paid our respects to the brave souls.
我們參訪了諾曼第戰場，並向逝去的英靈致敬。

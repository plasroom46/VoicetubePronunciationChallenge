Pronunciation tips:

Astonishing
Lack of
Deficit

1.
* astonishing [əˋstɑnɪʃɪŋ] (adj.) 令人驚訝的；驚人的
: very surprising
- The total amount of trash a human being produces in a year is quite astonishing.

2.
* compensate [ˋkɑmpən͵set] (v.) 彌補，補償
: to provide something good or useful in place of something or to make someone feel better about something that has failed or been lost or missed
- They’re just trying to make you feel bad to compensate for how they really feel inside.

3.
* deficit [ˋdɛfɪsɪt] (n.) 赤字；逆差；虧損
: the total amount by which money spent is more than money received
- A lack or a deficit of calcium can lead to dental changes, cataracts, alterations in the brain, and osteoporosis, which causes the bones to become brittle.

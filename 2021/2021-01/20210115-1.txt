Throw your phone off a cliff, for a bit, so you can finally notice stuff, especially your partner and your mom.

把手機丟下懸崖，這樣你才能夠注意到別人，特別是你的伴侶和你的母親。

一分鐘內學會快樂的秘密 (The Secrets of Happiness – in 60 Seconds)


1.
* cliff [klɪf] (n.) 懸崖，峭壁
- Stay away from the cliff! It’s dangerous.
離懸涯遠一點，那裡很危險。
* cliff edge [klɪf ɛdʒ] (n.) 懸崖邊緣
-  I’m wondering why the quality of the service in this restaurant has gone over the cliff edge recently. 
我很好奇為何這家餐廳的服務品質突然變糟了。

2.
* throw [θro] (v.) 投；拋
- The boy accidentally threw the ball to his opponents when they’re playing dodgeball.
那位小男孩在玩躲避球的時候，不小心把球丟給他的對手。
* throw sb. to the dogs [θro...tu ðə dɔgz] (idiom.) 棄…來自保
* throw sth. back in sb.'s face [θro...bæk ɪn...fes] (idiom.) 翻舊帳

3.
* notice [ˋnotɪs] (v.) 注意到，感覺到
- Have you noticed that Harry and Jenny are holding hands?
你有注意到Harry 跟 Jenny 手牽著手嗎？

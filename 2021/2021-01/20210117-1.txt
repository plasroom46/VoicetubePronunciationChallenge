考試來不及準備？最強抱佛腳三招！ (3 Last Minute Test Taking Strategies that WORK! | Exam and Test Prep)


1.
* equation [ɪˈkweɪʒən] (n.) 數學或化學當中等式；方程式
- I can't solve this complicated equation. Can you work it out with me?
我解不了這個複雜的等式。你能來幫我一起想嗎？
* equate [ɪˈkweɪt] (v.) 使相等；同等看待、將…視為相等

2.
* specific [spəˈsɪfɪk] (adj.) 具體的，明確的
= clear、exact 
<-> abstract、unclear、obscure
- I like what you talk about in your essay, but I think it's a little too ambiguous. Can you try to make your points more specific?
我喜歡你在這篇作文中談及的內容，但我覺得它有點太模糊了。你可以試著讓你的論點更確切一點嗎？

3.
* definition [ˌdɛfəˈnɪʃən] (n.) 定義；解釋，說明
- Everyone has their own definition of happiness.
每個人對於何謂幸福都有不同的定義。
* define [dɪˈfaɪn] (v.)  給（尤指單詞）下定義；說明，解釋
: to say and explain what the meaning of something, especially a word, is
- The borderline between kindness and evil can sometimes be hard to define.
善惡之間的界線有時很難界定。

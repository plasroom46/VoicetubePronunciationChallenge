An ASMR artist is someone who focus mainly on visual triggers and sounds to bring you some type of relaxation.

ASMR 藝術家主要專注於視覺上的刺激，以及會讓你感到某種程度上放鬆的聲音。

什麼！ASMR 可以減緩焦慮！？ (Anxiety Relief with ASMR)


1.
* visual [​vɪʒuəl​] (adj.) 視覺的
: Relating to seeing
- The winner of the 92nd Academy Award for Best Visual Effects is the movie “1917”. 第九十二屆奧斯卡最佳視覺效果獎的得主是電影《1917》。
* auditory [​ɔdə͵torɪ​] (adj.) 聽覺的
: Of or about hearing
- I have a bad auditory memory, so I prefer looking at a word and memorizing it rather than just listening to it. 我聽覺記憶很差，所以我比較傾向用看的記住一個字，而非用聽的。

2.
* trigger [​trɪgɚ] (n.) （引起某種壞事的）一種情況
: An event that is the cause of a particular situation
- People start putting trigger warnings in front of articles to prevent readers from thinking about their trauma. 大家開始在文章前面加上「敏感警告」，避免讀者想起他們經歷過的創傷。
* trigger [​trɪgɚ] (n.) （槍的）扳機
: A part of a gun that causes the gun to fire when pressed 
- pull the trigger 開槍
- The detective wants to find out who pulled the trigger. 警探想找出開槍的是誰。

3.
* relaxation [​rilæksˋeʃən] (n.) 放鬆
: The feeling of being relaxed
- For me, making origami stars is my form of relaxation. 摺紙星星是我放鬆的方式。

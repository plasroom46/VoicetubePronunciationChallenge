You figured out your triggers for this bad habit and you decided on ways to avoid and replace these triggers with healthier alternatives.

你找出了這些壞習慣的誘發因子，接著訂出避免的方法，並用更健康的選擇來取代。

壞習慣！到底要如何完全戒掉？！ (How to Break Bad Habits)


1.
* figure out [ˈfɪɡ.jɚ aʊt] (phr.) 弄懂，弄明白；想出
: To figure out something is to solve or understand it.
- In the previous episode, Sherlock Holmes figured out the mastermind behind the murder case.
在前一集中，夏洛克 · 福爾摩斯弄清楚這樁暗殺事件的主謀者了。
* go figure (phr.)  去搞清楚吧！（衍生：真搞不懂。）
: Is used to express disbelief incredulity
- Did you know that Josh left the country without telling anyone? Go figure.
你知道 Josh 在沒告知任何人就離開國家了嗎？真搞不懂！

2.
* trigger [ˈtrɪɡ.ɚ] (n.) （引起某種壞事的）一件事（或情況）
同義字: cause, reason
: Something that causes another.
- Remember to add trigger warnings before releasing this article, as suicide is a sensitive subject matter and might potentially upset readers.
在發表這篇文章前，記得加上情緒觸發警告，因為輕生是個敏感的主題，有可能使讀者情緒不穩定。

* trigger [ˈtrɪɡ.ɚ] (v.) 引起某種壞事的 
: To initiate something, usually something bad.

3.
* alternative [ɑːlˈtɝː.nə.t̬ɪv] (n.) （計劃或方法）可替代的；可供選擇的
: An alternative means an option that could act as a replacement.
- Before sending in your application for this university, you should consider other alternatives.
在送出這所大學的申請資料前，你應該考慮一下其他的選項。

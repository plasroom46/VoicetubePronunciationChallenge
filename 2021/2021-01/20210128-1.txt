I like to bring a ton of different anger sources with me. Whether it’s external irritations, internal worrying, traumatic incidents from the past, or even just a memory or two that makes me feel embarrassed or ashamed.

我出門喜歡帶各種不同來源的怒氣。不管是受外在的刺激、內在的擔憂、過去的創傷，又或者是一兩個使我尷尬、難為情的回憶。

【Anna Akana】這些人生中的情緒包袱，你是不是也帶在身上？ (What's In My Baggage?)

1.
* irritation [ˌɪr.əˈteɪ.ʃən] (n.) 激怒，惱怒；惱人的事
: the feeling of being angry or annoyed, or something that makes you feel like this
v. irritate
adj. irritable
- Your behavior has grown from mild irritation to outright harassment.
你的行為已經從輕微的干擾累積成無疑的騷擾。
- skin irritation 皮膚過敏發癢、不適


2.
* internal [ɪnˈtɝː.nəl] (adj.) 體內的；內部的；國內的
: existing or happening inside a person, object, organization, place, or country
adv. internally
<->external
- Mental clarity is dependent on a combination of external systems and internal maintenance.
清淨的心理狀態取決於外在系統和內在管理的組合。

3.
* traumatic [trɑːˈmæt̬.ɪk] (adj.) （經歷）痛苦難忘的，造成精神創傷的
: If an experience is traumatic, it causes you severe emotional shock and upset.
n. trauma
- Being the victim of domestic violence has proved to be a traumatic experience for Kelly, as it continues to affect her day to day life even as the case is resolved.
身為一個家暴的受害者確實對 Kelly 而言是個痛苦難忘的經驗，由於這持續的影響著她的日常生活，即便案子已了結。

4.
* exaggeration [ɪɡˌzædʒ.əˈreɪ.ʃən] (n.) 誇張；誇大；言過其實
（hyperbole 誇飾法）

5.
* sarcasm [ˈsɑːr.kæz.əm] (n.) 諷刺，挖苦，嘲笑
- sarcasm, sarcastic 諷刺 （似：irony, ironic）

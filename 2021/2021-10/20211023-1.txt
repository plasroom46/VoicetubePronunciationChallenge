Pronunciation tips:

Flatulence
Deploy
Strategically

1.
* flatulence [ˋflætʃələns] (n.) 腸胃脹氣
: gas in the stomach and bowels
- That stinks! That seal over there has had a lot of flatulence lately.

2.
* deploy [dɪˋplɔɪ] (v.) 有效運用；發揮…的作用
: to use something or someone, especially in an effective way
- When did he deploy the troops?

3.
* strategically [strəˋtidʒɪk!ɪ] (adv.) 戰略上，戰略性地
: in a way that helps to achieve a plan, for example in business or politics
- We must think strategically to defeat our opponents.

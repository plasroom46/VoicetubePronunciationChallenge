Pronunciation tips:

Inspiring (in·spai·ur·uhng)
Collaborative (kuh·la·br·uh·tiv) /kəˈlæb.ɚ.ə.t̬ɪv/
Innovation (i·nuh·vay·shn)

1.
* inspiring [ɪnˋspaɪrɪŋ] (adj.) 鼓舞人心的
: encouraging, or making you feel you want to do something
- She was an inspiring example to her followers.

2.
* collaborative [kəˋlæbərətɪv] (adj.) 合作的；協作的
: involving two or more people working together for a special purpose
- The presentation was a collaborative effort by all the children in the class.

3.
* innovation [͵ɪnəˋveʃən] (n.) 新觀念；新方法；（新觀念或方法的）創造，使用
: (the use of) a new idea or method
- Innovation distinguishes between a leader and a follower.

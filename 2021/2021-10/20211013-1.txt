Validity (va-li-di-dy)

1.
* valid [ˋvælɪd] (adj.) 確鑿的；合理的；有根據的；讓人信服的
: based on truth or reason; able to be accepted
- My way of thinking might be different from yours, but it's equally valid.

2.
* overarching [͵ovɚˋɑrtʃɪŋ] (adj.) 首要的；包羅萬象的，支配一切的
: most important, because of including or affecting all other areas
- The overarching theme of the election campaign was tax cuts.

3.
* theory [ˋθiərɪ] (n.) 理論；學說；意見
: a formal statement of the rules on which a subject of study is based or of ideas that are suggested to explain a fact or event or, more generally, an opinion or explanation
- In theory, the journey should take three hours, but in practice, it usually takes four because of roadworks.

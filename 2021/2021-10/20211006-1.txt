Nemo's Garden is an alternative agriculture project where we are trying to find a way to grow plants underwater in underwater greenhouses.

「尼莫的花園」是一個替代性的農業計畫，我們正在努力尋找一種在水下溫室種植水下植物的方法。

[農業的未來在水底 (The Future of Farming Is Underwater)](https://tw.voicetube.com/videos/71327)

[主持人解講](https://cdn.voicetube.com/everyday_records/5708/1625146372.mp3)

Pronunciation tips:

Alternative (aal · tur · nuh · tuhv)
Agriculture (a·gruh·kuhl·chr)
Find a way

1.
* alternative [ɔlˋtɝnətɪv] (adj.) （計劃或方法）可替代的；可供選擇的
: An alternative plan or method is one that you can use if you do not want to use another one. 
- An alternative venue for the concert is being sought.
正在尋找其他可舉辦音樂會的地方。

2.
* agriculture [ˋægrɪ͵kʌltʃɚ] (n.) 農業；農藝，農學
: farming
- The area depends on agriculture for most of its income.
這個地區的大部分收入來自農業。

3.
* greenhouse [ˋgrin͵haʊs] (n.) 溫室，暖房
: a building with a roof and sides made of glass, used for growing plants that need warmth and protect
- There’s a lot of tomatoes in this greenhouse.
在這溫室裡有很多番茄。

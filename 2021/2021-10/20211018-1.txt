Pronunciation tips:

Seems important 可以連音 /seem-zim-portant

Finance (重音放在中段 "nan")

Impact (重音放在前段 "im" 時為名詞，重音放在後段 "pact" 時為動詞)

1.
* finance [faɪˋnæns] (n.) 資金；財務管理；財政；金融
: (the management of) a supply of money
- The project will only go ahead if the company can raise the necessary finance.
公司必須提高必要的資金，才能夠讓這個案子繼續下去。

2.
* impact [ɪmˋpækt] (n.) 衝擊（力）；撞擊（力）
: the force or action of one object hitting another
- The impact of the car crash left her with minor internal injuries.
車禍的撞擊力使她得到一點內傷。

3.
* option [ˋɑpʃən] (n.) 選擇，選項；選擇權
: one thing that can be chosen from a set of possibilities, or the freedom to make a choice
- I like coming to this food court because there are so many options to choose from.
我喜歡來這個美食街，因為選擇很多。

Pronunciation tips:

To others = tuh others
Not to = no-tuh
But until = buttun-til
Consumption = con-sump-shun

1.
* advice [ədˈvʌɪs] (n.) 意見；忠告；勸告
: an opinion that someone offers you about what you should do or how you should act in a particular situation
- If you want my advice, make sure the computer you buy has really good fans.
給你個建議，你買的電腦最好要配個好的風扇。

2.
* energy [ˈɛnədʒi] (n.) 能，能量；能源
: the power from something such as electricity or oil that can do work, such as providing light and heat
- You can tell my smartphone is getting old now because it just drains energy so fast.
可以看出我的智慧型手機越來越舊了，因為它電量消耗的太快了。

3.
* consumption [kənˈsʌm(p)ʃ(ə)n] (n.) 消費量；消耗量
: the amount used or eaten
- If you’re really worried about your health, it might be time to look at your sugar and salt consumption.
如果你真的擔心自己的健康，可能是時候該留意一下糖和鹽的攝取量了。

Pronunciation tips:



1.
* rapid [ˈræpɪd] (adj.) 迅速的
: fast or sudden
- The 1990s is a period of rapid growth
1990 年代是一個迅速發展的時期。

2.
* takeover [ˈtekˌovɚ] (n.) 取代；接管
: to get control of something
- The Biden administration condemned the military takeover in Myanmar.
拜登政府譴責了緬甸的軍事奪權。

3.
* calamity [kəˈlæmɪti] (n.) 災難；災禍
: a serious accident or bad event causing damage or suffering
-  The recent heavy downpour in Henan was a major calamity.
最近發生在河南的暴雨是場大災難。

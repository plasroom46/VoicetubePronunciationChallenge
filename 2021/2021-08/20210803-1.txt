Most routers have two antennas on them, and ideally you could position one of them vertically and one horizontally.

大多數的路由器有兩根天線。理想上一根應與地面垂直，另一根則與地面水平。

Pronunciation tips:



1.
* antenna [ænˈten.ə] (n.) 天線；觸角
: a rod, wire, or other device used to transmit or receive radio or television signals
- The reception is very poor. Let me adjust the antenna.
收訊太差了。讓我調整一下天線。
- Animals and insects have antennas（觸角）as well.

2.
* vertical [ˈvɝː.t̬ə.kəl] (adj.) 垂直的；立式的
: standing or pointing straight up or at an angle of 90° to a horizontal surface
- Rain or shine, our flagpole stands vertically above the ground.
不論風雨，我們的旗桿垂直屹立於地。

3.
* horizontal [ˌhɔːr.ɪˈzɑːn.t̬əl] (adj.) 水平的；橫式的
: parallel to the surface or ground.
- Keep the patient horizontal with the feet slightly raised.
讓病人平躺，雙腳略微抬高。
* horizon [həˈraɪ.zən] (n.) 地平線

* insert [ɪnˋsɝt] (v.) 插入
: to put something inside something else
- In order to continue the game, I’ll have to insert another dollar to play.
如果想要繼續玩剛剛的遊戲，我必須再投一塊錢才可以玩。

2.
* unwillingly [ʌnˋwɪlɪŋlɪ] (adv.) 不情願地
: in a way that involves someone doing something they do not want to do.
- Peter was unwillingly to believe that his house was robbed by thieves last night.
Peter 不願意相信昨天晚上他的家遭小偷了.

3.
* virus [vaɪrəs] (n.) 病毒；電腦病毒
: an extremely small piece of organic material that causes disease in humans, animals, and plants
- The Coronavirus is one of the deadly viruses that has appeared over the last 10 years.
新冠狀病毒是過去 10 年以來出現過最致命病毒之一。
- My brother likes to download movies online and he accidentally downloaded a virus onto our computer.
我弟弟很喜歡下載東西，結果不小心下載到一個電腦病毒。

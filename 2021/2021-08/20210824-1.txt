Generally it means that a country has declared their non-participation during a war, and more specifically cannot be counted on to oppose a belligerent country.

中立表示國家在戰爭中不參與任何一方，更準確來說，中立國對交戰任何一方都不採取敵對行動。

Pronunciation tips:



1.
* declare [dɪˈklɛr] (v.) 宣告；聲明
: to announce something officially
- America declared war on Japan in 1941.
美國在1941對日本宣戰。

2.
* oppose [əˈpoʊz] (v.) 反對；對抗
: to disagree with or fight against
- Most of the local residents opposed the closing of the school.
當地大多數居民反對關閉這所學校。

3.
* belligerent [bəˈlɪdʒərənt] (adj.) 好戰的；交戰的
: wishing to fight and argue, or a country that is currently at war
- The belligerent countries are having difficulties funding the war.
交戰國均缺乏戰爭資金。

我們對外界的感知與視覺息息有關。

Pronunciation tips:

Subscribe - 兩個音節 Sub-scribe， 裡面有個silent e 所以那個 “i” 是發 ï

Physical - 三個音節，ph是發 F 的音，y是發 i 的音然後s發z的音

Phenomenon - 4個音節，ph 是發 f 的音

1.
* perception [pZˋsєpʃәn] (n.) 見解，觀念，看法
: a belief or opinion, often held by many people and based on how things seem
- A lot of people say that the most common perception of smoking is that it’s bad for you.
很多人的對抽菸的觀念就是對你身體不好的。

2.
* link [lIŋk] (v.) 使聯繫；使相關聯
: to make a connection between two or more people, things, or ideas
- Kevin bought a new iphone so he needs to link his phone to his computer to back up all his data.
Kevin 買來了一台新的 iPhone ，所以他必須把他的手機連結到他的電腦去才能備份他的質料。

3.
* vision [ˋvIʒәn] (n.) 眼光；遠見；眼力
: the ability to imagine how a country, society, industry, etc. could develop in the future and to plan for this
- My boss had a vision that we could use the internet to make our sales better.
我的老闆有個遠見，那就是藉由網路來幫助我們的業績變得更好。

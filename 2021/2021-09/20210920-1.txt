1.
* panic [ˋpænɪk] (n.) 恐慌，驚慌
: a sudden strong feeling of fear that prevents reasonable thought and action
- No need to panic, it’s just a fire drill, we don’t need to evacuate the building. 
別恐慌，這只是一個消防演習，我們不需要撤離大樓。

2.
* sign up (ph.) 註冊；報名登記；簽約；簽署；簽名
:  to agree to become involved in an organized activity
- Don’t forget to sign up for the debating club by Friday noon. 
別忘了在星期五中午前報名參加辯論社。

3.
* the rest (ph.) 其餘者
: the other things, people, or parts that remain or that have not been mentioned
- John, Peter and Grag, please go and (run) five more run of the field, the rest of your can take a rest.
John, Peter和Grag請你們再跑操場五圈，剩下的人可以再休息一下。
* rest (v.) 休息；睡
: to cause someone or something to stop doing a particular activity or stop being active for a period of time in order to relax and get back your strength

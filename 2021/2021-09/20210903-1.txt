Pronunciation tips:

Associate - 4個音節 裡面的C是發sh的音
Apparatus US: /ˌæp.əˈræt̬.əs/ UK: /ˌæp.əˈreɪ.təs/
Executive - 4個音節，重音在第二個音接。而且 x 有點像gs 的音。

1.
* genius [dʒinjəs] (n.) 天資，天才
: very great and rare natural ability or skill, especially in a particular area such as science or art.
- Kevin is a genius for finding a way to get out of the forest when we were lost.
Kevin 是一個天才，當我們迷路的時候他找到出去的路了。

2.
* apparatus [æpəˋretəs] (n.) 手法
: the functional processes by means of which a systematized activity is carried out
- The demonstrators set up a piece of apparatus for the experiment
示範者為了實驗搭建一個設備。

3.
* associate [əˋsoʃɪ͵et] (v.) 將…（與…）聯繫起來
: to connect someone or something in your mind with someone or something else
* Be associate with sb 與（尤指他人不贊同的人）交往
: to spend time with a group of people, especially people who are disapproved of
- Diana associated herself with some pretty strange people recently.
Diana 最近和一些滿奇怪的人來往。
* associate [əˋsoʃɪɪt] (n.) 朋友；同事
: someone who is closely connected to another person as a companion, friend, or business partner
- Gus has many associates that respect him for his hardwork in the company.
Gus 有許多同事對他在公司的辛勤工作表示敬意。

Pronunciation tips:

Competitor (the two ts’ are slightly lazy pronounced in American English, whereas for British English, the T is very emphasized)
Phenomenon /fəˈnɑː.mə.nɑːn/ the stress is on the second syllable.
Years of (years-uv)

1.
* phenomenon [fəˋnɑmə͵nɑn] (n.) （尤指不尋常的或有趣的）現象
: something that exists and can be seen, felt, tasted, etc., especially something unusual or interesting
- There are UFO groups which investigate the UFO phenomenon.
有很多 UFO 團在調查一些 UFO 的現象

2.
* competitor [kəmˋpɛtətɚ] (n.) 競爭者；參賽者
: a person, team, or company that is competing against others
- Nike has always been a competitor to Adidas as they are two of the biggest sporting brands in the world.
Nike 一直以來都是 Adidas的競爭對手，因為它們是世界上最大的兩個運動品牌

3.
* choke [tʃok] (v.) 失敗；怯場
: (usually in sports) to fail to do something at a time when it is urgent, usually because you suddenly lose confidence
- Choking under pressure can decrease the standard level of an athlete's performance.
在壓力下怯場會降低運動員表現的標準值。

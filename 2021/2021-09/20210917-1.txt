1.
* shrink [ʃrɪŋk] (v.) 縮小，變小
: to become smaller, or to make something smaller
- Ant-man had the ability to shrink himself so he could escape buildings easily.
蟻人有能力把自己縮小，這樣才可以容易逃出大樓。

2.
* fit [fɪt] (v.) 合身；可容納
: to be the right size or shape for someone or something
- Jack wanted to stay fit so he was on a strict diet for 3 months.
Jack 為了保持身材，這三個月很嚴格的在減肥。

3.
* equivalent [ɪˋkwɪvələnt] (adj.) 等值的；相等的
: having the same amount, value, purpose, qualities, etc.
- They want their product to become the equivalent of the original.
他們希望他們的產品成為原始產品的等價物。

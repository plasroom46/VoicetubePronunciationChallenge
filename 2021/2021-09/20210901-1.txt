Pronunciation tips:

decadent (deh·kuh·dnt)
Vanilla (vuh·ni·luh)
Basically (bay·si·klee)

1.
* decadent [ˋdɛkədnt] (adj.) （人或組織）腐朽的；頹廢的；墮落的
: Part of the pleasurable when used with food
- Champagne and chocolates for breakfast - how decadent!

2.
* combination [͵kɑmbəˋneʃən] (n.) 聯合；混合；結合；綜合
: the mixture you get when two or more things are combined
- A combination of tiredness and boredom caused me to fall asleep.

3.
* basically [ˋbesɪk!ɪ] (adv.) 基本上
: used when referring to the main or most important characteristic or feature of something
- The car's basically OK, but the paintwork needs some attention.

Pronunciation tips:

from a (linking words)
content (n.) /ˈkɑːn.tent/ (adj) /kənˈtent/
sustainable /səˈsteɪ.nə.bəl/ (break it down to syllables for them.) sus is /ses/ not /sues/

1.
* perspective [pɚˋspɛktɪv] (n.) 角度，觀點
: a particular way of considering something
- I know you meant well, but from her perspective, you gave advice that wasn’t asked for.
我知道你是好意，但從她的角度來說，沒有人請你給的建議。

2.
* optimal [ˋɑptəməl] (adj.) 最佳的；優化的
: best; most likely to bring success or advantage
- The white-water rafting tour got postponed because the weather is less than optimal.
急流泛舟的旅行因為天氣不是最佳的狀況被延期了。

3.
* revenue [ˋrɛvə͵nju] (n.) （政府）稅收；（公司）收益
: the income that a government or company receives regularly
- Tourism is the biggest source of revenue for this little town, but it’s limited to the summer months.
這小鎮最大的收益來自觀光，但只限制於暑假。

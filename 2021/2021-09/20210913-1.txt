Pronunciation tips:

Measure (s = not ‘sh’ but ‘zh’ sound)
Europe (not rope but rup)
At the (linking words) /a-the/

1.
* measure [ˋmɛʒɚ] (v.) 測量
: to discover the exact size or amount of something
- Remember to measure the length of the TV before we go shopping for a TV cabinet at the furniture shop tonight.
今晚要去家具店買電視櫃之前，記得量一下電視的長度。

2.
* population [pɑpjəˋleʃən] (n.) 人口
: all the people living in a particular country, area, or place
- The population of Japan is larger than that of New Zealand.
日本的人口比紐西蘭還要多。

3.
* low-end [ˈlō-ˌend] (adj.) 低價的；低檔的
: the cheapest or bottom level of something
- I don’t go for high-end fashion brands as they usually come with an expensive price tag, low-end brands are more affordable and it’s still possible to find comfortable clothes.
我不太會選擇高端時尚因為價格通常都很貴，低階品牌比較買得起而且也是能夠找到舒服的衣服。

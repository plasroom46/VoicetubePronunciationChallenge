The flu is a highly contagious respiratory illness that ranges in severity, from a sickness that bothers you just a little bit to one that can be so severe that in rare cases, it can absolutely kill you.

流感是一種具有高度傳染性的呼吸道疾病，其嚴重程度各異，有時可能只會讓你在短時間內感到有些困擾，在罕見情況下甚至可能會要了你的命。

1.
* contagious [kənˋtedʒəs] (adj.) 接觸傳染性的；（可能）帶接觸傳染原的
- （疾病）接觸性傳染的
: (of a disease) spread from one person or organism to another, typically by direct contact.
-（情感）具有感染力的
: (of an emotion, feeling, or attitude) likely to spread to and affect others.
- Her smile is contagious. Wherever she goes she brightens up everyone’s mood.
她的笑容具有感染力。無論她去到哪裡都會讓大家的心情變好。

2.
* respiratory [rɪˋspaɪrə͵torɪ] (adj.) 呼吸的
: related to breathing
- Smoking can cause respiratory diseases.
抽菸會造成呼吸道疾病。

3.
* severity [səˋvɛrətɪ] (n.) 嚴重程度
: seriousness
- I don’t think he understands the severity of the problem.
我覺得他不了解問題實際的嚴重性。

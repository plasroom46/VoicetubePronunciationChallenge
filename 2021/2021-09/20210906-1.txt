Despite the potential silliness and exaggeration of sections of the fashion industry, assembling a wardrobe is a serious and meaningful exercise.

儘管時尚界潛存著愚蠢和浮誇，但如何將衣著呈現出整體穿搭美感卻是一項嚴肅而饒富意義的工作。

Pronunciation tips:

Potential /poʊˈten.ʃəl/
Exaggeration (break it down into syllables) /ɪɡˌzædʒ.əˈreɪ.ʃən/
Sections of (linking words) /sections-uv/

1.
* despite [dɪˋspaɪt] (prep.) 不管；儘管；無論
= in spite of
: without taking any notice of or being influenced by; not prevented by
- Despite her young age, she still won a gold medal at the Olympics.
儘管她年紀輕輕，她還是贏得了一面奧運金牌。
- In spite of all the cook books I’ve bought, I’m still no good in the kitchen.
儘管我買了那麼多本食譜，我在廚房裡還是很不行。

2.
* exaggeration [ɪg͵zædʒəˋreʃən] (n.) 誇張；誇大；言過其實
: the fact of making something seem larger, more important, better, or worse than it really is
- The way my little brother described his stomachache may be an exaggeration, I think he just wants to skip school.
我小弟形容他的胃痛可能有點誇張，我覺得他只是想要翹課。

3.
* wardrobe [ˋwɔrd͵rob]] (n.) （某人的）全部衣服；衣櫃
: all of the clothes that a person owns
- Marie Kondo has a tidying method of going through every piece of clothing in your entire wardrobe and seeing which items spark joy.
近藤麻理惠有個整理術，就是把衣櫥裡所有衣服都檢視一遍，看那些會讓你碰然心動。

Pronunciation tips:

Blended words - to be at a
Obsession - schwa sound, short e, and another schwa sound
Antidote - short a (blended with n), short i, long o

1.
* mankind [ˋmæn͵kaɪnd] (n.) 人類
: to the whole of the human race, including both men and women.
- That’s one small step for man, one giant leap for mankind.
- We must work for peace. Not just one nation or one region, but peace for all mankind.

2.
* obsession [əbˋsɛʃən] (n.) 著迷，著魔
: something or someone that you think about all the time
- I’m worried about Billy. Playing computer games has grown from a simple hobby to a daily obsession for him.

3.
* antidote [ˋæntɪ͵dot] (n.) 解藥
: a chemical, especially a drug, that limits the effects of a poison
- Activated charcoal can work as an antidote for many types of poison.

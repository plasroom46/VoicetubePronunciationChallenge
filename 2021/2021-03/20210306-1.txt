1.
* increase [ɪnˋkris] (v.) 增大；增加
- Crimes have significantly increased around this area in the past few years.
在過去幾年中，該地區的犯罪活動明顯增加。
* decrease [ˋdikris] (v.) 減少；下降
- Tourists around the world have dramatically decreased due to the pandemic.
世界各地的遊客因為疫情的關係急遽減少。
- The decrease of traveling abroad influenced the increase of traveling in Taiwan.
出國旅遊減少影響台灣旅遊增加

2.
* percentage [pɚˋsɛntɪdʒ] (n.) 百分比，百分率
* percent [pɚˋsɛnt] (adv.) 百分之…
- 前面加上數字，20 percent, 50 percent 等等
- I’m 100 percent sure that he is the soulmate that I’ve been looking for.
我百分之百的確定他就是我在尋找的靈魂伴侶。

3.
* raise [rez] (v.) 舉起；抬起
- If you have any further questions, please raise your hands.
如有其他疑問，請舉手。
* rise [raɪz] (v.) 上升；升高
- The unemployment rate is rising due to COVID-19.
因為COVID-19的關係，失業率正在上升。
* arise [əˋraɪz] (v.) 發生；產生
- Some mechanical problems started to arise after we bought this second-hand car.
我們購買了這輛二手車後，開始出現一些機械性問題。

raise/ rise/ arise 差異
raise 是及物動詞，根本上的意義是「使上升」；rise 是不及物動詞；arise 同樣為不及物動詞但沒有上升的意思。

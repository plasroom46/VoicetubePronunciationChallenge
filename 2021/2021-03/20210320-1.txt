1.
* unfortunately [ʌnˋfɔrtʃənɪtlɪ] (adv.) 不幸地；倒楣地
- Unfortunately, I didn’t make it to the airport on time due to the traffic.
很不幸地，因為塞車我沒有準時趕到機場。
* fortunately [fɔrtʃənɪtlɪ] (adv.) 幸運地，走運地
- Fortunately, I won the lottery which was worth 1 million dollars.
很幸運地，我中了一百萬元的樂透。

2.
* possible [ˋpɑsəb!] (adj.) 可能的
- Will it be possible for a human to fly in the air in the future?
未來人類有可能在天空中飛行嗎？
* impossible [ɪmˋpɑsəb!] (adj.) 不可能的
- Have you ever watched the Mission Impossible series? They are my favorite movies of all time.
你有看過不可能的任務嗎？它們是我有史以來最愛的電影。

3.
* defenseless [dɪˋfɛnslɪs] (adj.) 沒有防衛能力的
- How abhorrent it is to attack a defenseless woman on the street.
在大街上襲擊一個沒有防衛能力的女人真的很可惡。
* defense [dɪˋfɛns] (n.) 防護；防禦
- My boyfriend plays defense games on his phone all the time.
我男友一直玩他手機上的防守遊戲。
* defend [dɪˋfɛnd] (v.) 防衛；為…辯護
- The defendant decided to defend himself on the trial.
被告決定在庭審中為自己辯護。

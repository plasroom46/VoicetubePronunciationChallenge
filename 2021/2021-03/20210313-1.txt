Imagine remembering every single day of your life, all in perfect detail. For those with highly superior autobiographical memory, or H.S.A.M for short, this is their reality.

試想，你能記住你一生中的每一天，包含所有的細節。對於那些具有「高度優越自傳體記憶」或者簡稱為 H.S.A.M 的人而言，這就是他們的真實人生。

[好驚人！這名女子記得人生中的每一天 (This Woman Remembers Every Day of Her Life)](https://tw.voicetube.com/videos/65346?ref=everyday)

Pronunciation

Imagine
superior
autobiographical 
memory
reality

1.
* imagine [ɪˋmædʒɪn] (v.) 想像；設想
- Can you imagine that humans might emigrate to Mars in the future?
你能想像人類在未來移居到火星嗎？
- Emigarte 為移出移民；immigrate 為移入移民，比如移民到美國，對台灣人來說是 emigrant，對美國人來說是 immigrant。

2.
* superior [səˋpɪrɪɚ] (adj.) 優越的
同義詞：better
- The reason that we bought this car is that it’s superior to other same class cars.
我們之所以買這輛車，是因為它比其他同級別車要好。
- be superior + n.
* inferior [ɪnˋfɪrɪɚ] (adj.) 差的；比…不如的
同義詞：worse
- These fruits are inferior to those we bought last season.
這些水果不如我們上季買的那些。
- be inferior to + n.

3.
* for short [] (phr.) 簡稱
- Her name is Jenny, or you can call her Jen for short.
她的名字叫 Jenny, 或是你也可以簡稱她 Jen。
* in short [] (phr.) 總之
- In short, we have to cut down the staff due to the economic slump.
總之，我們因為經濟不景氣的關係必須裁員。
- cut down 減少
- economic slump 經濟不景氣

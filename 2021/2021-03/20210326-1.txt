Pronunciation Tips: (VoiceTube)
Activity [ækˈtɪvəti]
Recommend [ˌrɛkəˈmɛnd]
especially [ɪˈspɛʃəli]
Value [ˈvælju]


1.
* recommend [rɛkəˋmɛnd] (v.) 介紹，建議
: to say that (someone or something) is good and deserves to be chosen
- Which dish do you recommend in this restaurant?
這家餐廳裡你推薦哪一道菜？

2.
* especially [əˋspɛʃəlɪ]] (adv.) 特別，尤其
:  very much; more than usual or more than other people or things
-  Due to the pandemic, we must wear masks when going out, especially when taking public transportation.
由於疫情的關係，我們外出時一定要戴上口罩，尤其是搭公共運輸時。

3.
* specially [spɛʃəlɪ] (adv.) 特別地
: extremely or in particular; for a particular purpose
-  This course is specially designed for those who want to take the TOEIC test.
這門課是專門為想要考多益的人設計的。
- especially 指的是「想要特別強調其中某件事在程度上勝出」；specially 則是當我們想要說明「某事是特別為了某原因而做的時候」使用。

4.
* value [vælju] (n.) 價值, 價格
: the amount of money that can be received for something
- Color, purity, polish, and weight determine the value of diamonds.
顏色，純度，拋光度和重量決定了鑽石的價值。

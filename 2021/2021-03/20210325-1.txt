1.
* vital [ˈvaɪ.t̬əl] (adj.) 必不可少的；極其重要的
Synonyms: important, crucial
: necessary for the success or continued existence of something; extremely important
- Being hydrated is vital for your overall health, so you better make sure you keep tabs on your water intake.
身體保持充足的水份對整體健康是極其重要的，所以你應該要確保自己有密切關注自己的攝取水量。

2.
* potential [poʊˈten.ʃəl] (adj.) 潛在的，可能的
: possible when the necessary conditions exist
- I can’t believe she’s gone. She had so much potential.
我真不敢相信她離開了。她曾那麼有潛力。
Footnotes:
I see the potential in… 我看好...

3.
* exist [ɪɡˈzɪst] (v.) 存在；實際上有
: to be, or to be real
- Dean believes that supernatural beings exist and that they are to be exterminated.
Dean 相信超自然生物存在著，而他們必須被消滅。
Footnotes:
Existence n. ···的存在
Extinct 滅絕

As hero, rebel, or trickster, Prometheus remains a symbol of our capacity to capture the powers of nature. And ultimately, he reminds us of the potential of individual acts to ignite the world.

不論他是英雄、叛逆者或騙子，普羅米修斯都是我們有能力掌握自然力量的象徵。以及最重要地，他提醒我們個人行動足以點燃世界的潛力。

1.
* trickster [ˈtrɪk.stɚ] (n.) 騙子
Synonyms: Fraud, liar, cheat, two-faced, etc.
: a person who deceives people
- Loki is a notable trickster in Norse mythology, and is known to stir up trouble.
Loki 是在維京神話中一個有名的騙子，以惹事生稱著。


2.
* capacity [kəˈpasɪti] (n.) 容積，容量；生產能力；（尤指某人或某組織的）辦事能力
Synonyms: Ability, power, etc.
: the total amount that can be contained or produced, or (especially of a person or organization) the ability to do a particular thing
- I never realized that Caleb had the capacity to do such things.
我從沒發現 Caleb 有能力做處這種事。

3.
* ultimately [ˈʌl.tə.mət.li] (adv.) 最終，最後
: finally, after a series of things have happened
- Although we each have our own take on this situation, the choice is ultimately yours.
雖然我們大家對這件事都有各自的看法，最終的決定還是你的。

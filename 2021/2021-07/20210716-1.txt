Pronunciation tips:

Accent - 兩個音節，前面的 AC 聽起來像 ax， 第二個C是 “s” 的發音因為是 soft C.

Significant - 4個音節，Sig-ni-fi-cant 後面的”a“ 唸 UH” 的音

Influence -3個音節，後面C 是soft C 所以也是念 S 的音 In-flu-ence

1.
* In general [ɪn dʒɛnərəl] (phr.) 一般來説
: usually, or in most situations
- Life in general was going down hill for her after she got caught cheating.
她的人生整體來說，在她被抓到在偷吃之後就走下坡了。

2.
* evolve [ɪˋvɑlv] (v.) 逐步演變
:  to develop gradually, or to cause something or someone to develop gradually
- How did your personality evolve from being introvert and into this very extrovert personality?
你是怎樣從很害羞的個性逐步形成這種很有外向的個性的？

3.
* numerous [njumərəs] (adj.) 許多的，大量的
: many
- They encountered numerous difficulties when conducting the experiment.
他們在做這項實驗時碰到了許多困難。

In 1989, designer Carol Twombly adapted inscriptions from Roman emperor Trajan's column into a digital typeface.

1989 年，設計師 Carol Twombly 根據羅馬皇帝的圖拉真凱旋柱上的銘文改寫出數位字體。

Pronunciation tips:



1.
* adapt [əˈdæpt] (v.) 改編；適應
: to change something to suit different conditions or uses
- David is busy adapting J.K. Rowling's latest novel for television.
大衛正忙著把J.K.羅琳的最新小說改編成電視劇。

2.
* inscription [ɪnˈskrɪpʃən] (n.) 銘文；題詞；刻印
: words that are written or cut in something
- The inscriptions on the gravestones were worn away.
墓碑上的碑文已經消蝕了。

3.
* typeface [ˈtaɪpˌfes] (n.) 字體
: letters and numbers in a particular design, used in printing or on a computer screen
- The commonly seen Times New Roman font belongs to the serif typeface.
一般常見的Times New Roman屬於serif襯線體中的一種字體類別。
＊字體typeface／字型font

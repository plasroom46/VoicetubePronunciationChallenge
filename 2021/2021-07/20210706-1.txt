Pronunciation tips:



1.
* parking [ˈpɑ:rkɪŋ] (n.) 停車處
: leaving a vehicle in a particular place for a period of time
- Jack was fined for illegal parking.
傑克因違規停車被罰款。

2.
* garage [ɡəˈrɑʒ, -ˈrɑdʒ] (n.) 車庫
: a building where a car is kept, built next to or as part of a house
- You left the garage door open last night, didn’t you?
你昨晚把車庫門開著，對吧？

3.
* sudden [ˈsʌdn] (adj.) 突然的
: happening or done quickly and without warning
- Drop the gun, put your hands in the air, and don't make any sudden movements.
把槍放下，手舉起來，別輕舉妄動。

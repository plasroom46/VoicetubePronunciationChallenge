Pronunciation tips:

Process - 兩個音節, 裡面的 C 是 SOFT C 所以發音是 S 的音

Ingredient - 4個音節， 後面的 DIENT 分成兩個部分年 Di-ent

Ancient - 兩個音節，C 是發 CH 的音， 前面AN 是發 “Ain” 的音

1.
* modern [ˋmɑdɚn] (adj.) 現代的，新式的
: designed and made using the most recent ideas and methods
- In this modern world, we have a lot of new technology.
在這個現代的時代，我們有很多新的科技。

2.
* approach [əˋprotʃ] (v.) 接近，靠近
: to come near or nearer to something or someone in space, time, quality, or amount.
- My pet dog likes to approach me whenever I have food in my hands.
我的小狗會在我手上有食物的時候接近我。

3.
* renaissance [rəˋnesns] (n.) 復興
: a new growth of activity or interest in something, especially art, literature, or music
- The creation of the Internet brought about a renaissance in communication.
互聯網的創建帶來了通信的複興。

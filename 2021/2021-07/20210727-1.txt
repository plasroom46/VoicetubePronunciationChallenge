Pronunciation tips:



1.
* once [wʌns] (adv.) 一度；曾經
: One single time
- I went bungee jumping once, and I loved it a lot.
我玩過一次高空彈跳，而我超喜歡的。

2.
* fiction [ˈfɪkʃən] (n.) 虛構（故事）
: A story that is written about imaginary characters and events and not based on real people and facts
- “Sci-Fi” is the abbreviation for Science Fiction.
“Sci-Fi” 就是科幻電影的簡稱。

3.
* lightning [ˈlaɪtnɪŋ] (n.) 閃電
: a flash of bright light in the sky that is produced by electricity moving between clouds or from clouds to the ground
- That old tree was struck by lightning.
那棵老樹被閃電擊中了。

Pronunciation tips:

Awkward - 兩個音節， 前面的 aw 念 ”ah“ 的音
Protein - 兩個音節，“tein” 唸起來像 “teen”
Subscribe - 兩個音節 sub-scribe

1.
* ace [es] (v.) 考得很好
: To do exceptionally well in something, especially an exam or other high-pressure situation.
- Tomorrow I’m going to ace that English test.
明天的英文考試我要考個Ａ （國外九十分以上都是 A)。

2.
* break the bond (phr.) 掙脫…的束縛
: breaking a trust or a shared relationship，betray someones trust
- Nobody can break the bond between Terry and Zoe.
沒有人可以破壞 Terry 跟 Zoe 之間的感情。.

3.
* bae [beɪ] (n.) 寶貝
acronym for “Before Anyone Else”
: which often is used for someone that you love or care about, so you can say it is for someone who you also call “baby, babe”
比較常用在另外一伴，可是女生好姐妹之間也可以用喔。
- Last night my bae and I went to the movies on our date night.
昨天晚上我和我的寶貝在約會上去看了一部電影。

Pronunciation tips:

lot of 
opportunity
get to

1.
* due to (phr.) 由於，因爲
= because of
- Due to the pandemic, going out without a mask on will be fined.
因為疫情的關係，外出沒有戴口罩會被罰款。
* due [dju] (adj.) 預計的，預期的
- due date 到期日
- (something a student can relate to)
What’s the due date of the final report? I really need to turn it in before the deadline or I won’t be able to graduate!
期末報告的截止日期是什麼時候？ 我真的需要在截止日前繳交，要不然我就不能畢業了！
- due date = deadline

2.
* lack of sth (phr.) 沒有…，…短缺
- The main problem in her project is a lack of details.
她計劃案的主要問題是缺少細節。
* shortage [ˋʃɔrtɪdʒ] (n.) 短缺、缺少
- 東西 + shortage 
- Food shortage and water shortage have caused severe health problems for thousands of African people.
食物及水短缺已經造成成千上萬的非洲人嚴重的健康問題。

3.
* check sth off (phr.) 在…上打勾
- After you finish checking all the equipment, check each item off on the list.
檢查完所有設備後，把清單上的每個選項都打勾。

Pronunciation tips:

up-and-down

1.
* forceful [ˋforsfəl] (adj.) 強有力的；有說服力的
- His argument is very forceful. I have nothing else to say.
他的論點很有說服力，我無話可說。

2.
* motion [ˋmoʃən] (n.) 運動；移動 (一連串的 movement = motion)
- Can you play that clip in slow motion again?
你可以用慢動作再播一次那個片段嗎？
* movement [ˋmuvmənt] (n.) 移動；運動 (位置的改變)
- When the bank robber shot at the ceiling, no one dared to make a movement.
當銀行搶劫犯朝天花板開槍時，沒有人敢動。

3.
* internal [ɪnˋtɝn!] (adj.) 體內的；內部的；國內的
- Since you just had a car accident, you should get a thorough checkup to see if any internal organs are injured.
因為你剛出車禍，你應該要去做個全面檢查看看體內有沒有臟器受傷。
* external [ɪkˋstɝnəl] (adj.) 外面的；外表的；來自外部的
- The idiom “don’t judge a book by its cover” means that we shouldn’t judge someone by their external appearances.
「以貌取人」這個俗諺指的是我們不要以外在的樣貌來評斷一個人。

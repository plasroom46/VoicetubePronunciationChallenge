By changing a negative attitude into a positive one, you can help change the tone of your workplace.

透過將消極的態度轉變為積極的態度，你可以對改變工作場所的氛圍有所貢獻。

[主持人解講](https://cdn.voicetube.com/everyday_records/5375/1624440844.mp3)

Pronunciation tips:

Changing - long a, first g is a j sound
Attitude - short a, short i, long u
Blended words - changing a, into a, of your

1.
* negative [ˋnɛgətɪv] (adj.) 負面的，消極的
: Someone who is being negative is not expecting good things, or likely to consider only the bad side of a situation.
- I’ve tried to explain all the good things about working here, but he’s so negative that he just wants to look for something to complain about.

2.
* positive [ˋpɑzətɪv] (adj.) 積極的；懷有希望的
: The opposite of being negative is being positive, which means full of hope and confidence, or giving cause for hope and confidence.
- Even though the deal fell through, she just shrugged it off and said it was a learning experience. I really admire her positive attitude.

3.
* tone [ton] (n.) 氣氛
: The tone of something is its general mood or main qualities.
- I enjoyed the movie, but I felt the seriousness of the story didn’t match the movie’s goofy, comedic tone.

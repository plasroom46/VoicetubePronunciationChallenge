You mainly get it if a rabid animal bites you after it's had rabies long enough for the infection to make its way into its saliva.

感染狂犬病的主因是被長期罹患狂犬病的動物咬，患病時間長到病毒已進入唾液中。

Pronunciation Tips:

After it’s & Make its - blended words
Rabid - short a, short i
Rabies - long a, long e <- Rabid and rabies are two related words, but they are pronounced differently
Saliva - short a - long i, short a

1.
* rabid [ˋræbɪd] (adj.) 患狂犬病的
:  suffering from rabies
- A bite from a rabid animal can cause severe illness or even death.
* rabid [ˋræbɪd] (adj.) 極端的；過激的
: having and expressing extreme and unreasonable feelings
- Popular KPOP bands need to keep making appearances and churning out products in order to satisfy their rabid fanbase.

2.
* infection [ɪnˋfɛkʃən] (n.) 感染
: a disease in a part of your body that is caused by bacteria or a virus.
- If you have a serious cut, it’s important to clean and bandage it to prevent infection.
* infect [ɪnˋfɛkt] (v.) 傳染，感染
: to pass a disease to a person, animal, or plant.
- In Taiwan, people who have a cough or a cold will often wear a surgical mask so they are less likely to infect others.

3.
* saliva [səˋlaɪvə] (n.) 口水，唾液
: Saliva is the liquid produced in your mouth to keep the mouth wet and to help to prepare food to be digested.
- I am really looking forward to tonight’s dinner. I can feel the saliva form in my mouth just thinking about it.

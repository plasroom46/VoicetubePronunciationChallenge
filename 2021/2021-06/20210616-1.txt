The essence of sentimentality is the desire to be liked, even by those you don't like and can no longer be bothered with.

多愁善感的本質就是渴望被喜歡，即使是對你不再喜歡或今後也不會有交集的人也一樣。

Pronunciation tips:

the essence
sentimentality
desire 
liked
bothered

1.
* essence [ˋɛsns] (n.) 本質；實質；要素
: the basic or most important idea or quality of something
- The essence in this dish is the egg yolk. If you get it wrong, the whole dish is ruined
這道菜最重要的元素是蛋黃，如果你做錯了，整道菜就毀了。
* essential [ɪˋsɛnʃəl] (adj.) 必要的，必不可少的
: necessary or needed
- Sunblock is very essential if you’re going to the beach. Remember to bring it.
如果你要去海邊的話防曬乳很重要，要記得帶。

2.
* sentimentality [ˌsɛntɪmɛnˈtælɪti] (n.) 多愁善感；感傷性
: expression of gentle emotions such as love, sympathy, or caring.
- You are aware of other people's needs, but don't let sentimentality pull you into being too generous.
你對於別人的需求很有感知，但不要讓你的多愁善感讓你變得太大方。
* sentimental [͵sɛntəˋmɛnt] (adj.) 過於感傷的
: too strongly influenced by emotional feelings
- Don't get all sentimental all of a sudden. That’s very unlike you.
不要突然這麼感傷，這樣很不像你。

3.
* desire [dɪˋzaɪr] (n.) （尤指強烈地）渴望，希望，想要
: to want something, especially strongly
- He has no desire for luxury. That’s why he lives happily.
他不渴望奢華生活，這就是為什麼他活得這麼開心。

4.
* bother [ˋbɑðɚ] (v.) 努力，盡力；費心
: to make the effort to do something
- Don’t bother telling her not to do something. She’ll do it anyway.
別費心叫她不要做什麼事情，她還是會去做。
* bother [ˋbɑðɚ] (n.) 麻煩，煩惱；問題
: trouble or problems
- I can grab what you need when I go to the market later. It’s no bother at all.
我等等去市場的時候可以順便買你要的東西，完全不會麻煩。

Probably the best way to fall in love is intimacy. The act of sharing a little bit about yourself and having someone respond in kind. Of course, this happens naturally, usually.

或許讓人墜入愛河最好的方法就是親密感了。像是與別人分享你自己然後某人善意回覆你的這種行為，這理所當然地，通常應該是自然地發生的。

[你能讓別人愛上你嗎？ (Can You Make Someone Fall In Love With You?)](https://tw.voicetube.com/videos/31697)

[主持人解講](https://cdn.voicetube.com/everyday_records/5542/1622796801.mp3)

Pronunciation tips:

the act
a little bit
respond in 

1.
* intimacy [ˋɪntəməsɪ] (n.) 親密；密切
- The intimacy between the boss and the secretary has aroused the suspicion of the boss’ wife.
老闆與秘書之間的親密關係已經引起老闆娘的懷疑。
- arouse
- suspect /ˈsʌs.pekt/ (n.) 嫌疑犯

2.
* respond [rɪˋspɑnd] (v.) 回答，作答
- I knocked on the door for 5 minutes, but no one responded.
我敲門敲了5分鐘，但沒有人回應。
* response [rɪˋspɑns] (n.) 回答
- The police interrogated the suspect but he didn’t get any response at all.
警察審問了嫌疑犯，但他根本沒有得到任何回應。
- interrogate
- suspect /səˈspekt/ (v.) 懷疑
- at all 用在否定或疑問句

3.
* naturally [ˋnætʃərəlɪ] (adv.) 自然地
- He didn’t ask if I would like to be his girlfriend. We just naturally became a couple.
他沒有問我是否想跟他交往，我們自然而然地就在一起了。
* nature [ˋnetʃɚ] (n.) 大自然
- Mother Nature (開頭要大寫)
- We should cherish the natural resources that Mother Nature gives us.
我們應該要珍惜大自然給我們的自然資源。

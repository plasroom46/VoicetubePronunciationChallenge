1.
* tribute [ˈtrɪbjut] (n.) （表達敬意的）頌詞
: something that you say, write, or give that shows your respect and admiration for someone, especially on a formal occasion
- The prime minister paid tribute to the firemen who had sacrificed themselves fighting the blaze.
首相對奮力撲滅大火的消防員們表示哀悼。

2.
* serve [sɚv] (v.) 服務；服役
: to work for; to do your duty to
-  He served under Ronald Reagan as Secretary of State.
他在隆納‧雷根手下擔任國務卿。

3.
* conflict [ˈkɑnˌflɪkt] (n.) 衝突；爭端
: an active disagreement between people with opposing opinions or principles
- There was a lot of conflict between John and his father.
約翰和他的父親之間存在著許多分歧與爭論。

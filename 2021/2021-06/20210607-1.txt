People think that only dumb or gullible people fall for a cult, but if it's the right time and place, a charismatic figure can walk into anyone's life.

人們會覺得只有很笨、容易受騙的人才會加入邪教組織，但只要是在對的時間、對的地點，有魅力的領袖就能輕易地走進任何人的生活中。

Pronunciation tips

dumb /dʌm/
figure /ˈfɪɡ.jɚ/
cult /kʌlt/

1.
* gullible [ˈɡʌl.ə.bəl] (adj.) 容易上當的，易受欺騙的，輕信的
: easily deceived or tricked, and too willing to believe everything that other people say
- Ted’s mom taught him that no one should take advantage of gullible people.
Ted 的媽媽教他沒有任何人可以佔容易上當的人的便宜。

2.
* fall for (phr.) 受...欺騙
- Ace is mad because he fell for Dorian’s prank.
Ace 很生氣因為他被 Dorian 的惡作劇騙了。
* fall for (phr.) 對…傾心，迷戀…
= fall in love
: to suddenly have strong romantic feelings about someone
- Do you see how Harry is falling for Sally?
你有察覺到 Harry 是如何愛上 Sally 的嗎？


3.
* charismatic [ˌker.ɪzˈmæt̬.ɪk] (adj.) 充滿個人魅力的
Synonyms - captivating, magnetic, charming, etc.
: used to describe a person who has charisma
- Many say that villains are actually often charismatic individuals.
很多人宣稱反派角色其實大部份都是充滿魅力的人。

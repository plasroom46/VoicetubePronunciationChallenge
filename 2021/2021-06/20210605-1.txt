1.
* generate [ˋdʒɛnə͵ret] (v.) 造成，引起；産生，發生
: to cause something to exist; to produce energy in a particular form
- The government spokesman’s statement generated a series of harsh debates
政府發言人的聲明引起了一連串激烈的辯論。.

2.
* greenhouse [ˋgrin͵haʊs] (n.) 溫室
: a building with a roof and sides made of glass, used for growing plants that need warmth and protection
- greenhouse gas 溫室氣體
- Countries all over the world are now dedicated to the reduction of greenhouse emission.
世界各地的國家正致力於減少溫室氣體的排放。

3.
* emission [ɪˋmɪʃən] (n.) 排放，散發
: the act of sending out gas, heat, light, etc.
- We have successfully reduced the amount of carbon dioxide emissions.
我們已經成功減少了二氧化碳排放量。
* emit [ɪˋmɪt] (v.) 發出，射出
: to send out a beam, noise, smell, or gas
- You should consider changing your car when it starts to emit black exhaust smoke.
當你的車子開始排放黑色廢氣時，你該考慮換車子了。

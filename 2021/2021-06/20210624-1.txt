Pronunciation tips:

Slavery /ˈsleɪ.ɚ.i/
There are 3 syllables in this word, but the last 2 are said more quickly. Stress the first syllable.
Trade of /traid-uv/
Say the ‘f’ in ‘of’ more like a ‘v’. Also, these words are linked, so you can squish them together.

1.
* trafficking [ˋtræfɪkɪŋ] (n.) 非法交易
: illegal trade
- Customs officials recently busted a large drug trafficking ring worth millions of dollars.

2.
* trade [tred] (v.) 貿易
: the activity of buying and selling, or exchanging, goods and/or services between people or countries
- If you’re looking for second-hand stuff, why not try one of those Buy/Sell/Trade groups online?
* fair trade [fɛr tred] (n.) 公平貿易
: a way of buying and selling products that makes certain that the people who produce the goods receive a fair price
- I try to buy fair-trade coffee whenever possible, even though it’s more expensive.

3.
* labor [ˋlebɚ]] (n.) 工人，勞工
: workers, especially people who do practical work with their hands
- It’s just a desk job, but after years of manual labor, I’ll take a soft chair!

1.
* ancient [ˋenʃənt] (adj.) 古代的；古老的；年代久遠的
: of or from a long time ago, having lasted for a very long time
- Why don’t you get a smartphone? Your Nokia flip phone is ancient!
你為什麼不換支智慧型手機呢？你的 Nokia 翻蓋式手機已經是古代的了。

2.
* mixture [ˋmɪkstʃɚ] (n.) 混合物；混合體；混合料
: a substance made from a combination of different substances, or any combination of different things
- His skin care routine involves putting a mixture of honey and egg whites on his face.
他每天的肌膚保養包括將蜂蜜和蛋白混合物擦在臉上。
* mix [mɪks] (v.) （使）混合，拌和
: to (cause different substances to) combine, so that the result cannot easily be separated into its parts
- You can make honey mustard by mixing dijon mustard, honey, and mayonnaise together.
將法式芥末、蜂蜜和美乃滋混合再一起就是蜂蜜芥末。

3.
* material [məˋtɪrɪəl] (n.) 材料，原料
: a physical substance that things can be made from
- I love the pattern of the shirt, but I dislike the material of it.
我喜歡這件衣服的圖案，但我不喜歡它的材質。

4.
* waterproof [ˋwɔtɚ͵pruf] (adj.) 防水的，不透水的
: not allowing water to go through
- These boots are not waterproof. You can’t wear it when it’s raining.
這雙靴子不是防水的，你不能在下雨的時候穿。
*-proof 防…的、耐…的
fireproof (防火的)
bulletproof (防彈的)
soundproof(隔音的)

5.
* utensil [juˋtɛns!] (n.) （尤指廚房或家用的）器具，用具
: a tool with a particular use, especially in a kitchen or house
- In this class, we’ll learn dining etiquette and the purpose of every utensil.
在這堂課中，我們會學餐桌禮儀和每一種餐具的用意。

Pronunciation Tips:
Universe - long u, ‘er’ sound, s sound
Since it’s - words blend together
Gravity - short a, short i, long e
Is acting - words blend together


1.
* depend on [dɪˋpɛnd ɑn] (phr.) 需要；依賴
: to need something, or need the help and support of someone or something, in order to live or continue as before
: to be controlled or determined by. 
- Which restaurant I choose will depend on what kind of food I want to eat.

2.
* universe [ˋjunə͵vɝs] (n.) 宇宙
: everything that exists, especially all physical matter, including all the stars, planets, galaxies, etc.
- The universe is of a size and scope that humans cannot comprehend.
- I think she’s the most beautiful girl in the universe!
- hyperbolic: to exaggerate on purpose

3.
* determine [dɪˋtɝmɪn] (v.) 決定；影響
: to control or influence something directly, or to decide what will happen.
- The race times will be added up, and the winner will be determined by who has the lowest total time.

4.
* determined [dɪˋtɝmɪnd] (adj.) 下定決心的，堅決的
: wanting to do something very much and not allowing anyone or any difficulties to stop you. 
- Despite many setbacks, Edmund Hillary remained determined to climb Mt. Everest.

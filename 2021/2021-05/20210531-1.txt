The original cynics might not have approved of these fresh takes: they believed that their values of rejecting custom and living closely with nature were the only true values.

最原始的犬儒學派可能不會認同這些新的形式：他們相信，他們排拒習俗，靠近大自然生活的價值觀， 才是唯一真正的價值觀。

Pronunciation tips

cynic /ˈsɪn.ɪk/
fresh /freʃ/
values /ˈvæl.juː/

1.
* approve [əˋpruv] (v.) 贊成；同意；稱許
: to have a positive opinion of someone or something
- Your homeroom teacher needs to approve your leave request before you’re allowed to leave the school premise.
你的導師必須核准你申請的假才能離開校園。
- premise /ˈprem.ɪs/ (n.)（作為先決條件的）假定，前提

2.
* take [tek] (n.) 意見，看法，主張
: a thought or belief about something or someone
- Sam has a very unique take on this topic and contributes vastly to our conversation.
Sam 對這主題有個很特別的主張，並對我們的談話內容有很大的貢獻。

3.
* custom [ˋkʌstəm] (n.) 風俗，習俗；傳統
: a way of behaving or a belief that has been established for a long time
- It’s our custom to leave our shoes outside the door before we come indoors.
我們的習俗是在進門前把鞋子留在門外。
- Customs 海關

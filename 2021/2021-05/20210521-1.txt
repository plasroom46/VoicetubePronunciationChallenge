專家估計，約 85% 的原因在於「你」的基因，所以這幾乎是天註定的。

* estimate [ɛstə͵met] (v.) 估計，估算

- estimate sth. at...

2.
* prefer [prɪˋfɝ] (v.) 寧可，更喜歡
: to like one thing or person better than another; to choose one thing rather than something else because you like it better
-  prefer A to B (比起 B 更喜歡 A)
- prefer to do sth./doing sth. (寧可做某事)
- I prefer drinking coffee to drinking tea.
比起茶，我更喜歡喝咖啡。
* preference (n.) 偏愛
- preference for sth/sb. (更喜歡...)
- Being a fair teacher, you should not show your preference for specific students.
作為一位公平公正的老師，你不應該偏愛特定的學生。

3.
* genetic [dʒəˋnɛtɪk] (n.) 基因的，遺傳的
: belonging or relating to genes (= parts of the DNA in cells) received by each animal or plant from its parents
- genetic defects (基因缺陷)
- genetic disease (遺傳疾病)
- According to the research, this disease is caused by genetic defects.
根據研究指出，這個疾病是由基因缺陷導致的。

1.
* urine [ˋjʊrɪn] (n.) 尿液，小便
- If you see a lot of bubbles in your urine, it means there’s something that might be wrong with your body.
如果你的尿液中有很多氣泡，可能代表你的身體出了毛病。
尿尿的動詞可以怎麼講？urinate是比較正式的，但通常不會這樣說，
口語：take a piss, take a leak, go number 1
小孩：pee-pee, go potty

2.
* organic [ɔrˋgænɪk] (adj.) 有機的，不使用化肥的
- These vegetables are all organic, that’s why they’re so expensive.
這些蔬菜都是有機的，所以才會這麼貴。
* artificial [͵ɑrtəˋfɪʃəl] (adj.) 人工的；仿造的
- I didn’t notice that this plant is actually artificial at the first sight. It looks so real.
我第一眼沒看出這職務是人工的，做得太真了。

3.
* nasty [ˋnæstɪ] (adj.) 糟糕的；令人不快的
= bad
- I just had my nastiest date ever with my online friend! He looked nothing like his picture!
我剛經歷了有史以來最糟的約會，跟一個網友！他長得跟他的照片一點都不像！

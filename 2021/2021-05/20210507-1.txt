These cycles take about 90 minutes each, and you feel most refreshed if you wake up at the end of the cycle, because by then, you are closest to your normal waking state.

一個週期大概是 90 分鐘，而如果在週期的最後醒來的話，最能消除疲憊，因為那是最接近自然醒的狀態。

1.
* cycle [ˋsaɪk!] (n.) 週期，循環
- life cycle (生命週期)
- the cycle of sth. (...的週期)
- the cycle of the seasons 季節的循環
- His recent study is mainly about human’s sleep cycle and its different stages.
他最近的研究主要是關於人類的睡眠週期和它的不同階段。

2.
* refreshed [rɪˋfrɛʃt] (adj.) 涼爽的；精神振奮的
- I feel refreshed after taking a nap and drinking a cup of coffee.
在小睡片刻及喝了杯咖啡後，我感到神清氣爽。
* refresh  (v.) (使涼爽；使恢復精力) 
- refresh oneself
- The best way to refresh yourself on hot summer days is to eat some ice cream.
吃冰淇淋是炎炎夏日中最棒的解熱方式。

3.
* waking [ˋwekɪŋ] (adj; n.) 清醒的，醒著的；清醒
(adj.)
- waking hours/life/day (醒著的時刻)
- It seems that Sally spends every waking hour on her work.
Sally 似乎將所有醒著的時間都花在工作上。
(n.)
- between waking and sleeping 半睡半醒
- The baby started to cry when she was between waking and sleeping and her mother tried to comfort her by putting a pacifier into her mouth.
寶寶在半睡半醒之際開始哭，而媽媽將奶嘴放進寶寶的嘴裡，試著安撫她。

Pronunciation tips:

1. Gonna /ˈɡɑː.nə/ (informal for going to)

2. Throughout /θruːˈaʊt/ ("gh" is silent)

1.
* The North Pole [nɔrθ pol] (n.) 地球的北極
: the point on the earth's surface that is furthest north
- Polaris is located just above the North Pole. 北極星位於北極的正上方。

2.
* throughout [θruˋaʊt] (adv.) 在各處；自始至終
: in every part, or during the whole period of time
- The horrible disease spread throughout the country. 可怕的疾病在全國蔓延。

3.
* place [ples] (n.) 地方；場所
: an area, town, building, etc.
- We must find a place to stay tonight. 我們今晚必須找個地方住。

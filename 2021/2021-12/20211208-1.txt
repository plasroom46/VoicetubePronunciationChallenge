Pronunciation tips:

Inanimate
Microscopic
Arbitrary

1.
* inanimate [ɪnˋænəmɪt] (adj.) 無生命的
: having none of the characteristics of life that an animal or plant has
- I sometimes catch myself apologizing to inanimate objects when I accidentally bump into them.
我發現自己不小心撞到沒有生命的物體時，會向它們道歉。

2.
* microscopic [ˋmaɪkrəˋskɑpɪk] (adj.) 極小的；需要用顯微鏡才可看見的
: relating to the universe and the natural processes that happen in it
- NASA plans to launch a satellite to study cosmic rays.
NASA 計劃發射一顆衛星來研究宇宙射線。

3.
* arbitrary [ˋɑrbə͵trɛrɪ] (adj.) 任意的；隨機的；隨心所欲的
: based on chance rather than being planned or based on reason
- The borders assigned to Asia on the west are somewhat arbitrary.
分給亞洲西部的邊界有些隨意。

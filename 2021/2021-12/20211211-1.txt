There's a condition known as Assidy, which was experienced by monks who spent prolonged periods in silence, and it's basically distinguished by a feeling of torpor, of not being able to do anything,

佛教中有種叫做阿西迪的境界，會在僧人長期處於沉默之中後達到。這個境界基本上會讓人的感覺變得遲鈍，什麼事情都做不了，

Pronunciation tips:

Experienced
Distinguished
Torpor

1.
* prolonged [prəˋlɔŋd] (adj.) 延續很久的，長期的
: continuing for a long time
- The sudden rain prolonged the soccer match.
突然其來的雨延長了足球比賽的時間。

2.
* distinguished [dɪˋstɪŋgwɪʃt] (adj.) 傑出的，突出的，卓越的
: used to describe a respected and admired person, or their work
- He looked so distinguished in that outfit he wore.
他穿著那身衣服，顯得格外出眾。

3.
* torpor [ˋtɔrpɚ] (n.) 不活躍；萎靡；遲鈍
: the state of not being active and having no energy or enthusiasm
- After a hearty meal, we felt a pleasant torpor.
吃過一頓豐盛的飯菜後，我們感到愉快的麻木。

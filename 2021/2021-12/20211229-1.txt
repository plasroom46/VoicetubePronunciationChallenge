Pronunciation tips:

bottle (when said quickly, sounds like bo-dal)
water (difference between Australian/British and American pronunciation)
pesticide (first i = uh, second i = ai)
gasoline (o = short uh sound)

1.
* permeable [ˋpɝmɪəb!] (adj.) 可滲透的；可滲入的
: If a substance is permeable, it allows liquids or gases to go through it.
- He used permeable building materials on purpose to capture and retain rainwater.
他刻意用可滲透式的材質來建蓋因為想要保留住雨水。

2.
* store [stor] (v.) 儲存；儲備；儲藏
: to put or keep things in a special place for use in the future
- Use this empty drawer to store your clothes while you stay here this weekend.
這周末你住這的時候，可以用這個空的抽屜來儲放你的衣服。

3.
* pesticide [ˋpɛstɪ͵saɪd] (n.) 殺蟲劑，農藥
: a chemical substance used to kill harmful insects, small animals, wild plants, and other unwanted organisms
- Make sure you keep the pesticides out of reach of young children.
記得將農藥放在小孩子拿不到的地方。

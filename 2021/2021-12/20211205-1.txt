雅典娜決定給這個不知感恩的女孩一點教訓，於是偽裝成一位老婦，下凡遊走在人群之間。

Pronunciation tips:

1. ungrateful /ʌnˈɡreɪt.fəl/

2. disguised /dɪsˈɡaɪzd/ (the pronunciation of "gui")

3. mortals /ˈmɔːr.t̬əl/(the "t" pronounces as /d/)

1.
* disguise [dɪsˋgaɪz] (v.) 給…喬裝打扮；將…偽裝起來
: to give a new appearance to a person or thing, especially in order to hide its true form
- He disguised himself as a homeless man. 他偽裝成一個街友。

2.
* amongst [əˋmʌŋst] (prep.) 在…中；為…所環繞
: in the middle of or surrounded by other things
- My boyfriend was able to find me amongst the crowd at a glance. 我男朋友一眼就能在人群中找到我。

3.
* mortal [ˋmɔrt!] (n.) 凡人、普通人
: an ordinary person, rather than a god or a special, important, powerful person
- We are all mortals, struggling with our problems. 我們都是凡人，都在自己的問題中掙扎。

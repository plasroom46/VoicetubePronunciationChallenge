Pronunciation tips:

1. "clarification" /͵klærəfəˋkeʃən/ (Let's break it down into 5 syllables.)
2. "paraphrasing" /ˋpærə͵frezɪŋ/ (Let's break it down into 4 syllables.)

1.
* paraphrase [ˋpærə͵frez] (v.) （以幽默或更簡短、清晰的方式）意譯，解釋，改述
: to repeat something written or spoken using different words, often in a humorous form or in a simpler and shorter form that makes the original meaning clearer
- Instead of writing your opinion about this book, the assignment is to paraphrase the author’s content.
這個作業並不是要寫出你對這本書的看法，而是解釋作者的內容。

2.
* reflect [rɪˋflɛkt] (v.) 顯示；反映；表達
: to show, express, or be a sign of something
- Your report of the book should reflect your understanding of the material and be more than just a short summary.
你對這本書的報告應該要反映出你對它的理解程度，而不僅僅只是做一個簡短的總結。

3.
* clarification [͵klærəfəˋkeʃən] (n.) 闡明，說明
: an explanation or more details that makes something clear or easier to understand
- If you need any clarification about the assignment, please visit me during my office hours.
如果你需要任何有關作業的近一步說明，請在我的辦公時間來找我。

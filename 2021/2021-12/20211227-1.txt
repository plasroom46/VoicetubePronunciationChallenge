Pronunciation tips:

horizontally
vertically
row

linking words: 

move on
need to be
in a (sounds like inna)

1.
* horizontally [͵hɑrəˋzɑnt!ɪ] (adv.) 橫地、水平地
: in a way that is parallel to the ground or to the bottom or top edge of something
- The show subtitles run horizontally from left to right at the bottom of the TV screen.
節目字幕在電視螢幕最下方由左到右平行的播放。

2.
* vertically [ˋvɝtɪk!ɪ] (adv.) 直地、垂直地
: straight up or at an angle of 90° to a horizontal surface or line
- I like to store my books vertically across on the bookshelf, but it seems to collect a lot of dust.
我喜歡在書架上垂直的擺放我的書，但這個方式好像很容易累積灰塵。

3.
* row [raʊ] (n.) 排、列
:  a line of things, people, animals, etc. arranged next to each other
* row [raʊ] (n.) 爭吵
: to argue, especially loudly
- I saw a row of people standing in the corridor looking into the classroom because there was a student that got into a row with the professor.
我看到走廊上站了一排人往教室裡面看，因為有位學生跟教授在爭吵。

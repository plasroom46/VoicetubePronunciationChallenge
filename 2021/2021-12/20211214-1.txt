Pronunciation tips:



1.
* supporter [səˋportɚ] (n.) 支持者，擁護者
: someone who supports a particular idea, group, or person
- Ann is a long-time supporter of the charity and its mission to fund the best childhood cancer research.
Ann 是該慈善機構及其為最頂尖的兒童癌症研究提供資金的使命的長期贊助者。

2.
* investigate [ɪnˋvɛstə͵get] (v.) （尤指為揭開真相）調查，審查
: to examine a crime, problem, statement, etc. carefully, especially to discover the truth
- Police are still investigating the matter and will follow up on any tips, regardless of the source.
警方仍在調查此案件，並且無論消息來源為何，都將跟進任何一條線索。

3.
* encourage [ɪnˋkɝɪdʒ] (v.) 刺激，激勵；促進；助長(as used in the Sentence of the Day)
: to make someone more likely to do something, or to make something more likely to happen
- 
* encourage [ɪnˋkɝɪdʒ] (v.) （透過言語或行動）鼓勵，支援
: to talk or behave in a way that gives someone confidence to do something
- The food may have been a little underwhelming, but I’d rather say something nice to encourage Amaresh to keep cooking.
這道菜可能有點平淡無奇，但我寧願說些好話來鼓勵 Amaresh 繼續做菜。

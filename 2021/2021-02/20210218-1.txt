We also all have this other brain; this other mindset that thinks about long-term consequences; remembers what our big values are; takes a kind of expansive view on our lives and our choices.

我們也都有另一個大腦 ，另一種思維方式會使我們設想長期的結果、記得自己的重要價值觀，以及用寬闊的目光檢視我們的人生和選擇。

你知道如何使用意志力嗎？(How To Say "No!" to Almost Anything)

Pronunciation tips

value /ˈvæl.juː/
expansive /ɪkˈspæn.sɪv/ vs. expensive /ɪkˈspen.sɪv/

Breaking it down at semicolons.


1.
* mindset [ˈmaɪnd.set] (n.) 思維模式
- Having the right mindset can help ease friction in our life.
有一個適當的思維模式可以幫助減少人生的阻力。
- fixed mindset : 定型心態
- growth mindset : 成長心態
- “Mindset, the new psychology of success” by Carol Dweck

2.
* consequence [ˈkɑːn.sə.kwəns] (n.) （常指不好或不利的）結果，後果
: a result of a particular action or situation, often one that is bad or not convenient
- Jade’s mother warns her of the consequences of staying out past curfew.
Jade 的媽媽警告她宵禁後在外逗留的後果
* consequently /ˈkɑːn.sə.kwənt.li/ adv. 因此；所以

3.
* expansive [ɪkˈspæn.sɪv] (adj.) 廣闊的，遼闊的；浩瀚的
- Looking out the window of the plane, Oscar caught an expansive view: London, awash(充滿的；充斥的；泛濫的) with the golden burn of sunset.
從飛機窗外，Oscar 瞥見了一幕遼闊的景觀：被日落洗金的倫敦。
* expand v. 
: covering a large area

The longer we stay in an unventilated space, especially with multiple people, the higher the concentration of carbon dioxide in the atmosphere.

在不通風的空間待得越久，尤其是人多的地方，二氧化碳在空氣中的濃度就會越高。

上班上課總是讓你昏昏欲睡嗎？不是你的錯！(How Classrooms Slow Your Brain Down)


1.
* unventilated [ʌnˈvɛntəˌleɪtɪd] (adj.) 不通風的
:  a room or a space isn’t provided with fresh air. 
- The terrorists seized 10 hostages and locked them in a dark and unventilated room.
恐怖份子劫持了 10 名人質，並把他們關在一個黑暗且不通風的房間裡。
* ventilate [ˈvɛntəˌleɪt] (v.) 使通風(常會使用被動的形式)
: to allow fresh air to enter and move around a room, building, etc.
- well ventilated 通風良好
- poorly ventilated 通風不良
* vent [vɛnt] (n.) 通風口

2.
* concentration [ˌkɑnsənˈtreɪʃən] (n.) 濃度
: the amount of a substance in a liquid or in another substance
- a concentration of sth. 某種東西的濃度
- the concentration of carbon dioxide 二氧化碳的濃度
- high concentration 高濃度
- low concentration 低濃度
- The concentration of greenhouse gases have increased in recent years. 
溫室氣體的濃度在最近幾年有所增加。

3.
* atmosphere [ˈætməˌsfɪr] (n.) 空氣；氣氛，氛圍
空氣 : the air that you breathe in a place
- stuffy atmosphere (不通風的空氣)
- smoky atmosphere (煙霧瀰漫的空氣)
- I need to go outside and get some fresh air. The atmosphere of the classroom is so stuffy!
我需要去外面呼吸新鮮空氣，教室的空氣太不通風了。
 氣氛，氛圍 : the feeling that an event or place gives you
前面加上形容詞，就會變成「...的氛圍」
- cosy asmosphere 舒適的氛圍
- welcoming atmosphere 熱情友善的氛圍
- Tom went to a restaurant, which had a romantic atmosphere, with his girlfriend.
Tom 跟他女友去一家有浪漫氛圍的餐廳用餐。

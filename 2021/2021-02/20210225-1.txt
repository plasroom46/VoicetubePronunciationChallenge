So in order to grow reliably calmer, we need to get a lot less optimistic about how life might go. Pessimism is the cure for anger.

因此，想要變得較為冷靜，我們必需對於人生抱持少一點的樂觀正面。因為悲觀才是治癒憤怒的王道。

怎樣才能避免一直生氣？ (How not to be Angry all the Time)


1.
* reliably [rɪˋlaɪəblɪ] (adv.) 可靠地；確實地
- According to reliable information, Ariana Grande is releasing a new album! 
根據可靠消息，Ariana Grande 要出新專輯了！
* reliable [rɪˋlaɪəb!] (adj.) 可信賴的；可靠的

2.
* calm [kɑm] (adj.) 冷靜的，鎮靜的
- We should all keep calm when an earthquake happens. 
在地震發生時我們應該要保持鎮靜。
* calm down [] (phr.) （使）平靜
- Calm down! It’s just a game, don’t take it too seriously. 
冷靜一點！這只是個遊戲，不要太認真。

3.
* optimistic [͵ɑptəˋmɪstɪk] (adj.) 樂觀的
- She’s over-optimistic and that makes her quite annoying sometimes. 
因為她過於樂觀，她有時候會讓人感到很煩。
* pessimistic [͵pɛsəˋmɪstɪk] (adj.) 悲觀的
- I’m a very pessimistic person as I tend to think more about the downside of things.
我是一個非常悲觀的人，我總是會想到所有事情不好的那一面。
[悲觀的人也可以用根據半杯水論理的 glass half-empty person 來表示]

The number thirteen was associated with the biblical Last Supper, where Jesus Christ dined with his twelve disciples just before being arrested and crucified.

數字 13 跟聖經「最後的晚餐」有關，也就是耶穌被逮捕並被釘在十字架上之前，與他的十二門徒共進晚餐的故事。

【TED-Ed】迷信從何而來 (Where do superstitions come from? - Stuart Vyse)

Pronunciation tips 

biblical  (bi-bli-cal)
disciples (duh-si-ples)
crucified (croo-si-fied)

1.
* associate [əˈsoʊ.ʃi.eɪt] (v.) 將…（與…）聯繫起來，把…聯繫在一起
:  to connect someone or something in your mind with someone or something else
- Jasmine learned recently that she is a synesthete, meaning her perception of numerals and letters is associated with the experience of colors.
Jasmine 最近發現她是有聯覺者，也就是她對數字及文字的感知伴隨著顏色的聯想。
* association

2.
* biblical [ˈbɪb.lɪ.kəl] (adj.) 《聖經》中的；有關《聖經》的
: in or relating to the Bible
- Some courses here are biblically-inclined as this is a Catholic school. 
這裡有些課程和《聖經》緊緊相扣因為這是個天主教學校。

3.
* disciple [dɪˈsaɪ.pəl] (n.) 信徒，門徒，追隨者
: a person who believes in the ideas and principles of someone famous and tries to live the way that person does or did
- The assassin’s disciple went on to become the most feared individual in the entire Empire. 
這個刺客的門徒後來成為帝國最受敬畏的人。

4.
* crucify [ˈkruː.sə.faɪ] (v.) 把…釘在十字架上
: to kill someone by tying or fastening them with nails to a cross and leaving them there to die
- A few centuries back, thieves and traitors were crucified for their crimes.  
幾個世紀前，盜賊和叛徒以被釘在十字架上為他們罪過的刑責。

A nation's foreign policy is a plan for how it intends to interact with other countries.

一個國家的外交政策是其如何與其他國家互動的計劃。

1.
* intend [ɪnˈtend] (v.) 打算；計劃
: to have a plan or purpose
- The course is intended for intermediate-level students.

2.
* interact [ˌɪn.t̬ɚˈækt] (v.) 交往；相互作用
: to communicate with or react to
- It's interesting at parties to see how people interact socially.

3.
* foreign policy [ˈfɔːr.ən ˈpɑː.lə.si] (n.) 外交政策
: a government's policy on dealing with other countries, for example in matters relating to trade or defense
- Former US President Donald Trump announced the “America First” approach for his main foreign policy in his inaugural address.

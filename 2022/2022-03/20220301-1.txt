Pronunciation tips:



1.
* physical [ˈfɪzɪkəl] (adj.) 物質的；實體的
: relating to things you can see or touch
- All physical objects occupy space.
一切實物都佔據空間。

2.
* ideology [ˌaɪdiˈɑlədʒi, ˌɪdi-] (n.) 意識形態
: a set of beliefs or principles, especially one on which a political system, party, or organization is based
- Socialism and capitalism are a set of opposing (conflicting) political ideologies.
社會主義和資本主義是一組對立的政治意識形態。

3.
* philosophy [fɪˈlɑsəfi] (n.) 哲學；哲理
: The system and a set of beliefs used to understand and interpret the world and the existences.
- Live in the moment. That’s my philosophy of life! 活在當下，這是我的人生哲學。

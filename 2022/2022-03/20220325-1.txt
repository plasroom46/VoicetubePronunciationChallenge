Pronunciation tips:

1. necessary (the pronunciations of two "e"s)
2. concentrate (the pronunciations of "cen" and "a")
3. the ability (the pronunciation of "the")

1.
* relaxation [͵rilæksˋeʃən] (n.) 放鬆；鬆弛
: the feeling of being relaxed
- Playing video games is his favorite relaxation. 打電動是他最喜歡
的放鬆消遣。

2.
* ability [əˋbɪlətɪ] (n.) 能力；才能，才智
: the physical or mental power or skill needed to do something
- It sounds like he's questioning her ability to be a teacher. 聽起來他在質疑她做老師的能力。

3.
* concentrate [ˋkɑnsɛn͵tret] (v.) 專注，專心；全神貫注；集中
: to direct your attention or your efforts towards a particular activity, subject, or problem
- It's really hard to concentrate when someone is playing card games next to you. 有人在你旁邊玩紙牌，實在很難專注。

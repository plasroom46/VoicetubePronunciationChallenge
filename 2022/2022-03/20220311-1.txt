1.
* interestingly [ˋɪntrəstɪŋlɪ] (adv.) 有趣地
: as a matter of interest
- He passed all of the advanced courses but for the introductory one, interestingly, he failed.
他通過了全部的進階課程，有趣的是，入門課程卻被當了。

2.
* original [əˋrɪdʒən!] (adj.) 最初的、原本的、有獨創性的
: not secondary, derivative, or imitative
- I handed in a copy to my teacher and kept the original hand-written report myself.
我繳交了副本給我的老師，原來的手寫報吿我自己留著。

3.
* bizarre [bɪˋzɑr] (adj.) 奇異的、怪誕的
: odd, extravagant, or eccentric in style or mode
- I totally can’t understand the bizarre patterns on her dress.
我完全不能理解她洋裝上奇異的圖騰。

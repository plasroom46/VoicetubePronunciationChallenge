Pronunciation tips:

Experience
Deter
Engaging

1.
* experience [ɪkˋspɪrɪəns] (n.) 經驗；經歷；實踐
: (the process of getting) knowledge or skill from doing, seeing, or feeling things
- I gained valuable experience from my failures.

2.
* deter [dɪˋtɝ] (v.) 阻撓，阻止；威懾；使不敢
: to prevent someone from doing something or to make someone less enthusiastic about doing something by making it difficult for that person to do it or by threatening bad results if they do it
- Dogs can deter an unwelcome visitor from your home.

3.
* engaging [ɪnˋgedʒɪŋ] (v.) 吸引住，使感興趣
: to interest someone in something and keep them thinking about it
- I found it difficult engaging the students in class today.

Pronunciation tips:

Regulating
Cosmetics
Ingredients
Detrimental

1.
* regulate [ˋrɛgjə͵let] (v.) 管理；控管
: to control something, especially by making it work in a particular way
- The government regulates where this kind of drug can be used strictly.
政府嚴格控管這種藥物的使用時機。

2.
* ingredient [ɪnˋgridɪənt] (n.) 成份
: one of the parts in a mixture
- Eggs, flour, and sugar are all ingredients in the birthday cake.
蛋、麵粉和糖都是生日蛋糕的成份。

3.
* detrimental [dɛtrəˋmɛnt!] (adj.) 有害的；不利的
: causing harm or damage
- Staying up late is detrimental to your health.
熬夜對你的身體有害。

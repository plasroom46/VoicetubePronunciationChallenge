Pronunciation tips:



1.
* recession [rɪˈsɛʃən] (n.) 不景氣；衰退
: a period when the economy of a country is not successful and conditions for business are bad
- The country is sliding into the depths of (a) recession.
這個國家一步步滑入經濟衰退的谷底。

2.
* dump [dʌmp] (v.) 拋棄；丟棄
: to put down or drop something in a careless way
- Several old cars had been dumped near the beach.
幾輛舊汽車被丟棄在海灘附近。

3.
* asset [ˈæsˌɛt] (n.) 資產；價值的東西
: something valuable belonging to a person or organization that can be used for the payment of debts
- A company's assets can consist of cash, investments, specialist knowledge, or copyright material.
一間公司的資產可包括現金、投資、專業技術或版權物。

Pronunciation tips:

1. How to say years
For years like 1927, we say the first two and the second two digits as if they were single numbers: nineteen twenty-seven.
2. survey (the pronunciation of “vey”)
3. major (the pronunciation of “o”)

1.
* survey [sɚˋve] (n.) 調查
: an examination of opinions made by asking people questions
- The survey is only concerned with women of childbearing age.
此調查著重於已達生育年齡的婦女。

2.
* major [ˋmedʒɚ] (adj.) 較重要的；主要的；重大的
: more important, bigger, or more serious than others of the same type
- Sugar is a major cause of tooth decay.
糖是造成蛀牙的主要原因。

3.
* department store [dɪˋpɑrtmənt stor] (n.) 百貨公司
: a large shop divided into several different parts, each of which sells different things
- Let's go to the department store this weekend!
這個週末我們去百貨公司吧！

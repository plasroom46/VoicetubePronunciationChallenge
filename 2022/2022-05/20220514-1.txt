1.
* convey [kənˋve] (v.) 表達，傳達（思想、感情或想法）
: to express a thought, feeling, or idea so that it is understood by other people
- I hope that this song can convey my feelings for you.

2.
* honesty [ˋɑnɪstɪ] (n.) 誠實
: the quality of being honest
- Can you stop lying and be honest for once?

3.
* sincerity [sɪnˋsɛrətɪ] (n.) 誠實，真誠
: Honesty (This is the Cambridge definition but let me expand on this definition. Sincerity can be something like an apology you give someone but you really mean it and you’re not just saying it. We feel that you know that you were wrong. That’s sincerity.)
- I decided to help him because he impressed me with his sincerity.

Pronunciation tips:



1.
* threat [θrɛt] (n.) 威脅；脅迫
: a suggestion or feeling that something unpleasant or violent will happen
- Drunken drivers pose a serious threat to other road users.
酒後駕車者對其他路人構成了嚴重威脅。

2.
* lack [læk] (n.) 缺乏
: the fact that something is not available or that there is not enough of it
- Julia’s only problem is a lack of confidence.
茱莉亞唯一的問題是缺乏自信。

3.
* industry [ˈɪndəstri] (n.) 行業；工業
: the people and activities involved in one type of business
- The tourism industry has suffered a great loss due to the COVID-19 pandemic.
由於新冠疫情爆發，旅遊業遭受巨大損失。

1.
* extreme [ɪkˋstrim] (adj.) 極大的；極度的；極端的

* mutation [mjuˋteʃən] (n.) （基因的）突變，變異
: the way in which genes change and produce permanent differences
- Radiation can cause a mutation in your DNA.

3.
* gene [dʒin] (n.) 基因
: a part of the DNA in a cell that controls the physical development, behaviour, etc. of an individual plant or animal and is passed on from its parents
- There is a specific gene in your DNA that decides what colour eyes you have.

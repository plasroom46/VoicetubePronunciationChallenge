Pronunciation tips:



1.
* conservationist [ˌkɑ:nsərˈveɪʃənɪst] (n.) 環保人士；環保主義者
: someone who works to protect the environment from the damaging effects of human activity
- Conservationists are concerned about the threat of global warming to the Arctic's unique ecosystems.
環保人士擔心全球變暖對北極獨特生態系統的威脅。

2.
* renewable [rɪˈnu:] (adj.) 可再生的
: an energy form or a material that can be renewed or used again
- Renewable energy sources such as wind and solar power are considered eco-friendly.
風能和太陽能這類的可再生能源被視為是環保的。

3.
* disturb [dɪˈstɜ:rb] (v.) 打擾；妨礙
: to interrupt what someone is doing
- I'm sorry to disturb you so late, but my car's broken down and I don't have my phone with me.
很抱歉這麽晚來打擾你，我的汽車壞了，而且我沒有帶電話。

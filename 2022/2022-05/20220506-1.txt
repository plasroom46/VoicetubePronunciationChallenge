Pronunciation tips:

1. looked at (linking sound)
2. disadvantages (the pronunciations of “a”s)
3. extroversion (the pronunciations of “o” and “sion”)

1.
* introversion [͵ɪntrəˋvɝʃən] (n.) 內向
: the quality of being shy and quiet, and preferring to spend time alone rather than often being with other people
- Introversion can also be considered a positive quality. 內向也可以是種正面的特質。

2.
* extroversion [͵ɛkstroˋvɝʃən] (n.) 外向
: the quality of being energetic and not shy, and enjoying being with other people
- Extroversion is a personality trait typically characterized by outgoingness and talkativeness. 外向性格的特徵通常是喜愛交際及健談。

3.
* advantage [ədˋvæntɪdʒ] (n.) 優點
: a good feature that makes something better than similar things
- The advantage of booking tickets in advance is that you get better seats. 提前訂票的優點是可以訂到比較好的座位。

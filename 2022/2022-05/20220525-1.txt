“In a coma, you don't dream, you just hope that someone sits with you” - Conor Oberst

1.
* coma [ˋkomə] (n.) 昏迷（狀態）
: a state in which a person is unconscious and cannot be woken, caused by damage to the brain after an accident or illness
- My friend was in a coma after his motorcycle accident.

2.
* dysfunction [dɪsˋfʌŋkʃən] (n.) 功能失調；機能不良
: a problem or fault in a part of the body or a machine
- The doctor said that the patient may be suffering from liver dysfunction.

3.
* cerebral [ˋsɛrəbrəl] (adj.) 腦的，大腦的
: relating to the brain or the cerebrum
- His poetry is very cerebral.

Pronunciation tips:

interrupt
restart
cycle

1.
* interrupt [͵ɪntəˋrʌpt] (v.) 打斷
: to stop a person from speaking for a short period by something you say or do
- I didn’t mean to interrupt you, but your baby was crying loudly.
我沒有要打斷你意思，但你的寶寶一直在大聲哭鬧。

2.
* restart [rɪˋstɑrt] (v.) 重新啟動
: to start something again
- I’ve been restarting my computer for the past 15 minutes, but it’s just not working.
我過去十五分鐘都在重新啟動我的電腦，但它就是無法運作。

3.
* cycle [ˋsaɪk!] (n.) 週期
: a group of events that happen in a particular order, one following the other, and are often repeated
- Life is a natural cycle, just like the changing seasons.
人生就像季節遞嬗，有著自然的週期。

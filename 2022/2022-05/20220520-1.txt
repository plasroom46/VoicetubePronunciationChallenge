Pronunciation tips:

1. realize (the pronunciations of "ea" and "i")
2. evaporated (the pronunciations of "e" and "po")
3. in front of (linking sound)

1.
* realize [ˋrɪə͵laɪz] (v.) 明白；認識到；意識到
: to understand a situation
-  They didn't realize that they were in danger.
他們沒有意識到自己正身處危險之中。

2.
* fight [faɪt] (v.) 努力爭取；與…鬥爭
: to use a lot of effort to defeat or achieve something, or to stop something happening
- He has been fighting the disease bravely for three years.
他已經勇敢地與病魔抗爭三年了。

3.
* evaporate [ɪˋvæpə͵ret] (v.) 消失；消散
: to disappear
- Body temperature drops when perspiration evaporates on the body surface.
汗水在體表蒸發時，體溫就會下降。

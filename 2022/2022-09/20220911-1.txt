Pronunciation tips:

1. explaining (the pronunciation of "p")
2. abstract (the æ sounds)
3. metaphoric (the pronunciations of "ta" and "ph")
4. gestures (the dʒ sound)
5. visibly (how to pronounce the "s")

1.
* abstract [ˋæbstrækt] (adj.) 抽象的
: existing as an idea, feeling, or quality, not as a material object
- He is good at thoroughly explaining complex, abstract ideas.
他擅長將複雜、抽象的理念解釋得很清楚。

2.
* concept [ˋkɑnsɛpt] (n.) 概念
: a principle or idea
- It is very difficult to define the concept of beauty.
很難給美這個概念下一個定義。

3.
* gesture [ˋdʒɛstʃɚ] (n.) 手勢
: a movement of the hands, arms, or head, etc. to express an idea or feeling
- The taxi driver made a rude gesture at her.
計程車司機對她比了一個不雅手勢。

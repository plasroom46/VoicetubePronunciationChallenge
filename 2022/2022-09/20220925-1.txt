Pronunciation tips:

1. enough (the pronunciations of "e" and "gh")
2. out of (linking sound)
3. motivation (how to pronounce the "ti")
4. concentration (the pronunciation of "con")

1.
* depression [dɪˋprɛʃən] (n.) 憂鬱症
: a mental illness in which a person is very unhappy and anxious (= worried and nervous) for long periods and cannot have a normal life during these periods
- Tiredness, loss of appetite, and sleeping problems are all classic symptoms of depression.
乏力、沒有食慾和失眠都是憂鬱症的典型症狀。

2.
* motivation [͵motəˋveʃən] (n.) 動力
: the enthusiasm for doing something
- He has the ability to achieve the goal. He just lacks motivation.
他其實有能力實現目標，他只是缺乏動力。

3.
* concentration [͵kɑnsɛnˋtreʃən] (n.) 專注
: the ability to think carefully about something you are doing and nothing else
- It requires tremendous skill and concentration to perform a heart surgery.
進行心臟手術需要極高的技術及專注力。

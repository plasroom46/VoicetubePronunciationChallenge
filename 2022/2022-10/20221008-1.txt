Pronunciation tips:

aligned
psychological
preferences

1.
* align [əˋlaɪn] (adj.) 支持的，贊同的
: supporting or agreeing with another person, organization, or view
- When hiring new employees, we have to make sure that their values align with our company’s.

2.
* psychological [͵saɪkəˋlɑdʒɪk!] (adj.) 心理的；心理學的
: relating to the human mind and feelings
- Childhood abuse and trauma can lead to negative psychological problems later on in life.

3.
* preference [ˋprɛfərəns] (n.) 偏愛；愛好；喜愛
: the fact that you like something or someone more than another thing or person
- I have a preference for rich, flavorful food rather than sweets.

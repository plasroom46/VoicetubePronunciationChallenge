Pronunciation tips:

1. wipe down: (1) A linking between “wipe” and “down”
2. sanitized (1) Verb root: sanitize (2) Three parts: sa-ni-tize /ˈsæn.ə.taɪz/
3. tissue:(1) Two parts: ti-ssue /ˈtɪʃ.uː/ (2) The pronunciation of /ʃ/ (3) Long vowel /u/

1.
* vent [vɛnt] (n.) 通風口
: small opening that allows air, smoke, or gas to enter or leave a closed space
- Air passes through a small vent in the ceiling to the room.
空氣通過天花板上的一個小通風口進入房間。

2.
* sanitize [ˋsænə͵taɪz] (v.) 消毒
: to make something completely clean and free from bacteria
- The five-star restaurant sanitizes utensils regularly.
這家五星級餐廳定期對餐具進行消毒處

3.
* handle [ˋhænd!] (n.) 把手
: a part of an object designed for holding, moving, or carrying the object easily
- I turned the door handle and found the door was unlocked.
我轉動門把手，發現門沒鎖。

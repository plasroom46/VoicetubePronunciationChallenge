Pronunciation tips:

but I 

1.
* barely [ˋbɛrlɪ] (adv.) 僅；剛好；幾乎沒有
: by the smallest amount
- I’m so out of shape, I barely just made it to the top of Mount Jade.
我最近身體狀況不佳，我差一點就爬不上玉山。

2.
* endurance [ɪnˋdjʊrəns] (n.) 忍耐力，耐受力
: ability to keep doing something difficult, unpleasant, or painful for a long time
- I was very close to the limit of my endurance when he was bad-mouthing our mutual friend.
當他說我們共同的朋友的壞話時，我的忍耐已經接近極限了。

3.
* track [træk] (n.) 徑賽，田徑運動
: the general name for a particular group of sports in which people compete, including running, jumping, and throwing
- Tomorrow is our school’s annual track and field event and I’ll be running the last 100m in the girls’ relay.
明天是我們學校一年一度的田徑比賽，我將參加女子接力賽的最後 100公尺。

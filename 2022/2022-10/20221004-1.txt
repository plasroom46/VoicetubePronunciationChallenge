Pronunciation tips:

1. using (the pronunciation of s)
2. particle (how to pronounce ti)
3. accelerators (the pronunciations of two "e"s)
4. mimic (the pronunciations of two "i"s)
5. nuclear (how to pronounce the second syllable in this word)

1.
* particle [ˋpɑrtɪk!] (n.) 粒子
: an extremely small piece of matter
- Electrons are atomic particles.
電子是原子粒子。

2.
* accelerator [ækˋsɛlə͵retɚ] (n.) 加速器
: in physics, a machine that makes particles (= small pieces of matter) move very fast
- Accelerators are used to destroy cancer cells and optimize vaccines and new drugs.
（粒子）加速器被用於破壞癌細胞和優化疫苗及新藥。

3.
* mimic [ˋmɪmɪk] (v.) 模仿
: to copy the way in which a particular person usually speaks and moves, usually in order to make people laugh
- Children often mimic their parents when they're learning how to speak.
學習如何說話時，孩童時常會模仿自己的父母。

5. surges (ges pronounce as juhz)

1.
* oxygen [ˋɑksədʒən] (n.) 氧氣
: a chemical element that is a gas with no smell or color. Oxygen forms a large part of the air on earth, and is needed by animals and plants to live
- Air is composed mainly of nitrogen and oxygen.
空氣主要由氮氣和氧氣組成。

2.
* deplete [dɪˋplit] (v.) 消耗
: to reduce something in size or amount, especially supplies of energy, money, etc
- Being chronically angry, frustrated, or apprehensive can deplete your physical resources.
長期處於生氣、沮喪或憂慮的情緒中會耗盡你的精力。

3.
* surge [sɝdʒ] (v.) 激增
: to increase suddenly and strongly
- There has been a surge in house prices recently.
最近房價飛漲。

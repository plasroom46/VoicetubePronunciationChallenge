1.
* session [ˋsɛʃən] (n.) （從事某項活動的）一段時間（或集會）；一場；一節
: a period of time or meeting arranged for a particular activity
- I felt like I learned a lot from our last therapy session.

2.
* alcohol [ˋælkə͵hɔl] (n.) 酒；酒精，乙醇
: a clear liquid that can make you drunk, also used as a solvent (= a substance that dissolves another) and in fuel and medicines
- Drinking too much alcohol is dangerous for driving.

3.
* socializing [ˈsəʊʃəlaɪzɪŋ] (n.) 社交，交際，社交活動
: the activity of spending time when you are not working with friends or with other people in order to enjoy yourself
- I always hated events where I had to be socializing all the time.

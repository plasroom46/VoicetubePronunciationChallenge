我不認為自己是一個多語高手。 我不使用這個詞是因為我覺得現在有很多人使用它只是為了提升自己的面子。

Pronunciation tips:

1. consider: (1) Three parts: con-si-der /kənˈsɪd.ɚ/
2. polyglot: (1) Three parts: po-ly-glot /ˈpɑː.li.ɡlɑːt/
3. there’s a lot of : (1) Linking
4. ego: (1) /ˈiː.ɡoʊ/ (2) Notice the pronunciation of “e.”

1.
* polyglot [ˋpɑlɪ͵glɑt] (n.) 多語高手
: a person who knows and is able to use several languages.
- She is a polyglot. She can speak fluently in six languages and read in ten.
她是位多語高手。她能流利地用六種語言說話，並能閱讀十種語言。

2.
* boost [bust] (v.) 提高
: to improve or increase something
- To boost the sale, the company launched a buy one get one free activity.
為了促進銷售，該公司推出了買一送一的活動。

3.
* ego [ˋigo] (n.) 自尊
: your idea or opinion of yourself, especially your feeling of your own importance and ability
- To protect the ego of her daughter, the father didn’t tell her the truth.
為了保護女兒的自尊，父親沒有告訴她真相。

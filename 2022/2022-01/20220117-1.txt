1.
* guarantee [͵gærənˋti] (v.) 保證
: to promise that something will happen or exist
* guarantee [͵gærənˋti] (n.) 保證書
: a promise that something will be done or will happen, especially a written promise by a company to repair or change a product that develops a fault within a particular period of time
- This new refrigerator comes with a 10-year guarantee.
這台新冰箱有十年的保固。

2.
* cathartic [kəˋθɑrtɪk] (n.) 情感宣洩
: the process of releasing strong emotions through a particular activity or experience, such as writing or theatre, in a way that helps you to understand those emotions
- Keeping a diary has been a cathartic experience for Eliza.
Eliza 覺得寫日記是一個可以讓情感宣洩的方式。

3.
* perspective [pɚˋspɛktɪv] (v.) 客觀地審查
* perspective [pɚˋspɛktɪv] (n.) （思考問題的）角度，觀點，想法
: a particular way of considering something
- In your perspective, is there anything I can do to improve my English pronunciation?
以你的看法 ，我還能夠如何讓我的英文發音更進步呢?
* perspective [pɚˋspɛktɪv] (adj.) 有透視效果的
: an object or person that is in perspective has the correct size and position in comparison with other things in the picture.

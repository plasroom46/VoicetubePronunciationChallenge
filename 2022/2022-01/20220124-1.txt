1.
* satellite [ˋsæt!͵aɪt] (n.) 人造衛星
: a device sent up into space to travel around the earth, used for collecting information or communicating by radio, television, etc.
- Satellites move very fast, at roughly 18,000 miles an hour, which allows them to orbit the earth 14 times a day.
衛星的移動速度非常快，大約每小時 18,000 英里，這使它們能夠每天繞地球運行 14 次。

2.
* constantly [ˋkɑnstəntlɪ] (adv.) 持續地，不斷地
: all the time or often
- His father constantly nags him about his school grades.
他的父親不斷的嘮叨關於學校的成績。

3.
* atmosphere [ˋætməs͵fɪr] (n.) 大氣層
n. 大氣層: the mixture of gases around the earth
n. 氣氛: the character, feeling, or mood of a place or situation
- The atmosphere is amazing at this club.

Pronunciation tips:

1. digestion (the "i" pronounced as aɪ, the pronunciation of "tion")
2. maximize (the pronunciation of two "i" s)
3. oxygen (the pronunciation of "y")

1.
* digestion [dəˋdʒɛstʃən] (n.) 消化；消化能力
: the process by which your body digests food, or your ability to digest food
- Eating too much fried foods is bad for your digestion. 吃太多油炸食品對你的消化有害。

2.
* nutrient [ˋnjutrɪənt] (n.) 養分；營養物；滋養物
: any substance that plants or animals need in order to live and grow
- Fruits and vegetables provide us the nutrients we need. 水果和蔬菜提供我們所需的營養成份。

3.
* vital [ˋvaɪt!] (adj.) 必不可少的；極其重要的
: necessary for the success or continued existence of something; extremely important
- He played a vital role in last year's project. 他在去年的計畫中扮演著不可或缺的重要角色。

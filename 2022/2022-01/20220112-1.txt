Pronunciation tips:

plateau

1.
* plateau [plæˋto] (n.) 平穩期；停滯期
: a period during which there are no large changes
- I have reached a plateau with my workout progress.

2.
* progress [ˈprɒɡres] (n.) 進步，進展
: movement to an improved or more developed state, or to a forward position
- You have made such good progress from the first day you started learning English.

3.
* regress [rɪˋgrɛs] (v.) 退步；退化；倒退
: to return to a previous and less advanced or worse state, condition, or way of behaving
- If a parent pushes a child towards any type of learning, the child may rebel and may even actually regress.

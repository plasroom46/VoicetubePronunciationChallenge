Pronunciation tips:



1.
* fraught [frɔt] (adj.) 充滿（問題、危險等令人不快之物）的
: full of unpleasant things such as problems or dangers
- Commonly used as “fraught with”
- Without a clear leader and a specific timeline to action, the project was fraught with troubles and setbacks.
由於沒有明確的領導者和具體的行動時間，該項目充滿了問題和挫折。

2.
* strategic [strəˋtidʒɪk] (adj.) 有助於計劃成功的；戰略（性）的
: helping to achieve a plan, for example in business or politics
- For most people, poker is a good way to lose money, but Jett is such a strategic player that he’s been able to win thousands of dollars.
對於大多數人來說，玩撲克很容易賠錢，但 Jett 是一位非常有策略的玩家，以至於他能夠贏得數千美元。

3.
* autonomy [ɔˋtɑnəmɪ] (n.) 自治，自治權
: the right of an organization, country, or region to be independent and govern itself
* autonomy [ɔˋtɑnəmɪ] (n.) 自主，自主權
: the ability to make your own decisions without being controlled by anyone else
- I appreciate everyone’s help while my leg is broken, but I still feel frustrated by the lack of autonomy.
我很感激大家在我腿斷掉的時候幫助，但我仍然對於沒有辦法自理感到沮喪。

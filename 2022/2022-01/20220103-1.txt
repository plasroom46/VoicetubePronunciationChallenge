Pronunciation tips:

texture
flavor
visually
probably

1.
* texture [ˋtɛkstʃɚ] (n.) 口感
: the quality of something that can be decided by touch; the degree to which something is rough or smooth, or soft or hard
- I just love the texture of your long silky hair.
我好喜歡妳長而柔滑的頭髮摸起來的感覺。

2.
* flavor [ˋflevɚ] (n.) 口味
: how food or drink tastes, or a particular taste itself
* flavor [ˋflevɚ] (v.) 給...調味
: to give a particular taste to food or drink
- The flavor of this bread reminds me of home!
這麵包的口味讓我想起我的家鄉。

3.
* visually [ˋvɪʒuəlɪ] (adv.) 視覺上地
: relating to seeing or appearance
- Although this children’s book doesn’t come with any words, it is visually exciting!
雖然這本童書沒有文字，但視覺上十分有趣。

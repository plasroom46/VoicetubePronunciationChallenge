Negative

1.
* negative [ˋnɛgətɪv] (adj.) 壞的；有害的
: bad or harmful

2.
* complaint [kəmˋplent] (n.) 抱怨，牢騷；投訴；不滿
: a statement that something is wrong or not satisfactory
- The waiter was very rude to me so I made a complaint.

3.
* quarantine [ˋkwɔrən͵tin] (n.) 隔離檢疫期
: a period of time during which an animal or person that might have a disease is kept away from other people or animals so that the disease cannot spread
- Upon arrival, I had to do a 14 day quarantine in Taiwan.

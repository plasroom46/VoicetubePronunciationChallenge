Organic also means that nothing was exposed to radiation, which some manufacturers do to sterilize food.

有機也代表裡頭沒有東西是曾經暴露在輻射中，有些製造商會為了殺菌而這麼做。

Pronunciation tips:

1. exposed (the first "e" pronounced as ɪ and the "s" pronounced as z)
2. radiation (the pronunciation of two "a"s)
3. sterilize (the pronunciation of two "i")

1.
* radiation [͵redɪˋeʃən] (n.) （核）輻射
: a form of energy that comes from a nuclear reaction and that can be very dangerous to health
- Exposure to very high levels of radiation can cause skin burns. 暴露在高強度的輻射之下可能導致皮膚灼傷。

2.
* manufacturer [͵mænjəˋfæktʃərɚ] (n.) 製造商，生產商（生產大批商品的企業）
: a company that produces goods in large numbers
- The manufacturer offers a lifetime guarantee on all of its products. 那家製造商所生產的所有產品都有終身保固。

3.
* sterilize [ˋstɛrə͵laɪz] (v.) 為…消毒，使無菌
: to make something completely clean and free from bacteria
- Make sure to sterilize your hands before eating. 用餐前請一定要消毒雙手。

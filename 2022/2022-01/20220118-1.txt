Pronunciation tips:



1.
* expert [ˈɛkˌspɚt] (n.) 專家；行家
: a person with a high level of knowledge or skill relating to a particular subject or activity
- John’s mother is an expert at dress-making.
約翰的媽媽是做裁縫的高手。

2.
* severe [səˈvɪr] (adj.) 嚴重的：苛刻的
: Something that is very serious or causing great pain
- The official name for the SARS disease is Severe Acute Respiratory Syndrome.
SARS疾病的正式名稱為嚴重急性呼吸道症候群。

3.
* mild [maɪld] (adj.) 溫和的 ； 柔和的
: Not too severe or serious
- She can't accept even mild criticism of her work.
即使別人對她的作品略有微詞，她也不能接受。

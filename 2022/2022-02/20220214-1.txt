Pronunciation tips:

ultimate / ultimately
historical / historically
elevate / elevating
closing
stagnation

1.
* ultimately [ˋʌltəmɪtlɪ] (adv.) 最終地，終究
adv. 最終地: finally, after a series of things have happened
adv. 終究: used to emphasize the most important fact in a situation
- There are many good universities but ultimately it comes down to your test results.
雖然有很多好的大學，但終究還是要看你的考試成績。

2.
* elevate [ˋɛlə͵vet] (v.) 提升，舉起
v. 提升: to make someone or something more important or to improve something
v. 舉起: to raise something or lift something up
- Seeing her crush enter the classroom made her heartbeat elevate immediately.
看到她的暗戀對象進了教室，她的心跳馬上加速了。

3.
* stagnation [stægˋneʃən] (n.) 停滯
: a situation in which something stays the same and does not grow and develop
- The pandemic brought about a long period of economic stagnation.
因為疫情的關係，有一段好長的時間經濟停滯不前。

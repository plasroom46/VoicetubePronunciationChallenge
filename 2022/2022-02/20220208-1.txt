Pronunciation tips:



1.
* invention [ɪnˋvɛnʃən] (n.) 發明，創造
: something that has never been made before, or the process of creating something that has never been made before
- The invention of the railroad was a milestone in the history of transportation.
鐵路的發明是交通史上的一個里程碑。

2.
* improvement [ɪmˋpruvmənt] (n.) 改進，改善
: an occasion when something gets better or when you make it better
- We’ve gone over budget this month but that’s understandable considering the major home improvements we’ve made.
這個月我們已經超出了預算，但考慮到我們做了重大的居家改善，這是可以理解的。

3.
* eventually [ɪˋvɛntʃʊəlɪ] (adv.) 最終，終於
: in the end, especially after a long time or a lot of effort, problems, etc.
- We may eventually reach the point where computers are capable of doing everything we need them to.
我們最終可能會達到電腦能夠完成我們需要它們做的所有事情的程度。

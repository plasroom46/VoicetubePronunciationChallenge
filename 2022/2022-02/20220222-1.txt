Pronunciation tips:



1.
* vast [væst] (adj.) 巨大的；廣大的
: extremely big
- After taking our personalized financial advice, our clients have saved vast sums of money.
在接受了我們的客製化的財務建議後，我們的客戶節省了大量資金。

2.
* goods [gʊdz] (n.) 商品，貨物；私人財產
: things for sale, or the things that you own
- There’s a 50 percent discount on all electronic goods at the store right now.
現在商店裡所有的電子產品都有五折的折扣。

3.
* groceries [ˋgrosərɪz] (n.) 食品雜貨
: the food that you buy in a grocer's shop or supermarket
- Could you give me a hand unloading all these groceries, please?
請你幫我卸下所有這些雜貨好嗎？

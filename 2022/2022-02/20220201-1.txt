Pronunciation tips:



1.
* wearable [ˈwɛrəbəl] (adj.) 可穿用的；可佩戴的
: Something that you can wear and easy to wear because they’re comfortable and acceptable in most social situations.
- Unlike a lot of women's fashion magazines, it features clothes that are both affordable and wearable.
與許多女性時尚雜誌不一樣，它介紹的衣服既不太貴又適宜穿。

2.
* tremendous [trɪˈmɛndəs] (adj.) 極大的；巨大的
: Very great in amount or extremely good. (And also one of Donald Trump’s signature words)
- She's been a tremendous (= very great) help to me over the last few months.
過去幾個月裡，她給我的幫助極大。
(congratulate someone)
- You won the game? That's tremendous!
你贏了比賽？太棒了！

3.
* relatively [ˈrɛlətɪvlɪ] (adv.) 相對地
: An adverb used in comparison with other similar things or with what you expect.
- John is a relatively good baseball player. 約翰是個相對不錯的棒球選手。
- Relatively speaking, her lasagna was disgusting. 相對而言，她做的千層麵很噁心。

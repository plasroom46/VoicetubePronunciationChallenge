Pronunciation tips:

1. talked about (linking sound)
2. establishing (how to pronounce the e and bli)
3. oversight (how to pronounce sight)
4. moderation (how to pronounce mo and der)
5. decisions (the pronunciations of de and s)

1.
* establish [əˋstæblɪʃ] (v.) 設立
: to start a company or organization that will continue for a long time
- The United States was established in 1776.
美國於 1776 年建國。

2.
* oversight [ˋovɚ͵saɪt] (n.) 監督
: responsibility for a job or activity and for making sure it is being done correctly
- We have the right to make choices about our bodies, free from legislative oversight.
我們有權在不受立法監督的情況下對自身身體做出選擇。

3.
* moderation [͵mɑdəˋreʃən] (n.) 適度
: the quality of doing something within reasonable limits
- You can eat whatever you want as long as it's in moderation.
你想吃甚麼都可以，只要適量就好。

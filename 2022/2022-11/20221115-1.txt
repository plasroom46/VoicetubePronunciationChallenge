Pronunciation tips:

1. infected (the pronunciations of two "e"s)
2. undergo
3. programmed (the pronunciation of d)
4. limit (how to pronounce the two "i"s)
5. spread of (linking sound)

1.
* infected [ɪnˋfɛktɪd] (adj.) 受感染的
: containing bacteria or other things that can cause disease
- After the operation the wound became infected.
手術後傷口受到了感染。

2.
* undergo [͵ʌndɚˋgo] (v.) 經歷
: to experience something that is unpleasant or something that involves a change
- After the accident, he underwent reconstructive surgery to rebuild his face.
意外發生後，他經歷了整形手術來重建臉部。

3.
* spread [sprɛd] (n.) 擴散
: the development or growth of something so that it covers a larger area or affects a larger number of people
- The wildfire spread rapidly due to the strong wind.
強風導致野火迅速蔓延。

Pronunciation tips:

1. delicate: (1) Three parts: de-li-cate /ˈdel.ə.kət/ (2) Notice the pronunciation of last part. The vowel here is /ə/, not /e/.
2. surgeries: (1) It is the plural of “surgery. (2) Three parts:sur-ge-ry /ˈsɝː.dʒər.i/ (3) /ɝ/ pronunciation : It is a stressed vowel, so you should raise your tone when pronunciation the world, like in “bird.” Ijn contrast, /ɚ / is unstressed, so you don’t have to raise intonation, like in “mother.”
3. equation: (1) Three parts: /ɪˈkweɪ.ʒən/ (2) Imagining you are pronouncinge /ʃ/, so the mouth shape is the same, while it’s voiced. It’s like a soft G.

1.
* delicate [ˋdɛləkət] (adj.) 需要小心處理的
: needing to be done carefully
- Such a delicate operation needs to be performed by an experienced surgeon.
如此精細的手術需要由經驗豐富的外科醫生進行。

2.
* surgery [ˋsɝdʒərɪ] (n.) 外科手術
: the treatment of injuries or diseases in people or animals by cutting open the body and removing or repairing the damaged part
- He's a specialist in brain surgery.
他是一名腦外科專家。

3.
* equation [ɪˋkweʃən] (n.) 方程式
: a mathematical statement in which you show that two amounts are equal using mathematical symbols
- The equation is too hard to solve for junior high students.
這個方程式對國中生來說太難解了。

While pretending to inform us about the state of the world, the news has become a formidable instrument of self-forgetting.

在假裝向我們通報世界狀況的同時，新聞已經成為一個強大的自我忘卻的工具。

Pronunciation tips:

1. Inform us: (1) Linking (2) Two parts: in-form /ɪnˈfɔːrm/
2. Formidable: (1) Four parts: for-mi-da-ble /fɔːrˈmɪd.ə.bəl/ (2) Notice the stress is in the first part.
3. Instrument: (1) Three parts: in-stru-ment /ˈɪn.strə.mənt/

1.
* inform [ɪnˋfɔrm] (v.) 告知
: to tell someone about particular facts
- The store didn’t inform customers about the change of the activity.
這家商店沒有告知顧客活動的變化。

2.
* formidable [ˋfɔrmɪdəb!] (adj.) 可怕的
: causing you to have fear or respect for something or someone because that thing or person is large, powerful, or difficult
- The invention of such a formidable weapon causes people to live in fear.
如此強大武器的誕生使人們生活在恐懼之中。

3.
* instrument [ˋɪnstrəmənt] (n.) 手段
: a way of achieving or causing something
- News has already become a kind of instrument to control people’s thoughts and mindsets.
新聞已經成為一種控制人們思想和心態的工具。

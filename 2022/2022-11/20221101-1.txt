Small and helpless things trigger an instinct to nurture and protect, which helps our species survive.

小而無助的東西觸發人們想養育和保護的本能，這幫助我們的物種存活。

Pronunciation tips:

1. trigger (the pronunciation of tri)
2. instinct to (the pronunciation of tin and the linking sound)
3. protect (how to pronounce the o)
4. species (the pronunciation of pe)
5. survive (how to pronounce sur)

1.
* trigger [ˋtrɪgɚ] (v.) 引發
: to cause something to start
- The accident may have triggered bad memories for him.
此次事故可能觸發了他不好的回憶。

2.
* instinct [ˋɪnstɪŋkt] (n.) 本能
: the way people or animals naturally react or behave, without having to think or learn about it
- My instinct told me to run, so I ran away from him once I had a chance.
直覺告訴我該逃，所以一有機會我就從他身邊逃走了。

3.
* nurture [ˋnɝtʃɚ] (v.) 養育
: to take care of, feed, and protect someone or something, especially young children or plants, and help him, her, or it to develop
- Many first-time parents wonder about the best way to nurture and raise their children.
許多初次做父母的人都想知道養育孩子的最佳方式。

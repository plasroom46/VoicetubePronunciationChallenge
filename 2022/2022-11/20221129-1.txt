Instead of luxuriating in the power of yet, they were gripped in the tyranny of now.

他們不但不能盡情享受「還沒」的力量，他們反而困在目前的艱難處境。

Pronunciation tips:

1. instead of (linking sound)
2. luxuriating (how to pronounce ri and a)
3. gripped (the short i)
4. tyranny (how to pronounce ty and ra)

1.
* luxuriate [lʌgˋʒʊrɪ͵et] (v.) （尤指物質方面）享受
: to get great pleasure from something, especially because it provides physical comfort
- People can come here and luxuriate in nature.
人們可以來到這裡盡情享受大自然。

2.
* grip [grɪp] (v.) （感情等）強烈地控制
: when an emotion such as fear grips you, you feel it strongly.
- He turned towards me, and I was suddenly gripped by fear.
他轉身面向我，而我突然感到驚恐無比。

3.
* tyranny [ˋtɪrənɪ] (n.) 暴虐
: a situation in which someone or something controls how you are able to live, in an unfair way
- The political crisis arose from threats of external invasion, internal tyranny, and discontent among the lower classes.
此次政治危機是因外部入侵的威脅、內部暴政和下層階級的不滿所引起。

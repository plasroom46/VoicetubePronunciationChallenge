我能夠說明我的觀點同時考量他們的意見， 最終提出一個所有人都認同的替代方案。

Pronunciation tips:

1.Stance: The speaker highlights the word by putting more stress here.
2.While: You don’t have to aspirate it. There is no /h/ sound.
3.Came up with: Linking. No need to pronounce the /p/ sound clearly.
4.Alternate: Notice the stees is in the first syllable, “al.” /ˈɑːl.tɚ.neɪt/
5.Get on: linking

1.
* explain [ɪkˋsplen] (v.) 解釋
: to make something clear or easy to understand by describing or giving information about it
- He explains his marketing plan to his colleagues.
他向他的同事們解釋了他的行銷計畫。

2.
* stance [stæns] (n.) （尤指公開表明的）觀點
: a way of thinking about something, especially expressed in a publicly stated opinion
- She maintained a neutral stance during the negotiations.
她在整場談判中持中立態度。

3.
* alternate [ˋɔltɚnɪt] (adj.) 供選擇的
: An alternate plan or method is one that you can use if you do not want to use another one.
- We have to come up with an alternative plan in case we cannot successfully persuade them.
我們必須想出一個替代方案，以免我們無法成功說服他們。

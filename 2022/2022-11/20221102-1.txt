Pronunciation tips:



1.
* event [ɪˋvɛnt] (n.) 事件，大事
: anything that happens, especially something important or unusual
- School sports day is a big event for students, where students can have sports games with each other, but parents can also come and see the games together.

2.
* weak [wik] (adj.) （身體）虛弱的
: not physically strong
- Tigers will abandon their cubs if they think the cub is too weak to survive in the wilderness.
如果老虎認為幼崽太虛弱，無法在野外生存，就會拋棄幼崽。

3.
* mix [mɪks] (v.) 混合
: to (cause different substances to) combine, so that the result cannot easily be separated into its parts
- Since oil cannot mix with water, it’ll float on the surface.
因為油和水不能相融，所以油會浮在表面上。

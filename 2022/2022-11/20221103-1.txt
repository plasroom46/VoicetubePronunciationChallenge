Pronunciation tips:



1.
* have a ball [hæv e bɔl] (phr.) 玩得開心，盡興
= have a good time = enjoy your time = have fun
: to enjoy yourself very much
- Thanks for throwing a birthday party for me. I really had a ball.
謝謝你們為我舉辦生日派對。我真的玩得很盡興。
* keep one’s eye on the ball [kip wʌns aɪ ɑn ðə bɔl] (phr.) 集中注意力
: to give your attention to what you are doing at the time
- Sarah, your sale 's record is way down the past quarter, you better start keeping your eye on the ball.
Sarah，你的銷售額在上個季度已經下降了，你最好注意一點。
* balls [bɔls] (n.) 勇氣，膽量
: have fun; have a blast

2.
* appreciate [əˋpriʃɪ͵et] (v.) 欣賞，重視；理解；感謝，感激
: to recognize how good someone or something is and to value him, her, or it
: used when you are thanking someone or showing that you are grateful
- The boss appreciated Kate’s talent and invited her to join this team.
老闆很欣賞 Kate 的能力，邀請她加入這個團隊。
* thank [θæŋk] (v.) 感謝，感激
* thanks to [θæŋks tu] (phr.) 由於，因為
: as a result of; due to.; because of
- It’s thanks to you that he’s in this mess.
多虧了你，他陷入了一團亂。

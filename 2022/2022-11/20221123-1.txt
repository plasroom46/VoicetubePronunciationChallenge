There's a wonderful quote by the spiritual writer Richard Rohr. He says, "...if we do not transform our pain, we will most assuredly transmit it."

精神作家理查德·羅爾有一句棒的名言， 他說「……如果我們不改變我們的痛苦，我們肯定會散播它。」

Pronunciation tips:

1. There’s a: Linking.
2. Spiritual: (1) /ˈspɪr.ə.tʃu.əl/ (2) It is /ˈspɪr.ə.tʃu.əl/, not /ˈspɪrt..tʃu.əl/
3. Assuredly: (1) Two parts: assure-dly /əˈʃʊr.ɪd.li/ (2) There is no /ə/ sound in /d.li/
4. Transmit it: (1) Linking together (2) The repetitive /ɪt/ sound: Only needs to pronounce once.

1.
* quote [kwot] (n.) 引文
: a quotation from a text or speech.
- She began her speech with an inspirational quote.
她用一句鼓舞人心的名言開始她的演講。

2.
* assuredly [əˋʃʊrɪdlɪ] (adv.) 肯定地
: certainly
- Their attempts will assuredly fail.
他們的努力一定會白費。

3.
* transmit [trænsˋmɪt] (v.) 傳播
: to pass something from one person or place to another
- As a leader, you should be confident about the challenges, or your fear will transmit to your teammates.
作為一個領導者，你應該對挑戰充滿信心，否則你的恐懼會散播給你的隊友。

Pronunciation tips:

tile
assemble
coaster

1.
* tile [taɪl] (n.) 瓦片；瓷磚
: a thin, usually square or rectangular piece of baked clay, plastic, etc. used for covering roofs, floors, walls, etc.
- 
* tile [taɪl] (v.) 給…鋪瓦；給…貼瓷磚
: to cover a wall or floor with tiles
- We are going to re-tile the bathroom this weekend.
這周末我們要重新鋪浴室的磁磚。

2.
* assemble [əˋsɛmb!] (v.) 組裝
: to make something by joining separate parts
- 
* assemble [əˋsɛmb!] (v.) 集合，聚集；收集
: to come together in a single place or bring parts together in a single group
- The students are assembled outside by the school gate, getting ready to go on their field trip.
學生們集合在校門旁邊，準備出發校外教學。

3.
* coaster [ˋkostɚ] (n.) 杯墊；船隻
(n.)杯墊: a small piece of wood, plastic, or other material that you put a glass or cup on to protect a surface from heat or liquid
(n.)船隻: a ship that sails between ports along a coast
- Hang on, the coffee is really hot. Let me get you a coaster to protect the wooden table.
等等，這咖啡很燙。讓我幫你拿一個杯墊來保護木頭桌子。

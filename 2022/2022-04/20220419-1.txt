Pronunciation tips:



1.
* official [əˈfɪʃəl] (n.) 官員；公務員
: a person who has a position of responsibility in an organization
- I’ve heard that government officials get paid a lot.
我聽說政府官員薪資豐厚。

2.
* troop [trup] (n.) 軍隊；一群
: soldiers on duty in a large group
- The King's Troop of the Royal Horse Artillery has returned.
國王的皇家騎炮兵團歸來了。

3.
* deploy [dɪˈplɔɪ] (v.) 部署；調配
: to move soldiers or equipment to a place where they can be used when they are needed
- The US has made the decision to deploy more troops to the battlefield.
美國已決定向戰場部署更多軍隊。

Pronunciation tips:

recently
campaign
issue

1.
* recently [ˋrisntlɪ] (adv.) 最近、不久前
: not long ago
- It’s been a while since I last met you. How have you been recently?
上次看到你已經是好久以前了。最近過得好嗎？

2.
* campaign [kæmˋpen] (n.) 運動、活動
: a planned group of especially political, business, or military activities that are intended to achieve a particular aim
- A religious campaign is taking place in front of the historical building.
一個宗教活動正在那具歷史性的大樓前進行著。


3.
* issue [ˋɪʃjʊ] (v.) 發布、核發
: to produce or provide something official
- That organization will issue all the travel documents you need as soon as possible.
那個組織將會儘早核發所有您需要的旅行資料。
* issue [ˋɪʃjʊ] (n.) 爭議、問題
: a subject or problem that people are thinking and talking about
- There has been a constant drumbeat over this ongoing issue recently.
這個持續存在的問題最近廣受辯論。

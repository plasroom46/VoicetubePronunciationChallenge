Pronunciation tips:



1.
* civil [ˈsɪvəl] (adj.) 國內的；民事的
: relating to citizens of a state or nation
- Helicopters are mainly used for military rather than civil use.
直升機主要用於軍事而非民用。

2.
* previous [ˈpriviəs] (adj.) 先前的；過去的
: happening or existing before something
- John has two daughters from a previous marriage.
約翰在前次婚姻中育有兩個女兒。

3.
* flee [fli] (v.) 逃離
: to escape by running away, especially because of danger or fear
- In order to avoid being captured, Arthur fled to the mountains.
為了免遭俘虜，亞瑟逃到了山裡。

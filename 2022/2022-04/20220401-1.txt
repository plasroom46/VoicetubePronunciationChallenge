十幾歲的時候，我總是幻想自己遇到另一半的那一天。

Pronunciation tips:

1. teenager (the pronunciation of "ee")
2. fantasize (the pronunciations of two "a"s)
3. significant (the "i" of "fi")

1.
* used to [juzd. tu] (ph.) 過去慣常，過去常常
: something that repeatedly happened in the past
- I used to go to the movies with my friends a lot. 我以前常常跟朋友一起去看電影。

2.
* fantasize [ˋfæntə͵saɪz] (v.) 空想；幻想
: to think about something very pleasant that is unlikely to happen
- Emma used to fantasize about having a white horse at her wedding. Emma 曾經幻想過她的婚禮上會有匹白馬。

3.
* significant [sɪgˋnɪfəkənt] (adj.) 有特殊意義的
: having a special meaning
- All of us want our lives to be significant. 每個人都希望自己的人生是有意義的。

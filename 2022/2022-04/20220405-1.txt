Pronunciation tips:



1.
* invention [ɪnˈvɛnʃən] (n.) 發明品；發明
: Something created that has never been made before
- If you don't patent your invention, other people might make all the profit out of it.
如果你不為自己的發明申請專利，別人很可能藉此牟利。

2.
* tinker [ˈtɪŋkɚ] (v.) 修補；玩弄；瞎搞；改良
: to make small changes to something
- He spends every weekend tinkering (around) with his car.
他每個週末都在擺弄他的汽車。

3.
* flavor [ˈflevɚ] (n.) 味道；風味
: the taste of food or drink
- The store sells 32 different flavors of ice cream.
這間店販賣32種不同口味的冰淇淋。

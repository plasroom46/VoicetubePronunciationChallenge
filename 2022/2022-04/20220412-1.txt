Pronunciation tips:



1.
* asymptomatic [ˌeɪ.sɪmp.təˈmæt̬.ɪk] (adj.) 無症狀
: showing no symptoms of a particular disease
- Even if you're asymptomatic, you can still transmit the virus, so never let your guard(防護n.) down.

2.
* required [rɪ'kwaɪəd] (v.) 必須的
= mandatory = obligatory= compulsory
: It means something that’s very necessary.
- You're required by law to stop your car after an accident. Now, if you choose to drive away, that is a hit-and-run(肇事逃逸).

3.
* facility [fəˈsɪlɪti] (n.) 設施；場所
: a place or a building where a particular activity happens
- A U.S. spy(間諜) was arrested(被逮捕) in a military facility in China.

Pronunciation tips:

perfectionism
despairing
impulsive

1.
* perfectionisim [pɚˋfɛkʃənɪzm] (n.) 至善論，完美主義
: the wish for everything to be correct or perfect
- I have an issue with perfectionism and cannot start anything as a result.

2.
* desparing [dɪˋspɛrɪŋ] (adj.) 絕望的；失去希望的
: showing or feeling that there is no hope and that you can do nothing to improve a difficult or worrying situation
- With each passing day, he became even more despairing.

3.
* impulsive [ɪmˋpʌlsɪv] (adj.) 衝動的
: showing behaviour in which you do things suddenly without any planning and without considering the effects they may have
- I avoid casinos now because I was an impulsive gambler.

Pronunciation tips:

inevitable
extinction
regrets

1.
* inevitable [ɪnˋɛvətəb!]] (adj.) 不可避免的；必然發生的
: certain to happen and unable to be avoided or prevented
- It seems inevitable that they'll lose with the way they started the game.

2.
* extinction [ɪkˋstɪŋkʃən] (n.) 滅絕；絕種
: a situation in which something no longer exists
- The mountain gorilla is very close to extinction.

3.
* regrets [rɪˋgrɛt] (n.) 懊悔，遺憾；惋惜；痛惜
: a feeling of sadness about something sad or wrong or about a mistake that you have made, and a wish that it could have been different and better
- It’s always best to live life with no regrets.

Pronunciation tips:

analyze
accordingly
specific

1.
* analyze [ˋæn!͵aɪz] (v.) 分析
: to study or examine something in detail, in order to discover more about it
- We had to properly analyze the situation before we went any further.

2.
* in advance [ɪn ədˋvæns] (adv.) 預先，事先，提前
: before a particular time, or before doing a particular thing
- I made sure to give my landlord 2 months of rent in advance before my long trip.

3.
* accordingly [əˋkɔrdɪŋlɪ] (adv.) 照著；相應地
: in a way that is suitable or right for the situation
- We have to follow the user manual accordingly so we won’t make any mistakes building this table.

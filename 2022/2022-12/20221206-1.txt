Obesity is a condition of excess body fat that occurs when a person's BMI is above 30, just over the overweight range of 25 to 29.9.

體脂肪過高是肥胖的起因，當一個人的 BMI 值超過 30 就會發生，就正好和過重在 25-29.9 的範圍銜接。

* obesity [oˋbisətɪ] (n.) 肥胖；肥胖症
: the fact of being extremely fat, in a way that is dangerous for health
- Obesity is a complex disease involving an excessive amount of body fat.
肥胖症是一種體內脂肪過多的複雜疾病。

* occur [əˋkɝ] (v.) （尤指事故及其他意外事件）發生
: (especially of accidents and other unexpected events) to happen
- About three-quarters of suicide incidents occur at home.
大約有四分之三的自殺案件是發生在 (自殺者) 家中。

A lightyear is a unit of astronomical distance equivalent to the distance that light travels in one year.

光年是一個天文距離單位，相當於光在一年中傳播的距離。

Pronunciation tips:

1. lightyear (the silent t)
2. astronomical (the pronunciations of the two o)
3. equivalent (how to pronounce the e)
4. distance (how to pronounce the a)

1.
* astronomical [͵æstrəˋnɑmɪk!] (adj.) 天文的
: connected with astronomy
- The scientist made many astronomical observations.
那位科學家進行了許多天文觀測。

2.
* equivalent [ɪˋkwɪvələnt] (adj.) 相等的
: having the same amount, value, purpose, qualities, etc.
- One liter is equivalent to 1000 milliliters.
一升相當於1000毫升。

Pronunciation tips:

extent
occasional
indicate

1.
* intense [ɪnˋtɛns] (adj.) 強烈的
: extreme and forceful or (of a feeling) very strong
- The intense cold weather in winter in Massachusetts made life extremely hard when the pilgrims first landed in the New World.
麻州劇烈的寒冬使得剛到新大陸(美洲)的清教徒，生活極度艱辛。

2.
* occasional [əˋkeʒən!] (adj.) 偶爾的
: not happening or done often or regularly
- The weather was good except for an occasional shower.
天氣非常好，除了那偶然的陣雨。

3.
* indicate [ˋɪndə͵ket] (v.) 指示；象徵；暗示
: to show, point, or make clear in another way
- Her words indicate that she is not going to stay on the team anymore.
她的話暗示著她不會再繼續待在團隊裡了。

Pronunciation tips:

observing
behaviors
sanity

1.
* observe [əbˋzɝv] (v.) 觀察；觀測；監視
: to watch carefully the way something happens or the way someone does something, especially in order to learn more about it
- My science teacher told us to observe all details.

2.
* behavior [bɪˋhevjɚ] (n.) 行為，舉止
: the way that someone behaves
- The student was in detention for his bad behaviour.

3.
* sanity [ˋsænətɪ] (n.) 心智健全的狀態，神智正常的狀態；頭腦清醒的狀態
: the state of having a healthy mind and not being mentally ill
- After what I saw, I questioned my sanity.

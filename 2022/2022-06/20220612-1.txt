Next time you feel caught up in your thoughts, try paying attention to them with curiosity, and not judgment.

下次當你被自己的想法困住時，試著用好奇心去注意它們，而不是批評。

Pronunciation tips:

1. caught up
2. curiosity
3. judgment

1.
* catch up [kætʃ ʌp] (phr.) 趕上
: to reach the same quality or standard as someone or something else
- Don’t skip the class too often because it is not easy to catch up with the heavy load of work.
不要太常翹課，因為要跟上繁重的課業不容易。

2.
* curiosity [͵kjʊrɪˋɑsətɪ] (n.) 好奇心；求知慾
: an eager wish to know or learn about something
- Curiosity killed the cat.
好奇心害死一隻貓。

3.
* judgment [ˋdʒʌdʒmənt] (n.) 判斷力；識別力
: the ability to form valuable opinions and make good decisions
* judgment [ˋdʒʌdʒmənt] (n.) 判決；批判
: an opinion or conclusion
- It is considered to be a careless action to make a judgment before knowing the underlying reasons behind the scene.
在未了解背後的理由前就進行批判是一個草率的行為。

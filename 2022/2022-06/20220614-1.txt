Pronunciation tips:



1.
* nation [ˈneʃən] (n.) 國家；國族
: a country, especially when thought of as a large group of people living in one area with their own government, language, and traditions
- All the nations of the world will be represented at the UN conference.
聯合國會議上將有各國代表出席。

2.
* pray [pre] (v.) 祈禱
: to speak to a god either privately or in a religious ceremony in order to express love, admiration, or thanks or in order to ask for something
- Let us pray for the victims of this terrible massacre.
讓我們為這次恐怖屠殺的受害者祈禱吧。

3.
* sibling [ˈsɪblɪŋ] (n.) 兄弟姊妹；手足
: a brother or sister
- I have seven siblings: six sisters and a brother.
我有七個兄弟姐妹：六個姐姐，一個哥哥。

Pronunciation tips:



1.
* halt [hɔlt] (v.) 停下；中止
: to make or to stop moving or doing something
- Military forces halted the demonstrators by blocking the road.
軍隊用封路的辦法來制止示威者前進。

2.
* indefinitely [ɪnˈdefɪnətli] (adv.) 無限期地；不確定地
: for a period of time with no fixed end
- The negotiations have been postponed indefinitely.
談判被無限延期了。

3.
* dozen [ˈdʌzən] (n.) 一打；十二個
: twelve of something
- A dozen eggs means 12 eggs.
一打雞蛋就是12顆雞蛋的意思。

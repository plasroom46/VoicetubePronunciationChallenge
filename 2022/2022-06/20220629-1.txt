Pronunciation tips:

detecting
Intelligence
Tantalizing

1.
* detect [dɪˋtɛkt] (v.) （尤指用特殊方法）發現，察覺，看出
: to notice something that is partly hidden or not clear, or to discover something, especially using a special method
- There are some dogs at the airport that can detect drugs on a person from smell alone.

2.
* tantalizing [ˋtænt!͵aɪzɪŋ] (adj.) 逗引性的；挑逗性的
: Something that is tantalizing causes desire and excitement in you, but is unlikely to provide a way of satisfying that desire.
- That new burger at McDonald’s looks tantalizing.

3.
* tragic [ˋtrædʒɪk] (adj.) 哀痛的；悲慘的
: very sad, often involving death and suffering
- Romeo and Juliet is a tragic love story.

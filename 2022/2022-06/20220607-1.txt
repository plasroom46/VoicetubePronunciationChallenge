Pronunciation tips:



1.
* shortage [ˈʃɔ:rtɪdʒ] (n.) 短缺
: a situation in which there is not enough of something
- The long hot summer has led to serious water shortages.
這個夏天漫長而炎熱，導致嚴重缺水。

2.
* outage [ˈaʊtɪdʒ] (n.) 停電；停電期間
: a period when a service, such as electricity, is not available
- Breaking news reported power outages affecting over 50 homes.
突發新聞報導停電影響逾50家戶。

3.
* overturn [ˌovɚˈtɚn] (v.) 推翻
: To defeat or remove someone from power, using force
: To challenge and overthrow, subvert an authority.
- Some high-ranking officers plotted to overturn the government.
有幾名高級軍官策劃推翻政府。

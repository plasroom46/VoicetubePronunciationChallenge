1.
* glad [glæd] (adj.) 高興的
: pleased and happy
- I’m so glad to hear from you.
很高興能聽到你的消息。

2.
* reminder [rɪˋmaɪndɚ] (n.) 提醒者/物；令人回憶的東西
: a person or thing that makes you remember a particular person, event, or situation
- Her inspirational story is a reminder of how big your dream can be.
她的勵志故事提醒著人們夢想可以有多大。

3.
* strength [strɛŋθ] (n.) 力量；長處
: strong or powerful things
- Knowing what your strength is and what sets you apart from others are the key to success.
知道自己的長處與知道自己與眾不同的地方為何是成功的關鍵。

Pronunciation tips:

dwell on
vulnerable
obey

1.
* vulnerable [ˋvʌlnərəb!] (adj.) 易受傷的；脆弱的
: able to be easily physically, emotionally, or mentally hurt, influenced, or attacked
- In the presence of nature, human beings are so vulnerable.
在大自然面前，人類是如此脆弱。

2.
* dwell on (phr.) 老是想著，一直在說（尤指不好或令人不快之事）
: to keep thinking or talking about something, especially something bad or unpleasant
- It’s the time to move on, and we shouldn’t dwell on the past anymore.
現在是向前看的時候，我們不應該再糾纏於過去。

3.
* obey [əˋbe] (v.) 服從；遵守
: to act according to what you have been asked or ordered to do by someone in authority,or to behave according to a rule, law, or instruction
- Soldiers have to obey the general’s command.
士兵們必須服從將軍的命令。

Pronunciation tips:



1.
* potential [pəˈtɛnʃəl] (n.) 可能性；潛力
: someone's or something's ability to develop, achieve, or succeed
-  I don’t know... I don't feel I'm achieving my full potential in my present job.
不曉得耶，我覺得目前的工作沒有完全發揮我的潛力。

2.
* derive [dɪˈraɪv] (v.) 源自
: to take or receive something from something else
- Many English words are actually derived from the French language.
許多英文字其實源自於法語。

3.
* ingredient [ɪnˈɡridiənt] (n.) 成分；原料
: one of the parts to make up something
- The list of ingredients included 200 grams of almonds.
清單上列出的材料包括200克的杏仁。
- Trust is a vital ingredient in a successful marriage.
信任是婚姻幸福的重要因素。

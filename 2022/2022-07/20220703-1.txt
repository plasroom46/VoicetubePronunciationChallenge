1.
* cannibalism [ˋkænəb!͵ɪzəm] (n.) 食人；自相殘殺
: the practice of a person who eats human flesh, or the behavior of an animal that eats others of its own type
- They were forced to practice cannibalism in order to survive.
為了生存，他們被迫自相殘殺。


2.
* frown upon [fraʊn əˋpɑn] (phr.) 不贊成；不許
: to disapprove of (something)
- Smoking is frowned upon in many restaurants.
許多餐廳都禁止吸煙。

3.
* society [səˋsaɪətɪ] (n.) 社會
: a large group of people who live together in an organized way
- The role of women in modern society is no longer restricted to doing household work and looking after their children and family.
女性在現今社會的角色已經不僅限於做家事和照顧小孩與家庭。

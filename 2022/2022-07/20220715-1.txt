Pronunciation tips:

1. cooperation (how to pronounce "coo")
2. put into (linking sound)
3. split (the pronunciations of "p" and "i")

1.
* incentivize [ɪnˈsentɪvaɪz] (v.) 刺激；激勵
: to make someone want to do something
- The government should try to incentivize businesses to create jobs.
政府應嘗試激勵企業創造工作機會。

2.
* cooperation [ko͵ɑpəˋreʃən] (n.) 合作；協作；配合
: the act of working together with someone or doing what they ask you
- I need your cooperation to make this event a success.
這次活動想成功需要你的配合。

3.
* evenly [ˋivənlɪ] (adv.) 平均地；均等地
: in or into equal amounts
- The pie was evenly divided into eight slices.
那塊派被平均的分成了八片。

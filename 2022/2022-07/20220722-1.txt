在人類進化過程的一開始，並不是一醒來就可以在冰箱裡拿到食物的。

Pronunciation tips:

1. evolved (the pronunciation of "e")
2. available (the pronunciations of "a"s)
3. woke up (linking sound)

1.
* evolve [ɪˋvɑlv] (v.) 逐步演變；逐漸形成；進化
: to develop gradually, or to cause something or someone to develop gradually
- Did humans evolve from apes?
人類是由猿進化而來的吗？

2.
* readily [ˋrɛdɪlɪ] (adv.) 迅速地；心甘情願地；輕而易舉地
: quickly, immediately, or without any problem
- The new proposals were readily adopted by the company.
新的提議很迅速就被公司採納了。

3.
* available [əˋveləb!] (adj.) 可獲得的；可用的
: able to be bought or used
- This article is now available on the website.
現在在網站上可以取得這篇文章。

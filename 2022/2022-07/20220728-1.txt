Pronunciation tips:

applicants
certified
reservation

1.
* applicant [ˋæpləkənt] (n.) 申請人
: a person who formally requests something, especially a job, or to study at a college or university
- The job applicant must have good writing skills.
這份工作的申請人必須有良好的寫作能力。

2.
* certified [ˋsɝtə͵faɪd] (adj.) 被證明的、公認的
: having a document that proves that you have successfully finished a course of training, or have the necessary qualifications.
- After finishing the whole program, Sharon becomes a certified TESOL teacher。
完成整個學程後，Sharon 成為了一位認證的TESOL 老師。

3.
* onward [ˋɑnwɚd] (adv.) 向前地
: moving forward to a later time or a more distant place
- The bear moves onward into the forest.
熊向前往森林邁進。

4.
* reservation [͵rɛzɚˋveʃən] (n.) 保留
: an arrangement in which something such as a seat on an aircraft or a table at a restaurant is kept for you
- I would like to make a table reservation for four people for 6:30 PM.
我想要訂位，四人，晚上六點半。

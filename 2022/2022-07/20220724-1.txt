I would get away with it a lot of the time, but some sometime down the line, I realized that pretending like this person is not me was affecting other people negatively.

我在很多時候都能這樣成功脫困，但在某個時候意識到，假裝這個人不是我，對其他人產生了負面影響。

Pronunciation tips:

get away with it
realized
pretending
negatively

1.
* realize [rɪə͵laɪz] (v.) 領悟；明白
: to understand a situation, sometimes suddenly
- Not until his girlfriend left him did Johnny realize how selfish he used to be.
一直到他女朋友離開他時，Johnny才明白以前的自己有多自私。

2.
* pretend [prɪˋtɛnd] (v.) 假裝
: to behave as if something is true when you know that it is not, especially in order to deceive people or as a game
- After a big fight, Lucy and Stacy pretend they don’t know each other。
大吵一架後，Lucy 和 Stacy 假裝他們不認識彼此。

3.
* affect [əˋfɛkt] (v.) 影響
: to have an influence on someone or something, or to cause a change in someone or something
- Family education can greatly affect one’s thoughts and behaviors.
家庭教育會大大的影響一個人的思想與行為。

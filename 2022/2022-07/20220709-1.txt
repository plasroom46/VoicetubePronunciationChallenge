In order to grow, you have to surround yourself with trustworthy people who will support you and offer candid and honest opinions.

為了成長，你身邊必須有值得信賴的人，他們會支持你並提供坦率和誠實的意見。

Pronunciation tips:

Surround
Trustworthy
Honest

“Stay away from negative people, they have a problem for every solution.” Albert Einstein

1.
* surround [səˋraʊnd] (v.) 環繞，圍繞，包圍
: to be everywhere around something
- I always make sure to surround myself with successful people so I can always be improving.

2.
* trustworthy [ˋtrʌst͵wɝðɪ] (adj.) 值得信任的；可信的；可靠的
: able to be trusted
- We have to make sure he/she is a trustworthy person before we allow him/her to enter our group.

3.
* candid [ˋkændɪd] (adj.) （尤指對棘手或令人痛苦之事）率直的，坦誠的，直言不諱的
: honest and telling the truth, especially about something difficult or painful
- I am quite candid with my friends.

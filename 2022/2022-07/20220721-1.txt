Pronunciation tips:

serve
vehicle
pump

1.
* serve [sɝv] (v.) 為...服務；供應
: to work for
- Kenny has served in the army for more than 20 years。
Kenny已經在軍中服務超過20年了。

2.
* vehicle [ˋviɪk!] (n.) 運載工具；車輛
: a machine, usually with wheels and an engine, used for transporting people or goods, especially on land
- He was not the registered owner of the vehicle, so he wasn’t able to change the signed contract.
他並非註冊的車輛擁有者，所以他無法更動已簽約的合約。

3.
* pump [pʌmp] (v.) 打（氣）
: to force liquid or gas to move somewhere
- You should pump enough air into the bicycle tires.
你需要打足夠的氣到腳踏車輪胎裡。

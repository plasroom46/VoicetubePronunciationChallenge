Pronunciation tips:

resources

1.
* evolve [ɪˋvɑlv] (v.) （使）逐步發展；（使）逐步演變；（使）逐漸形成；（使）進化
: to develop gradually, or to cause something or someone to develop gradually
- The company has evolved into a major semiconductor manufacturer. 
這家公司已經發展成為一家主要的半導體製造商。

2.
* protection [prəˋtɛkʃən] (n.) 保護，防護；警戒
: the act of protecting or state of being protected
- Remember to carry rain protection just in case it starts to rain during the hike. 
記得攜帶雨具，萬一爬山時候開始下雨。

3.
* resource [rɪˋsors] (n.) 資源；財力；物力
: a useful or valuable possession or quality of a country, organization, or person
- Some of the most important natural resources to human life and society include water, oil, coal, forests and iron. 
一些對人類生活和社會最重要的自然資源包括水、石油、煤炭、森林和鐵。

You feel and function better when you are aware of and deal with your "real" emotions, even if they are painful.

就算是痛苦的感受，只要願意敞開心胸、傾聽自我，就能體認到自己的真實感受，內心也才會愉悅並且充滿力量。

Pronunciation tips:

function
aware of
deal
painful

1.
* function [ˋfʌŋkʃən] (v.) 運行；起作用
: to work or operate
- Thankfully, the organization functions very efficiently.
謝天謝地，這個機構運作地十分有效率。

2.
* be aware of (phr.) 意識到；明白
: having or showing realization, perception, or knowledge
- I wasn’t aware of the danger at all when I walked into the sixth street in downtown San Francisco。
當我走進位於舊金山鬧區的六街時，我完全沒有意識到危險。

3.
* deal with (phr.) 處理
: to take action in order to achieve something or in order to solve a problem
- She is good at dealing with complex data.
她擅長處理複雜的數據。

4.
* painful [ˋpenfəl] (adj.) 痛苦的；困難的
: causing emotional or physical pain
- It was a painful decision, but Nancy decided to leave her beloved, worked abroad, and fulfilled her childhood dream as an astronaut.
這是個困難的決定，但Nancy決定要離開她的愛人，到異地工作，並且完成兒時想成為太空人的夢想。

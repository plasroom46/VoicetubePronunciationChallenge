We live in a world that is constantly demanding not only our attention but dictating what our reasonable response time is.

我們生活在一個不斷要求我們注意的世界中，而且還要在合理的時間內回應。

Pronunciation tips:

1. constantly (the pronunciation of "tan")
2. dictating (the pronunciations of "c" and "a")
3. reasonable (how to pronounce "son")

1.
* constantly [ˋkɑnstəntlɪ] (adv.) 總是；經常地，不斷地
: all the time or often
- He's constantly changing his mind.
他的想法總是在變。

2.
* demand [dɪˋmænd] (v.) 堅決請求，強烈要求；強要
: to ask for something forcefully, in a way that shows that you do not expect to be refused
- Mary's boss demanded she finish the project and report to him before Friday.
Mary 的老闆要求她在星期五之前完成項目並向他匯報。

3.
* dictate [ˋdɪktet] (v.) 命令，下令；決定，規定
: to give orders, or tell someone exactly what they must do, with total authority
- He disagrees with the government dictating what children are taught in schools.
他不同意由政府來決定孩子在學校學習哪些課程。

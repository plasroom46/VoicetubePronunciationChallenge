Pronunciation tips:

technical
limitation
variation

1.
* technical [ˋtɛknɪk!] (adj.) 技術（上）的
: relating to the knowledge, machines, or methods used in science and industry
- Because of some technical problems, the television cannot be turned on right now.
因為一些技術上的問題，電視現在無法打開。

2.
* limitation [͵lɪməˋteʃən] (n.) 限制；極限
: the act of controlling and especially reducing something
- Knowing her limitations, Nancy decides not to participate in the basketball game.
知道自己的能力的極限，Nancy 決定不參加籃球比賽。

3.
* variation [͵vɛrɪˋeʃən] (n.) 變化
: a change in amount or level
- There are many regional variations in Taiwanese pronunciation.
台語的發音會隨著地區而有所變化。

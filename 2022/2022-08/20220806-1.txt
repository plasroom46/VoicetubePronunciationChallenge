Pronunciation tips:

calibrated
mixtures
ingredients

1.
* calibrate [ˋkælə͵bret] (v.) 劃分刻度，標定
: to mark units of measurement on an instrument such so that it can measure accurately
- The machine has been properly calibrated to work perfectly.

2.
* mixture [ˋmɪkstʃɚ] (n.) 混合物；混合體；混合料
: a substance made from a combination of different substances, or any combination of different things
- The color green is a mixture of the colors blue and yellow.

3.
* ingredient [ɪnˋgridɪənt] (n.) （食品的）成分，材料
: a food that is used with other foods in the preparation of a particular dish
- What was the secret ingredient you used for your fried chicken recipe?

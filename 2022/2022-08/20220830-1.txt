要做到完美無瑕的壓力很大，因為我們在隨處可見的社交媒體上看到所謂完美。

Pronunciation tips:



1.
* pressure [ˋprɛʃɚ] (n.) 壓力
: a difficult feeling that you get from being worried or unhappy
- Be nice to him - he's been under a lot of pressure recently.
對他好一點——最近他壓力很大。

2.
* flawless [ˋflɔlɪs] (adj.) 無瑕疵的；無缺點的
: perfect or without mistakes
- Congratulations, Julia. That was a flawless performance!
恭喜你，茱莉亞，真是完美的演出！

3.
* perfection [pɚˋfɛkʃən] (n.) 完美；卓越
: the state of being complete and correct in every way
- Voltaire once said, “Perfection is the enemy of good.”
伏爾泰曾經說過「完美是善的敵人」。

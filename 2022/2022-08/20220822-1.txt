Pronunciation tips:

sensation (has three syllables)
inherently (UK and US versions are a little bit different)
prompt (is a one-syllable word and read as it is spelt) 

1.
* sensation [sɛnˋseʃən] (n.) 感覺；知覺
: the ability to feel something physically, especially by touching, or a physical feeling that results from this ability 
- Putting my feet inside a fish spa was an odd, tingling sensation.
把我的腳放在魚療池裡是一種奇怪的、刺痛的感覺。

2.
* inherently [ɪnˋhɪrəntlɪ] (adv.) 與生俱來地
: in a way that exists as a natural or basic part of something
- It is inherently dangerous to be a firefighter. 
成為一名消防員本質上是危險的。

3.
* prompt [prɑmpt] (v.) 促使（某人）決定說（或做）某事
: to make something happen
- I don't know what prompted him to leave so suddenly.
我不知道是甚麼促使他突然離開的。

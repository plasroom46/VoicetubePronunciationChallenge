Pronunciation tips:

stipulate
minimum
average

1.
* stipulate [ˋstɪpjə͵let] (v.) 規定; 明確說明
: to say exactly how something must be or must be done
- The school rule stipulates that all students must wear school uniforms and backpacks to school.
學校規定所有學生都要穿學校制服和背書包上學。

2.
* minimum [ˋmɪnəməm] (n.) 最小值
: the smallest amount or number allowed or possible
- According to the policy, everyone should be guaranteed a monthly minimum wage.
根據政策，所有人都應該被保障最低月薪。

3.
* average [ˋævərɪdʒ] (adj.) 平均的
: An average number is the number you get by adding two or more amounts together and dividing the total by the number of amounts.
- Average incomes are rising more slowly.
平均收入增加的越來越緩慢。

Pronunciation tips:



1.
* shift [ʃɪft] (v.) 移動；改變
: to change from one position or direction to another.
- Media attention has shifted recently onto environmental issues.
媒體關注的焦點最近轉到了環保問題。

2.
* recognize [ˈrek.əɡ.naɪz] (v.) 承認；接受
: to accept that something is legal, true, or important
- The international community has refused to recognize the newly independent country.
國際社會拒絕承認這個新獨立的國家。

3.
* necessarily [ˌnesəˈserəli] (adv.) 必定 ； 必然
: used in negatives to mean "in every case" or "therefore"
- The fact that something is cheap doesn't necessarily mean it's of low quality.
價廉未必物不美。

1. 
a. continental (adj.)  洲的；大陸的
  -> A continental breakfast usually consists of fruit juice, coffee and bread with butter and jam.
    歐陸式早餐通常會有果汁、咖啡和奶油果醬麵包。
b. continent (n.)  大陸；陸地
  -> He's planning to drive across the North America continent from East to West.
    他計畫從北美洲的東岸開到西岸。
2. 
a. farthest (adj.)  最遠的
  * 通常形容時間or距離
  -> What is farthest you have ever run?
    你跑過最遠的距離是多遠？
b. furthest (adj.)  最大程度的
  * 常用於程度上的
  -> My guess was the furthest far from the actual situation.
    我的猜測與實際情況相差極遠。
c. far (adj/adv.)   遠；遙遠地（的）
  -> He doesn't live far from the office.
    他的住處離辦公室不遠。
  -> far from... 完全不
  -> He is a nice man, but far from handsome.
    他雖然是個好人，但是長得不帥。
3. 
a. grassland (n.)  草原
  -> Inner Mongolia is famous for its grasslands.
    中國內蒙古的草原非常著名。
b. lawn (n.)  草坪
  * 通常只定期有人修剪的草坪
  -> He needs to mow the lawn every two months or so. 
    他大約每隔兩個月就要去修剪草坪。
c. meadow (n.)  牧場；草地
  -> To find the log cabin, you need to follow the path through the meadow.
    沿著小徑穿過牧場，你就能找到那間小木屋。
4. 
a. hamlet (n.)  小村莊；哈姆雷特
  -> There are only two small hamlets in the mountain.
    這座山裡只有兩個小村莊。
  -> Hamlet is one of the four great tragedies of William Shakespeare.
    哈姆雷特是莎士比亞的四大悲劇之一。
b. village (n.)  村莊；村民
  -> This fishing village has a hundred year history.
    這個漁村有百年的歷史了。
c. town (n.)  鎮；市鎮
  -> I was in the town this morning, and I saw the parade.
    我今早在小鎮看到遊行。

1. 
a. elements (n.)  元素；要素，成分
  -> The four elements are fire, earth, air and water.
    四元素是火、土、空氣和水。
b. atom (n.)  原子
  -> atomic bomb 原子彈
  -> The atomic bombs that were dropped on Japan are called Fat Man and Little Boy.
    被放在日本的原子彈叫做Fat Man和Little Boy。
c. particle (n.)  粒子
d. molecule (n.)  分子
  -> I always forget the difference between atom, particle and molecule.
    我總是忘記原子、粒子和分子的差別是什麼。
e. liquid (n.)  液體
f. solid (n.)  固體；(a.) 堅固的
  -> This thing is solid.
    這東西很堅固。
g. gas (n.)  氣體；瓦斯
  -> When the liquid evaporates, it becomes gas. 
    當液體蒸發，會變成氣體。
2. 
a. cuisine (n.)  烹飪；菜餚
b. dish (n.)  菜餚
  -> Din Tai Fung soup dumpling is a famous dish from Taiwan. The branch in Hong Kong got rated one star by Michelin in 2010.
    鼎泰豐的湯包是台灣著名的美食。它在香港的分店在2010年被頒為米其林一顆星。
  -> Sharing food with friends allows you to try more dishes without getting full instantly.
    跟朋友一起分東西吃，可以分到比較多種食物而且也不會這麼飽。
c. foodie (n.)  美食家，美食主義者
  -> I'm a big foodie. I even started an Instagram account to record all the restaurants I've been to.
    我很愛吃。我甚至創了一個Instagram帳號來記錄我去過的餐廳。
3. 
a. evolve (v.)  使逐步形成；發展，展開
  -> Our friendship has evolved to a stage, where we feel like family.
    我跟你的友情已經發展到像家人的階段。
b. evolution 
  -> The theory of evolution states that living creatures evolve over time.
    演化論表示，生物會隨著時間的改變慢慢進化。
4. 
a. maintain (v.)  維持；保養；使繼續
  -> You need to maintain your good relationship with him, so we can land the client.
    你要跟他保持好關係，這樣我們才能得到這個客戶。
b. high maintenance (adj.)  難以伺候
  -> It's okay if you're high maintenance, because I like maintaining you.
    你難以伺候沒關係，因為我喜歡伺候你。<3
  -> My roommate is extremely high maintenance. I can't stand him anymore.
    我的室友很難搞，我已經快受不了她了。

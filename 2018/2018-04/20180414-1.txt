1. 
a. immensely (adv.)  非常；極其
  = extremely
  -> I enjoy his housewarming party immensely.
    我在他新居落成的聚會上玩得很盡興。
b. extremely (adv.)  非常；極其
  -> I’m extremely sorry about what I did to you.
    我對於我做的事感到非常抱歉。
c. quite (adv.)  完全；相當；很
  -> I quite like playing basketball.
    我滿喜歡打籃球的。
2. 
a. enclosed (adj.)  與世隔絕的；封閉的
  -> He doesn’t like enclosed space. For example, he will get nervous in the elevator.
    他不喜歡封閉的空間。比如說，他在電梯裡會感到很緊張。
b. enclose (v.)  圍起來；包住
  -> The fancy house is enclosed by high walls.
    那棟高級的房子被高牆圍著。
c. surround (v.)  圍繞；圍
  -> The village is surrounded by trees.
    那座村莊被樹包圍了。
3. 
a. particularly (adv.)  特別；尤其
  -> Please don’t touch my parents’ stuffs, particularly my mom’s makeup.
    請不要動我父母的東西，尤其是我媽的化妝品。
b. in particular 尤其是
  -> Are you looking for anything in particular?
    你在找什麼特定的東西嗎？
c. especially (adv.)  特別；尤其
  -> He loves to cook, especially spicy food.
    他喜歡下廚，特別是辣的食物。
4. 
a. scaredy cat (n.)   膽小鬼
  * 尤其指小朋友，或小朋友互相稱對方膽小鬼的時候用
  -> Children were calling that boy a scaredy cat, because he wouldn’t pat the dog.
    小孩們叫那個男孩膽小鬼，因為他不願意摸那隻狗。
b. coward (n.)   膽小鬼；懦夫
  -> She considered him a coward for not defending his family.
    她認為他沒有保護家人是一個懦夫的行為。
c. freak (n.)  怪胎；怪事
  -> You are a freak.
    你是個怪胎。
  -> Don’t see her as a freak but unique.
    不要把她當成怪胎，那是獨特。

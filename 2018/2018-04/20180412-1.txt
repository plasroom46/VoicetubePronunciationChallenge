1. 
a. tend to (phr.)  傾向, 易於
Pretty girls tend to get their ways.
人們總是會順著漂亮的女生意。(人正真好。)
b. tendency (n.)  傾向
Don’t take her word for it, she has a tendency to exaggerate.
不要太相信他說的話，因為他常常講話講得很誇張。
c. tender (adj.)  嫩的；溫柔的
He might look tough from the outside, but he’s actually a very tender person.
    他的外表看起來可能很強硬，但是他實際上是一個非常溫柔的人。
2. 
a. slyly (adv.)  偷偷地；狡猾地
  * 通常為較負面的詞
  -> He slyly tries to change your opinion without you ever realizing.
    他常偷偷在你沒發覺的時試著改變你的意見。
b. quietly (adv.)  輕聲地；安靜地；偷偷地
The company wants to make the scandal go away quietly.
這家公司想偷偷的讓這醜聞消失。
c. sneaky (adj.)  鬼鬼祟祟的；暗中的
Why do you look so sneaky over there?
為什麼你這那邊看起來這麼鬼鬼祟祟的？
d. low-key (adj.)  低調的
俚語
He low-key likes to brag about how much money he has.
他其實很喜歡炫耀他有多有錢。
3. 
a. truth (n.) 實話；事實
You have to tell him the truth, he deserves to know.
你必須要告訴他實話，他應該要知道。
b. sincere (adj.) 真誠的，真心實意的 
If you sounded more sincere, I might have bought it.
你如果剛剛講得更有誠意的話，我可能會相信你。
I don’t buy it. 我不相信你
c. honest (adj.) 誠實的 
honesty(n)
Honesty is the best policy.誠實為上策
d. genuine (adj.) 真誠的；不造作的 
She seemed genuine, I think she really wants to patch things up with you.
她看起來很有誠意，我覺得她真的想要跟你和好。
patch things up with+人:跟某人和好

4. 
a. routine (n.) 例行公事；日常工作；慣例 
I’m sick of having the same routine every single day, so I quit.
我已經受夠了每天都做例行的公事，所以我就辭職了。
b. standard (adj.) 標準的 
Back off ma'am. This is standard procedure.
女士請你往後退，這是標準的流程。
Back off 退後
c. ordinary (adj.) 普通的，平凡的 
I'd rather be anything but ordinary.
什麼我都願意，我就是不甘於平凡。

5. 
a. grief (n.) 悲痛，悲傷 
She is in grief right now, just be there for her.
她現在非常悲痛，只要陪伴她就好了。
b. mourn (v.) 表示哀悼 
The class is mourning over the death of their rabbit.
這個班級正在為去世的小白兔哀悼。
c. suffer (v.) 受苦 
He's suffering from cancer.
他正在受癌症所苦。
d. torment (v.) 使痛苦，折磨
The victim was tormented for days, but it turned out they had a wrong guy.
受害者受折磨好幾天，結果發現他們抓錯人了。

--------------------------------------
Societies tend slyly to insist on cheerfulness. We end up not only struggling, but humiliated that we are in such difficulties. Yet, in truth, there is nothing more natural or routine than grief.

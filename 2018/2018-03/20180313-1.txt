1. 
a. significant (adj.)  重要的、有意義的
  -> Becoming a good father is one of the most significant achievements in his life.
    成為一個好父親是他人生中一間意義非凡的成就。
b. important (adj.)  重要的、重大的
  -> It is important to pay attention to your diet, if you want to be healthy.
    如果你想保持健康，注意飲食是一件重要的事。
c. remarkable (adj.)  非凡的、卓越的
  -> Elbert Einstein was a remarkable scientist.
    愛因斯坦是位傑出的科學家。
d. momentous (adj.)  重要的、重大的
2. 
a. dissipate (v.)  逐漸消失、浪費
  -> The cockroaches soon dissipated when the janitor sprayed pesticide.
    在清潔人員噴完殺蟲劑之後，蟑螂很快就消失了。
  -> You should seize the chance. Don't let your passion dissipate.
    你應該要把握機會，不要讓你的熱情消逝了。
b. scatter (v.)  分散、散佈
  -> He scattered his clothes all over the floor.
    他把衣服扔了一地。
c. dispel (v.)  驅散、消除
  -> How can we dispel our own doubts and fears?
    要如何消除自身的疑慮或是恐懼呢？
3. 
a. environment (n.)  環境
  * 大範圍的環境
  -> We need to put our thought into action to protect our environment.
    我們要將想法化為行動才能保護環境。
b. surroundings (n.)  環境、周圍的情況
  * 小範圍的環境
  -> Always check your surroundings before putting the car in motion.
    每次在車子發動前，都要觀察一下周遭的環境。
c. neighborhood (n.)  鄰居、鄰近地區
  -> They live in the neighborhood of the big shopping mall.
    他們住在大型購物中心附近。
4. 
a. consequence (n.)  結果、後果
b. result (n.)  結果、成果
  -> To ensure good results, he put a lot of hard work into this project.
    為了讓這個案子有個好結果，他付出很多心血。
c. outcome (n.)  結果、後果
  -> He was hopeful about the outcome of the interview last week.
    他對於上週的面試結果抱持著希望。
d. aftermath (n.)  後果、餘波
  * 用於不愉快的事情
  -> Many people were homeless in the aftermath of the typhoon.
    颱風肆虐後，有許多人無家可歸。

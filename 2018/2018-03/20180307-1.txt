1. 
a. roughly (adv.) 大致上地；粗暴地
  -> roughly+數字
  -> Roughly speaking, he made forty thousand a month.
    大致上來說，他一個月賺四萬塊。
  -> He pushed her aside roughly.
    他很粗魯地把他推到一旁。
b. approximately (adv.) 大約
2. 
a. consumption (n.) 消耗；消耗量
b. consume (v.) 大量消耗、消費；吃、喝
  -> time-consuming (a.) 很耗費時間的
  -> Layla decided to reduce the amount of beverages she consumes.
    Layla決定要少喝一點飲料。
c. consumer (n.) 消費者
  -> Consumer Protection Act 消費者保護法
d. assume (v.) 假定、承擔、就任
  -> assume duties 承擔義務
  -> assume office 就職
  -> We can't assume his guilty, just because he remains silent.
    我們不能因為他保持緘默就認定他的罪。
e. presume (v.) 假定、認定
  -> You're Helen. I presume.
    我想妳是Helen吧。
  -> I presume that she's not coming, because she hasn't replied to my invitation.
    我推測她不會來了，因為她沒有回復我的邀請。
3. 
a. staggering (a.) 令人震驚的
  -> It costs a staggering amount of money to study at that art school.
    要進入那間藝術學校就讀，花費是很驚人的。
b. stagger (v.) 踉蹌、蹣跚；使震驚
  -> The drunker staggered along the street.
    那個醉漢在路邊踉蹌的走。
c. astonishing (a.) 驚人的
  -> Her first novel was an astonishing success.
    她的第一本小說獲得驚人的成功。
d. astounding (a.) 令人震驚的
  -> astounding fact 令人震驚的事實
  -> astounding decision 令人震驚的決定
  -> He made an astounding decision to quit his high salary job.
    他辭掉他的高薪工作，跌破所有人的眼鏡。

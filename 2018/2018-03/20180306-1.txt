1. 
a. starvation (n.)  飢餓、挨餓
  -> The old man died of starvation.
     這位老人被餓死了。
b. starve (v.)  挨餓、渴望
  -> Let's get something to eat. I'm starving.
    我們去吃點東西吧，我快餓死了。
  hunger (n.) 飢餓
  -> He has a hunger for knowledge.
    他有很強烈的求知慾。
c. craving (n.)  渴望
  -> I'm craving for sth.
  -> After watching that movie, I'm craving for ice cream.
    看完那部電影後，我好想吃冰淇淋。
2. 
a. deplete (v.)  用盡、耗盡……的資源
  -> If we continue to deplete the earth natural resources, we will cause serious damage to the environment.
    如果我們繼續耗盡地球的自然資源，我們會對環境造成很嚴重的破壞。
b. deprive (v.)  剝奪、使喪失
  -> You can't think clearly when you are deprived of sleep.
    如果你的睡眠被剝奪了，你就不能很清晰的思考。
c. take away 拿走、帶走
  -> Please take these chairs away, we don't need them anymore.
    請拿走這些椅子，我們不再需要用到它們了。
  -> Would you like to have it here, or to take away?
    你想要內用還是外帶？
d. seize (v.)  抓住、奪取
  -> seize the chance/opportunity
抓住機會
  -> He seized the chance to have a little chat with the experts in the seminar.
    他在研討會上抓住機會和專家聊一聊。
3. 
a. essential (adj.)  必要的、不可或缺的
  -> Water is essential for living things.
    水對生物是不可或缺的。
b. necessary (adj.)  必要的、必需的
  -> Oxygen is necessary/essential for life.
    空氣對生存來說是必須的。
c. compulsory (adj.)  強制的、義務的
  * 法律、規則規定的
  -> The law made wearing seat belts in cars compulsory.
    法律規定坐車要繫安全帶。
4. 
a. antibody (n.)  抗體
b. antibacterial (adj.)  抗菌的
  -> antibacterial hand wash 抗菌洗手乳
c. anti-virus (adj.)  預防電腦病毒的
  -> anti-virus software 防毒軟體
  -> anti-virus program 防毒程式
5. 
a. infection (n.)  傳染、感染
b. infect (v.)  傳染、受影響
  -> The flu virus infected almost the entire class.
    全班幾乎人人都染上了流行性感冒的病毒。
c. infectious (adj.)  傳染的、易傳播的
  -> infectious disease 傳染病
  -> infectious laugh 有感染力的笑聲
d. infatuation (n.)  熱戀、著迷
  -> No one expected their infatuation for each other to last this long.
    沒有人認為他們的熱戀會這麼持久。
6. 
a. glucose (n.)  葡萄糖
b. protein (n.)  蛋白質
  -> Meat and fish are the vital source of the protein.
    肉和魚是攝取蛋白質的重要來源。
c. vitamin (n.)  維他命
  -> Oranges and other citrus are full of vitamin C.
    柑橘類富含維他命C。
--------------------------------------
After several weeks of starvation, fat stores become depleted, and all that is left for energy is proteins. At this point essential proteins for survival, like antibodies used to fight infection or muscle proteins used for strength, are now being used to make glucose.

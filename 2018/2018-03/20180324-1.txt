1. 
a. variation (n.)  變化、差異
  -> Every day without variation, he wakes up at 7 a.m.
    他每天都早上七點起床。
b. vary (v.)  變化、更改
  -> The culture varies between countries.
    每個國家都有不同的文化。
c. alter (v.)  改變、修改
  -> We have to alter our plan to hit the goal.
    我們必須改變計畫才能達到目標。
d. modify (v.)  更改、修改
2. 
a. comedy (n.)  喜劇
  -> Zootopia is one of my favorite comedies.
    動物方程式是我最喜歡的喜劇電影之一。
b. tragedy (n.)  悲劇
  -> Shakespeare's Hamlet is one of the best-known tragedies.
    莎士比亞的哈姆雷特是最有名的悲劇之一。
c. thriller (n.)  驚悚
  -> She likes to watch thriller movies with friends.
    她喜歡和朋友一起看驚悚片。
c. documentary (n.)  紀錄片
  -> The teacher played a documentary film about wild animals in class.
    老師在課堂上播了一部野生動物的紀錄片。
3. 
a. unwavering (adj.)  堅定的
  -> He is lucky to have his family's unwavering support.
    他很幸運地有家庭堅定的支持。
b. waver (v.)  搖擺、躊躇
  -> The trees wavered in the wind.
    樹在風中搖曳。
c. hesitate (v.)  猶豫
  -> Don't hesitate to contact me if you have any questions.
    如果你有任何疑問，請不用猶豫地聯繫我。
4. 
a. well-being (n.)  幸福、安康
  -> Regular meditation can increase one's mental well-being.
    定期的冥想可以使人的心靈健康。
b. happiness (n.)  幸福、快樂
c. delight (n.)  高興、愉快
  -> Her son is a real delight.
    她的兒子很討人喜歡。
  -> I'm delighted that you are back.
    我很高興你回來了。
5. 
a. undisturbed (adj.)  不受干擾的、寧靜的
  -> To make sure he has eight hours of undisturbed sleep, he uses earplugs.
    他帶耳塞以確保他有八小時的睡眠。
b. calm (adj.)   平靜的、冷靜的
  -> You must try to be calm.
    你必須想辦法冷靜下來。
c. tranquil (adj.)   平靜的、安靜的
  -> He lives a tranquil life in the countryside after retirement.
    他退休後就在鄉村過著平靜的生活。
